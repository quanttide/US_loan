<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Large Indent" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Based
on the recommendation of management, on December 22, 2004, the audit committee of TDS
determined that the Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows included in the Quarterly Report
on Form 10-Q for the period ended September 30, 2004 should be restated to correct the
classification of approximately $68.1 million of accreted interest paid on the redemption
of U.S. Cellular&#146;s Liquid Yield Option Notes (&#147;LYONs&#148;). As a result, TDS
intends to file an amended Form 10-Q to restate the Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows
for the nine months ended September 30, 2004. The restatement will reclassify the
accreted interest portion of the LYONs that were redeemed in July 2004 from &#147;Cash
Flows From Financing Activities&#148; to &#147;Cash Flows From Operating Activities.&#148;
This restatement will have no effect on TDS&#146;s &#147;Net increase (decrease) in cash
and cash equivalents&#148; for the nine months ended September 30, 2004, and will only
change the classification of cash flow items. The restatement of the Consolidated
Statements of Cash Flows will have no impact on revenues, expenses, net income, earnings
per share, or any balance sheet items. The following table shows the effect of the
reclassification on the affected captions of the Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad's Center Para" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>TDS TO RESTATE CASH
FLOW STATEMENT FOR SEPTEMBER 30, 2004 </B><BR><I>No impact on revenues, expenses, net income,
earnings per share or balance sheet</I> </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>CHICAGO &#150; Dec. 22, 2004 &#150;
Telephone and Data Systems, Inc. [AMEX:TDS] today announced that it will restate its
Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows for the nine months ended September 30, 2004. The
company&#146;s Consolidated Statements of Operations and Consolidated Balance Sheets will
not change. The restatement will reflect only a reclassification between Cash Flows From
Financing Activities and Cash Flows From Operating Activities. The restatement will have
no effect on TDS&#146;s Net increase in cash and cash equivalents. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In the third quarter of 2004,
TDS&#146;s 82%-owned subsidiary, United States Cellular Corporation, repaid certain zero
coupon debt at its accreted value of $163.3 million. The entire amount was classified as
Repayment of U.S. Cellular long-term debt under Cash Flows From Financing Activities on
the Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In the restated Consolidated
Statements of Cash Flows, $68.1 million will be reclassified from Repayment of U.S.
Cellular long term-debt under Cash Flows From Financing Activities to Accreted interest on
repayment of U.S. Cellular long-term debt under Cash Flows From Operating Activities. As a
result, Cash Flows From Financing Activities will increase by $68.1 million from $26.8
million to $94.9 million and Cash Flows From Operating Activities will decrease by $68.1
million from $543.6 million to $475.6 million. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>All of the numbers discussed above
are rounded. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>No other amounts in the
company&#146;s financial statements will change. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Tad Para Flush" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>As a result of the restatement, the
previously reported Consolidated Statements of Cash Flows for September 30, 2004 should
not be relied upon. The company intends to file an amendment to its Form 10-Q for the
period ended September 30, 2004 as promptly as possible. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>TDS, a FORTUNE 500 company, is a
