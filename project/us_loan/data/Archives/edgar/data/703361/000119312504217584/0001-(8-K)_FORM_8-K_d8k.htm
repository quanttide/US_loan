Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

 

On December 16, 2004, IDT entered into an agreement for the purchase of new Silicon Valley headquarters located at 6024 Silver Creek Valley Road, San Jose, California, from Electroglas, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Electroglas”). The purchase price is $29,000,000 and is scheduled to be paid at the close of escrow, currently scheduled to occur on January 5, 2005. The close of escrow and payment of the purchase price by IDT is subject to standard closing conditions, such as a title review, feasibility review, property survey and environmental review. Concurrently with the closing, Electroglas will enter into a lease agreement with IDT pursuant to which Electroglas will lease back from IDT portions of the facility up to June 30, 2005.
