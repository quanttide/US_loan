<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;22, 2004, Segue Software, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) entered into a
voting agreement (the &#147;Voting Agreement&#148;) with James Simons, a Director of the
Company and Chairman of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors, and S-7 Associates,
LLC, an entity controlled by James Simons (each a &#147;Stockholder&#148; and
collectively the &#147;Stockholders&#148;), pursuant to which, among other things, each
of the Stockholders agreed to vote in favor of adoption and approval of an
Amendment (the &#147;Amendment&#148;) to the Restated Certificate of Incorporation of the
Company providing for a change in the dividend and redemption rights of the
Company&#146;s Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock, par value $0.01 per share, and appointed
the Company as its irrevocable proxy to vote such Stockholder&#146;s shares of
capital stock of the Company in favor of adoption and approval of such
Amendment, in each case upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth
therein.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The foregoing description of the Voting Agreement does not purport to be
complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Voting Agreement,
a copy of which is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 to this Current Report and is
incorporated herein by reference. The Amendment is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1 to
this Current Report and is incorporated herein by reference.

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>VOTING AGREEMENT</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>VOTING AGREEMENT </B>(&#147;Agreement&#148;), dated as of December&nbsp;22, 2004, by and
between Segue Software, Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#147;Company&#148;), and the
undersigned (each, a &#147;Stockholder&#148;) beneficial holders of shares of Common
Stock, Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock and Series&nbsp;C Preferred Stock, each having par
value $0.01 per share, of the Company (the &#147;Common Stock,&#148; &#147;Series&nbsp;B Preferred
Stock&#148; and &#147;Series&nbsp;C Preferred Stock, respectively).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>WHEREAS</B>, the shares of Common Stock, Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock and Series&nbsp;C
Preferred Stock beneficially held by the Stockholders, together with any shares
of capital stock of the Company hereafter issued as a dividend in respect
thereof, are collectively referred to herein as the &#147;Shares&#148;.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>WHEREAS</B>, the Board of Directors of the Company (&#147;Board&#148;) has adopted
resolutions approving an Amendment to the Restated Certificate of Incorporation
of the Company providing for a change in the dividend and redemption rights of
the Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock (&#147;Amendment&#148;), as contained within Exhibit&nbsp;A of
the Certificate of Designations, Preferences and Rights of a Series of
Preferred Stock of Segue Software, Inc.;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>WHEREAS</B>, the Stockholders beneficially own and have voting power with
respect to the Shares;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>WHEREAS</B>, the Company agrees, subject to the Board&#146;s exercise of its

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>&#151; Dividend Rate to be Reduced, Redemption Date to be Extended &#151;</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>LEXINGTON, MASS., &#151; December&nbsp;27, 2004 &#150; </B>Segue Software, Inc. (NASDAQ -SCM:
SEGU), a leader in software quality optimization, today announced that its
Board of Directors approved an amendment to certain terms of Segue&#146;s Series&nbsp;B
Preferred Stock, reducing the dividend payable on its Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock
from 12% per year to 6% per year and extending the redemption date of its
Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock to October&nbsp;31, 2005. The amendment to the terms of
the Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock is subject to approval by Segue&#146;s stockholders at
their next annual meeting, which is expected to be held in June&nbsp;2005. The
holder of the Series&nbsp;B Preferred Stock, S-7 Associates LLC, a company managed
and owned by Dr.&nbsp;James Simons, Segue&#146;s chairman, has agreed to vote in favor of
the amendment. Dr.&nbsp;Simons has also agreed to all his other voting shares of
common stock(?) in favor of the proposed amendment. If the amendment is
approved by Segue&#146;s stockholders, Segue expects that the reduced dividend rate
would be in effect prior to the June&nbsp;30, 2005 dividend on the Series&nbsp;B
Preferred Stock.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&#147;We believe this amendment is in the best interests of both our Company and our
stockholders and represents another positive development as we continue our
efforts to build value for all our stakeholders&#148;, commented Joseph Krivickas,
Segue president and chief executive officer. &#147;This amendment, if approved by
stockholders, would, at current prices, have the effect of reducing our
dividend expense by approximately $70,000 per quarter .&#148;

measure, manage and maintain software quality throughout the entire software
application lifecycle. Segue&#146;s Quality Optimization solutions help companies
reduce business risk, ensure the deployment of high quality software and
increase return on investment. Leading businesses around the world, including
many of the Fortune 500, rely on Segue&#146;s innovative Silk family of products to
protect their business service levels, competitive edge and brand reputation.
Headquartered in Lexington, Mass., with offices across North America and
Europe, Segue can be reached at &#043;1-781-402-1000 or www.segue.com.
