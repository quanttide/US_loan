<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section&nbsp;1 - Registrant&#146;s Business and Operations</font></u></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Item 1.01.</font></u><u><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Entry
into a Material Definitive Agreement</u>.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;text-indent:-1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;21, 2004, AirNet Systems, Inc. (the &#147;Registrant&#148;)
entered into an Employment Separation Agreement and Release of Claims (the &#147;Snyder
Agreement&#148;) with Michael&nbsp;J. Snyder (&#147;Snyder&#148;), who has served as an
executive officer of the Registrant since February&nbsp;2004.&#160; The terms of the Snyder Agreement provide for
the change in Snyder&#146;s job title to Senior Vice President of Operations
effective August&nbsp;1, 2004, and the adjustment of Snyder&#146;s salary to
$190,000 per year effective (and retroactively adjusted and paid to) August&nbsp;1,
2004.&#160; Retroactive pay in the amount of $12,500
for the five-month period from August&nbsp;1, 2004 through December&nbsp;31,
2004 will be paid to Snyder, less required withholdings, no later than January&nbsp;17,
2005.&#160; All other existing terms and
conditions of Snyder&#146;s employment will remain the same until the &#147;Separation
Date&#148; described below.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the terms of the Snyder Agreement, Snyder&#146;s
employment with the Registrant will end effective January&nbsp;29, 2005 (the &#147;Separation
Date&#148;) and all further compensation, remuneration, bonuses and eligibility of
Snyder under the Registrant&#146;s benefit plans will terminate on the Separation
Date.&#160; Following the Separation Date,
Snyder has agreed to provide consulting services for the Registrant on such
matters as both parties agree upon.&#160;
Those future consulting services will be reasonably limited so as not to
interfere or conflict with any other responsibilities Snyder may have.&#160; Each hour of actual consulting service will
be compensated at a rate of $100.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Following the Separation Date, the Registrant will pay
to Snyder, as severance benefits, an amount equal to his last annual base
salary (i.e., $190,000) over the 12-month period from the Separation Date to January&nbsp;31,
2006, in 24 equal semi-monthly installments, less withholdings.&#160; The Registrant will also reimburse Snyder for
the costs of outplacement services incurred by him through the end of calendar
year 2005, up to a maximum of $15,000.&#160;
The Registrant will also make a lump sum payment to Snyder within 30
days following the Separation Date in an amount equal to Snyder&#146;s costs of
continuing the medical insurance coverage for Snyder and his eligible
dependents under COBRA for the 12-month period following the Separation Date,
less an amount equal to the premium Snyder would have paid for such insurance
had he continued employment for the 12-month period from the Separation Date to
January&nbsp;31, 2006.&#160; In</font></p>
