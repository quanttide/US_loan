OF<BR>
U.S. BANCORP NON-QUALIFIED RETIREMENT PLAN</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">The U.S. Bancorp Non-Qualified Retirement Plan (the &#147;Plan Statement&#148;) is amended in the following
respects:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%"><B>1.&nbsp;Appendix&nbsp;B-14. Effective December&nbsp;20, 2004, the Plan Statement shall be amended by the
addition of the attached Appendix&nbsp;B-14.</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%"><B>2.&nbsp;SAVINGS CLAUSE. Save and except as expressly amended above, the Plan Statement shall
continue in full force and effect.</B>


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; display: none; text-indent: 4%">1

<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>APPENDIX B-14</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>SUPPLEMENTAL BENEFITS</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">This Appendix&nbsp;B-14 summarizes the supplemental benefits payable to the named Participant under the
Plan.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Participant: </I>Pamela Joseph


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Formula: </I>Fifty-five percent (55%) of the Participant&#146;s Final Average Monthly Earnings (as defined
in Section&nbsp;2.17 of the Plan) reduced by all of the following (each of which shall be considered an
&#147;offsetting benefit&#148; for purposes of this Appendix&nbsp;B-14): the Participant&#146;s benefit under the
Qualified Plan and the Participant&#146;s Excess Benefit under this Plan.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Normal Form of Payment: </I>Life annuity with ten (10)&nbsp;years certain


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Vesting Service Start Date: </I>From December&nbsp;20, 2004


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Vesting: </I>100% vested at December&nbsp;20, 2014 if continuously employed by U.S. Bancorp from the Vesting
Service Start Date through December&nbsp;20, 2014


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Unreduced Retirement Age: </I>62


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Early Retirement Reduction: </I>1/180 per month prior to age 62, plus 1/360 per month prior to age 60


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Earliest Payout Date: </I>Age 55 and 100% vested

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>2005 NON-EMPLOYEE DIRECTOR COMPENSATION</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">The following is the 2005 compensation structure for the non-employee members of the U.S. Bancorp
Board of Directors as approved on October&nbsp;20, 2004:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Annual cash retainer</I>&#151;$70,000


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Cash retainer for Audit Committee Chairman</I>&#151;$15,000


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Cash retainer for Chairman of the Community Outreach and Fair Lending Committee, Compensation
Committee, Governance Committee and Credit and Finance Committee</I>&#151;$10,000


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Cash retainer for Audit Committee members</I>&#151;$5,000


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Number of shares subject to annual grant of Restricted Stock Units</I>&#151;A number of shares equal to the
quotient of (i) $53,000 divided by (ii)&nbsp;the fair market value of a share of U.S. Bancorp common
stock on the grant date


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><I>Number of shares subject to annual grant of Stock Options</I><sup>*</sup>&#151; A number of shares equal to
