<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 16, 2004 the registrant&#146;s board of directors appointed Barb
Bontrager as chief financial officer and terminated the position of treasurer,
previously held by Robert Miller.&#160; The
duties and responsibilities of the treasurer&#146;s office have been assigned to the
chief financial officer.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">From December 2001 through November 2004, Ms. Bontrager was a technical
specialist for Badgerland Farm Credit Services.&#160;
Prior to her position at Badgerland Farm Credit Services, Ms. Bontrager
held the position of controller at Bethel Grain Company.&#160; She is a certified public accountant.</font></p>
