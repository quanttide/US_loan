<p><b><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">Item 1.01&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</font></b></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December 23, 2004, Quantum Corporation (the &ldquo;<b>Company</b>&rdquo;) entered into a Mutual General Release and Global Settlement Agreement (the
&ldquo;<b>Settlement Agreement</b>&rdquo;) with Maxtor Corporation, the corporation to which the Company sold its former hard disk drive business on March 30, 2001 (&ldquo;<b>Maxtor</b>&rdquo;).&nbsp; Under the Settlement Agreement, among other things, the parties,
(a) settled, released and discharged each other from liability with respect to certain currently outstanding claims that the parties have against each other, and which the parties may have against each other in the future, arising out of or related to the disposition
of the hard disk drive business to Maxtor, including claims related to the Tax Sharing and Indemnity Agreement by and among Quantum, Maxtor and Insula Corporation, dated April 2, 2001 (the &ldquo;<b>Tax Sharing Agreement</b>&rdquo;), which agreement is attached to
this Form 8-K as Exhibit 10.1 and is incorporated herein by reference, (b) agreed that Quantum would be entitled to any refunds and be responsible for any deficiency with respect to the resolution of the Internal Revenue Service&rsquo;s audits of Quantum for the
fiscal years ending March 31, 1997 through and including March 31, 1999, (c) agreed upon Quantum&rsquo;s and Maxtor&rsquo;s joint control of and equal payment of fees and costs associated with the Internal Revenue Service&rsquo;s audit of the Company for the fiscal
years ending March 31, 2000 through and including March 31, 2002, and (d) amended certain provisions of the Tax Sharing Agreement as further described below.&nbsp; A description of the Company&rsquo;s disputes with Maxtor being addressed by the Settlement Agreement
was provided on page 43 of &ldquo;Management&rsquo;s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations &ndash; Risk Factors&rdquo; in the Company&rsquo;s Form 10-Q for the period ended September 26, 2004, filed on November 3, 2004.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Settlement Agreement will result in no charge to Quantum&rsquo;s earnings for the quarter ending December 26, 2004.&nbsp; However, Quantum will be reviewing
whether this settlement will result in any reduction to tax reserves.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Settlement Agreement amended the Tax Sharing Agreement in the following respects material to the Company:</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1.&nbsp; Section 1 of the Tax Sharing Agreement provided for the sharing of the tax liabilities of the hard disk drive business acquired by Maxtor and the
business retained by the Company for periods prior to April 2, 2001.&nbsp; The Company and Maxtor have disputed the operation of this section.&nbsp; Under the Settlement Agreement all claims arising under this section are released and the section is terminated in its
entirety.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2.&nbsp; Section 3(a) of the Tax Sharing Agreement provided for Maxtor&rsquo;s indemnity of the Company for various taxes attributable to periods prior to April
2, 2001.&nbsp; Under the Tax Sharing Agreement, this indemnity obligation was limited to the sum of $142,000,000 plus 50% of the excess over $142,000,000.&nbsp; Under the Settlement Agreement, Maxtor&rsquo;s remaining tax indemnity liability under section 3(a) for
all tax claims is limited to $8,760,000 (except that the amount may be increased in circumstances considered remote that relate to the IRS audit of the Company for the fiscal years ending March 31, 1997 through and including March 31, 1999).</font></p>

<p align="center"><b><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">Tax Sharing and Indemnity Agreement</font></b></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This Tax Sharing and Indemnity Agreement, dated April 2, 2001, is by and between Quantum Corporation (&ldquo;<b>Quantum</b>&rdquo;), a Delaware corporation,
Insula Corporation, a Delaware corporation, (&ldquo;<b>Spinco</b>&rdquo;), and Maxtor Corporation (&ldquo;<b>Maxtor</b>&rdquo;), a Delaware corporation.&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center"><b><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">Recitals</font></b></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A.&nbsp; Quantum is the common parent of an affiliated group of corporations (the &ldquo;<b>Quantum Group</b>&rdquo;).</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;B.&nbsp; The businesses of Quantum and other members of the Quantum Group involve two product lines: hard disk drives and DLTape and storage systems.&nbsp; (The
businesses related to these product lines are referred to as the &ldquo;<b>HDD Business</b>&rdquo; and the &ldquo;<b>DSS Business</b>.&rdquo;)&nbsp;</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;C.&nbsp; In August 1999, Quantum recapitalized its stock into two classes of tracking stock, the &ldquo;<b>HDD Stock</b>&rdquo; and the &ldquo;<b>DSS
Stock</b>&rdquo; (the &ldquo;<b>Recapitalization</b>&rdquo;).&nbsp; Each class tracks the results of one of the businesses of the Quantum Group.&nbsp;</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;D.&nbsp; Quantum prepares separate financial statements for each of its two lines of business.&nbsp; For this purpose, each line of business is allocated a
share of the group&rsquo;s aggregate tax liability.&nbsp; This allocation is made as though each business were conducted by a separate subsidiary, using the allocation method described in section 1.1502-32(b)(3)(iv)(D) of the Treasury regulations.&nbsp; Thus, each
line of business is allocated the amount of tax that it would have paid if it had been a separate and independent corporation.&nbsp; To the extent that the tax liability attributable to one business is reduced by losses, credits or other tax attributes of the other
business, the first business must pay the second business for the use of those tax attributes.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;E.&nbsp; Maxtor is also engaged in the disk drive business.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;F.&nbsp; Quantum and Maxtor would like to combine their disk drive businesses, without including Quantum&rsquo;s DSS Business in the combined entity.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;G.&nbsp; To accomplish their objectives, the parties agreed to a plan of reorganization reflected in an Amended and Restated Agreement and Plan of Merger and
Reorganization (the &ldquo;<b>Merger Agreement</b>&rdquo;) dated as of October 3, 2000, by and among Quantum, Spinco, Maxtor and Hawaii Acquisition Corporation, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Maxtor.&nbsp; As part of the plan, Quantum is transferring to Spinco all of
the assets of its HDD Business in exchange for all of the stock of Spinco.&nbsp; Quantum will distribute the Spinco stock pro rata to the holders of HDD Stock in redemption of their HDD Stock (the &ldquo;<b>Distribution</b>&rdquo;).&nbsp; Spinco will then merge with
and into Maxtor (the &ldquo;<b>Merger</b>&rdquo;).</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;H.&nbsp; Quantum and Spinco would like to allocate responsibilities for certain tax matters.&nbsp; In particular, the parties would like to provide for (1)
payments, as appropriate, to reflect differences</font></p>

<p align="center"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">1</font></p>

<div align="center"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font>
<hr size="2" width="100%" noshade color="#3366ff" align="center" />
</div>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp; between estimates of tax liability for periods beginning before the date of the Distribution (the &ldquo;<b>Distribution Date</b>&rdquo;) and the liability shown on the tax returns filed for those periods, (2) the
retention, maintenance and provision of access to all records necessary to prepare and file appropriate tax returns, and (3) indemnity for certain tax obligations.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I.&nbsp; In the Merger, Maxtor will assume the obligations of Spinco and succeed to Spinco&rsquo;s rights under this Agreement.&nbsp; Maxtor acknowledges its
assumption of these obligations and its succession to Spinco&rsquo;s rights under this Agreement as a result of the Merger.</font></p>

<p align="center"><b><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">Agreement</font></b></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To accomplish the purposes described above, Quantum, Spinco and Maxtor agree as follows:</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1.&nbsp; <i>Settlement of Tax Liability for Pre-Distribution Taxable Years</i>.&nbsp; Within 30 days after filing a tax return for any taxable period
beginning before the date of the Distribution Date, Quantum shall allocate the tax liability for the period covered by the return between the HDD Business and the DSS Business and notify Spinco of the allocation.&nbsp; Quantum shall make this allocation in a manner
consistent with its prior practice, using the method described in section 1.1502-32(b)(3)(iv)(D) of the Treasury regulations and treating the HDD Business and the DSS Business as though they had been conducted by separate members of the Quantum Group.&nbsp; Quantum
shall pay Spinco, or Spinco shall pay Quantum, as appropriate, to reflect the differences between the allocation of the liability shown on the tax return and the allocation of estimated liability used for purposes of determining prior payments between the two
businesses.&nbsp; For purposes of this Agreement, references to Spinco shall, when appropriate, be construed as references to Maxtor, as successor to Spinco.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2.&nbsp; <i>Retention of and Access to Records; Cooperation and Assistance</i>.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(a)&nbsp; <i>Retention of and Access to Records.&nbsp;</i> Quantum and Spinco shall retain all tax returns for periods beginning before the Distribution Date,
together with all related reports, work papers, schedules or other documents or computer files, and, subject to the confidentiality provisions of Section 5, shall make these documents or files available to the other upon request.&nbsp; Neither Quantum nor Spinco
shall dispose of any of these documents or files without the other&rsquo;s permission.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(b)&nbsp; <i>Cooperation and Assistance</i>.&nbsp; Subject to the confidentiality provisions of Section 5, Quantum and Spinco shall provide each other with such
cooperation, assistance, and information as either of them may reasonably request of the other with respect to the filing with any taxing authority of any tax return, amended return, claim for refund, or other document.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3.&nbsp; <i>Indemnity Obligations of Spinco</i></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(a)&nbsp; <i>Certain Pre-Distribution Tax Liabilities.</i> &nbsp; Subject to the limitation provided by Section 3(b), Spinco shall indemnify Quantum and its
affiliates and hold them harmless from and against any taxes, and related interest and penalties, imposed on Quantum or any affiliate attributable to</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(i) the conduct of the DSS business before the Recapitalization; or</font></p>

<p align="center"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">2</font></p>

<div align="center"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font>
