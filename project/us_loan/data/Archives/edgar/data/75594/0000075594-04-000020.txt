<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:8pt;'><font size=2>Item 5.02. &nbsp;Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:8pt;'><font size=2>On December 17, 2004, PacifiCorp appointed Ronnie Mercer (age 60) to the position of Executive Vice President, Operations, effective January 1, 2005. </font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:8pt;'><font size=2>Prior to this appointment,
  Ronnie Mercer was Group Director, Infrastructure of Scottish Power plc (&#147;ScottishPower&#148;),
  a position he held since April 2001, and was responsible in this role for the
  United Kingdom distribution and transmission businesses. ScottishPower is PacifiCorp&#146;s
  ultimate parent company. He joined the ScottishPower Generation Business in
  1994 and was appointed Generation Director in 1996 and then Managing Director
  of Southern Water in 1998. Previous career positions include Scottish Director
  and Managing Director roles at British Steel.</font></p>
<p style=' margin-bottom:0pt; margin-top:8pt;'><font size=2>Mr.&nbsp;Mercer currently serves as a member of the Board of Directors for the following companies: Electricity Association Services Ltd, Electricity Association Ltd, Scottish Power UK Holdings Ltd, Scottish Power UK plc, SP Distribution Ltd, SP Gas Ltd, SP Manweb plc, SP Power Systems Ltd and SP Transmission Ltd.</font></p>
