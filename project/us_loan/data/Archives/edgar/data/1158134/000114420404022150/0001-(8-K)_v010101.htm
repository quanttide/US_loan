Item 1.01 Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On December 14, 2004, Dwango North America Corp. (the “Company”) entered into a sub-lease contract with Microsoft Corporation whereby the Company will sub-lease approximately 15,000 square feet of office space in Seattle, WA for use as its headquarters site. The term of the lease is two years and the minimum lease commitment is approximately $527,000 for the entire lease term.
