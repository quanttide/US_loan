<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman,Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
On December 30, 2004, Atlas Pipeline Partners, L.P., a consolidated subsidiary of Atlas America, Inc. (which is a majority-owned
subsidiary of Resource America, Inc.), entered into an agreement with SEMCO Energy, Inc. (&#147;SEMCO&#148;) and k1 Ventures Limited (&#147;k1&#148;)
settling the legal proceedings among them with respect to SEMCO&#146;s termination of the sale of Alaska Pipeline Company to Atlas
Pipeline.  The agreement provides that, on or before December 31, 2004, SEMCO must pay Atlas Pipeline $5.5 million in settlement of
all of Atlas Pipeline&#146;s claims against SEMCO and k1.  The agreement further contains the release of Atlas Pipeline
by SEMCO and k1, and of SEMCO and k1 by Atlas Pipeline.</FONT></P>
