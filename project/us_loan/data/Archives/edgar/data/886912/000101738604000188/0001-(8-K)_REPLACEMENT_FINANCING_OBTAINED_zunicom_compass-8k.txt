Item 8.01.  Other Events

         On December 22, 2004, Zunicom Inc. issued the press release attached as
Exhibit 99.1  announcing  that  Universal  Power Group,  Inc. has entered into a
Revolving Credit and Security  Agreement with Compass Bank to replace its former
lender GE  Commercial  Credit.  The  revolving  line of credit for $12.0 million
carries an  interest  rate of LIBOR plus 250 basis  points and is secured by the
assets of Universal  Power Group  including  accounts  receivable and inventory.
This agreement expires May 2007.
