<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>AMENDMENT NO. 1 TO</B></FONT>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>THE PMI GROUP, INC.</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>OFFICER DEFERRED COMPENSATION PLAN</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">THE PMI GROUP, INC., having adopted The PMI Group, Inc. Officer Deferred Compensation Plan
(the &#147;Plan&#148;) effective as of July&nbsp;1, 1997, hereby amends the Plan, effective as of December&nbsp;31,
2004, as follows:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">1.&nbsp;Section&nbsp;9.1 is hereby amended by striking the last sentence of the paragraph in its
entirety.



<P align="left" style="margin-left:4%; font-size: 12pt">2. Section&nbsp;9.4 is hereby added to the Plan to read as follows:



<P align="left" style="margin-left:4%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;9.4 Effective December&nbsp;31, 2004, the Plan will be frozen. After that date, no new
Participants will be admitted into the Plan, Compensation Deferrals will be discontinued,
and no new Company Contributions will be made. Each Participant&#146;s Account will continue to
be maintained until it is scheduled to be paid to him or her in accordance with the
provisions of the Plan, unless the Plan is terminated at an earlier time pursuant to this
Section&nbsp;9.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">IN WITNESS WHEREOF, The PMI Group, Inc., by its duly authorized officer, has executed this
Amendment No.&nbsp;1 on the date indicated below.
