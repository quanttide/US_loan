<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=4>FLOTEK EXECUTES
AGREEMENT TO PURCHASE SPIDLE SALES &amp; SERVICES, INC: </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Center Bold" FSL="Default" -->
<A NAME=A007></A>
<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=4>ACQUISITION BROADENS
FLOTEK&#146;S DRILLING PRODUCTS BUSINESS </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>HOUSTON, Dec. 21, 2004 &#150; Flotek
Industries, Inc. (FLTK.OB), announced the signing of a definitive agreement to purchase
the stock of privately held Spidle Sales and Services, Inc., for $8 million in cash, debt
and stock on December 20<SUP>th</SUP>, 2004. The deal is anticipated to close during
January 2005. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Spidle Sales and Services, Inc.,
(&#147;Spidle&#148;) is one of the premier downhole tool companies with rental, sales and
manufacturing operations throughout the Rocky Mountains. Spidle, <U>www.spidle.com</U>,
serves both the domestic and international downhole tool markets with a customer base
extending into Canada, Mexico, South America, Europe, Asia and Africa. Spidle serves the
energy, mining, water and industrial drilling sectors. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;The addition of Spidle to our
Drilling Products Group is consistent with our strategy to grow our core businesses&#148;,
stated Jerry D. Dumas, Sr., Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Flotek. &#147;The
retention of Spidle&#146;s strong management team, outstanding market reputation and
geographic positioning will enhance our drilling products segment and provide greater
market penetration and geographic expansion for all our product lines. The acquisition
allows us to further accelerate cross-selling opportunities that currently exist among our
specialty chemical, production and drilling products businesses.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Agee Spidle, President and Founder of
Spidle Sales and Services, Inc., stated &#147;Flotek shares our views and philosophy of
customer service. I look enthusiastically to the benefits this business combination will
provide to our customers and employees.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>More detail will be available in the
Company&#146;s 8-Ks to be filed with the SEC. For product information and additional
information on the Company, please visit Flotek&#146;s web site at
<U>http://www.flotekind.com</U> </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Flotek is a publicly traded company
involved in the manufacturing and marketing of innovative specialty chemicals, downhole
drilling and production equipment, and management of automated bulk material handling,
loading and blending facilities. It serves major and independent companies in the domestic
and international oilfield service industry. </FONT></P>
