Item 2.01. Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.

On December 21, 2004, First Potomac Realty Trust (the “Company”) acquired Windsor at Battlefield, a two-building, 154,226-square-foot flex property located in Manassas, Virginia from DMV SUB 3, LLC, an unaffiliated third party, for $15.25 million in cash. The purchase was funded with borrowings on the Company’s revolving credit facility. The property is individually insignificant, but when aggregated with previously completed acquisitions, is “significant” under Regulation S-X.

Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

On December 22, 2004, the Company issued a press release announcing the acquisition of Windsor at Battlefield. A copy of the release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this current report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.

In accordance with General Instructions B.2 and B.6 of Form 8-K, the information included in this item of the current report on Form 8-K (including Exhibit 99.1 hereto), shall not be deemed “filed” for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference into any filing made by the Company under the Exchange Act or Securities Act of 1933, as amended, except as shall be expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
