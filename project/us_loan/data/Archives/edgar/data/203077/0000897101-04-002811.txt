<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Item 1.01.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Entry into Material Definitive Agreements.</FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>On December 24, 2004, St. Jude
Medical, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) entered into a Preferred Stock Purchase and
Acquisition Option Agreement (the &#147;Purchase and Option Agreement&#148;) and an
Agreement and Plan of Merger (the &#147;Merger Agreement&#148;), each with ProRhythm,
Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#147;ProRhythm&#148;) which is developing a High Intensity
Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) catheter-based ablation system for the treatment of Atrial
Fibrillation. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The Purchase and Option Agreement
provides that, subject to the approval of the stockholders of ProRhythm, the Company has
the exclusive right, but not the obligation, through March 31, 2007, to acquire ProRhythm
for $125 million in initial cash consideration payable to the ProRhythm stockholders
(other than the Company) pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth in the Merger
Agreement (the &#147;Merger&#148;), with additional cash consideration payable to the
ProRhythm stockholders (other than the Company) after the consummation of the Merger, if
ProRhythm achieves certain performance-related milestones. The Company is not obligated
to exercise this acquisition right.</FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In addition, the Purchase and Option
Agreement provides that, subject to the approval of the stockholders of ProRhythm and the
satisfaction of certain other conditions set forth in the Purchase and Option Agreement,
the Company will make two independent $12.5 million equity investments, both of which are
anticipated by the Company to occur in 2005. </FONT></P>
