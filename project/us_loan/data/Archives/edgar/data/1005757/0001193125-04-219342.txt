 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As explained in Item 5.02 below, on December 21, 2004, CSG Systems International, Inc.
(&#147;CSG&#148;) announced that Neal C. Hansen, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, plans to retire from CSG on June 30, 2005. In conjunction with Mr. Hansen&#146;s planned retirement, CSG and Mr. Hansen entered into the Fifth Amendment to
Employment Agreement with Neal C. Hansen, dated December 20, 2004 (the &#147;Fifth Amendment&#148;). A brief description of the material terms and conditions of the Fifth Amendment is as follows: </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Effective June 30, 2005, Mr. Hansen will: (i) resign as Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer of CSG and CSG Systems, Inc. (a subsidiary of CSG); (ii) resign as an
officer of any CSG subsidiary for which he serves in such capacity; and (iii) retire from employment with CSG. </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

stockholders. </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">

</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The post-termination consulting services provision, originally included in the Fourth Amendment to Employment Agreement with Neal C. Hansen, dated November 15, 2002 (the
&#147;Fourth Amendment&#148;), was modified in the Fifth Amendment as follows: </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<TD WIDTH="4%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#149;</FONT></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Previously under the Fourth Amendment, Mr. Hansen was required to provide up to 20 hours per month (at the discretion of CSG) of post-termination consulting services for a period of
ten years. In return for such services, Mr. Hansen was entitled to use CSG&#146;s aircraft at CSG&#146;s expense (not to exceed 40,000 miles annually), and was entitled to the use of office space at CSG&#146;s expense (not to exceed $40,000
annually). CSG was also required to reimburse Mr. Hansen on a &#147;gross up&#148; basis for any personal income taxes he was subject to as a result of his use of the aircraft and office space. </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">

entirety in the Fifth Amendment. </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As consideration for the above, and for the services to be provided under the Fifth Amendment, Mr. Hansen will receive a severance payment of $1.5 million and additional cash
payments of approximately $8.1 million, which are scheduled to be paid as follows: (i) $5.6 million on January 2, 2006; and (ii) two equal installments of $2.0 million each on July 1, 2006 and January 2, 2007. Mr. Hansen is required to be employed
by CSG up until his retirement date of June 30, 2005, unless he is terminated without cause, to be entitled to these cash payments. </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
