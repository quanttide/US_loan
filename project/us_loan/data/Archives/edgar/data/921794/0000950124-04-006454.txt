<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.02. Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;22, 2004, GE Franchise Finance Corporation (&#147;GE Capital&#148;) elected to terminate a
certain Agreement of Sale (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) dated November&nbsp;19, 2004, by and between Captec
Franchise Capital Partners L.P. III (the &#147;Partnership&#148;) and GE Capital. There is no material
relationship between the Partnership or its affiliates and GE Capital, other than in respect of the
Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Under the Agreement, the Partnership agreed to sell to GE Capital twelve of the Partnership&#146;s
properties, and certain real property leases, equipment and equipment leases related to such
properties, for an aggregate purchase price of $20,565,000. The Agreement provided that the
purchase price could be adjusted if, among other things, less than all the properties set forth in
the Agreement were sold to GE Capital due to the exercise by tenants of certain rights of first
refusal contained in the property leases or for certain changes in the 10 Year U.S. Swap Interest
Rate Index. The Agreement also provided that GE Capital&#146;s obligations under the Agreement were
contingent upon the satisfaction of certain conditions, which included, among other things, the
condition that GE Capital obtain all necessary board of directors&#146; and related investment approvals
for the transactions contemplated by the Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;22, 2004, GE Capital informed the Partnership that it was electing to terminate
the Agreement on the basis that GE Capital was unable to obtain the necessary board of directors&#146;
and related investment approvals for the transactions contemplated by the Agreement. The
Partnership did not incur any material early termination penalties in connection with the
termination of the Agreement.
