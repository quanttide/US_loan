<h6 align="left" style="font-weight:bold;letter-spacing:-.15pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-align:left;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;letter-spacing:0pt;">Item 5.03 (a)&#160; Amendments
to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal Year</font></b></h6>

<h6 align="left" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;letter-spacing:-.15pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-align:left;"><font style="font-weight:normal;letter-spacing:0pt;">&nbsp;</font></h6>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Effective
December&nbsp;21, 2004, the Board of Directors of First Financial Service
Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) amended the Company&#146;s Bylaws.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The
revised Section&nbsp;14 of Article&nbsp;II now reads as follows:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Article&nbsp;II-Section&nbsp;14.&#160; <u>Nominating Committee</u>.&#160; The Board of Directors shall act as a
nominating committee for selecting the nominees for election of directors.&#160; The nominating committee shall consist of all
directors, excluding any directors not deemed independent, under the rules of
The Nasdaq Stock Market, Inc.&#160; Except in
the case of a nominee substituted as a result of the death or other incapacity
of a nominee, the nominating committee shall deliver written nominations to the
secretary at least 20 days prior to the date of the annual meeting.&#160; Provided such committee makes such
nominations, no nominations for directors except those made by the nominating
committee shall be voted upon at the annual meeting unless other nominations by
shareholders are made in writing and delivered to the secretary of the
Corporation in accordance with the provisions of the Corporation&#146;s Articles of
Incorporation.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Prior
to the amendment, Section&nbsp;14 of Article&nbsp;II was as follows:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Article&nbsp;II-Section&nbsp;14.&#160; <u>Nominating Committee</u>.&#160; The Board of Directors shall act as a
nominating committee for selecting the management nominees for election of
directors.&#160; Except in the case of a
nominee substituted as a result of the death or other incapacity of a management
nominee, the nominating committee shall deliver written nominations to the
secretary at least 20 days prior to the date of the annual meeting.&#160; Provided such committee makes such
nominations, no nominations for directors except those made by the nominating
committee shall be voted upon at the annual meeting unless other nominations by
shareholders are made in writing and delivered to the secretary of the
Corporation in accordance with the provisions of the Corporation&#146;s Articles of
Incorporation.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>
