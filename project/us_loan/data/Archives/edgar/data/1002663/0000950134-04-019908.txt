<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;22, 2004, Photon Dynamics, Inc. and its Executive Chairman of the Board, Dr.&nbsp;Malcolm
Thompson, agreed to extend the consulting period under the consulting agreement, dated March&nbsp;18,
2004, between Photon Dynamics and Dr.&nbsp;Thompson, from December&nbsp;31, 2004 to March&nbsp;31, 2005. As a
result of the extension, Dr.&nbsp;Thompson will continue to provide consulting services and receive
consulting fees of $12,000 per month through March&nbsp;2005.
