"Commission"). All of the reports and other information filed by Allstate Life
under the Exchange Act are incorporated by reference into the registration
statement filed with the Commission by Allstate Life and Allstate Life Global
Funding ("Global Funding") (SEC Registration No. 333-112249) related to the

2004 was also incorporated by reference into the Current Report on Form 8-K of

can read and copy any reports or other information that Allstate Life files
under the Exchange Act at the SEC's public reference room at 450 Fifth Street,
