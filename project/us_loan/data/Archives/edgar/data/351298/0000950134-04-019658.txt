<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On October&nbsp;1, 2004, HEI, Inc., a Minnesota corporation (the &#147;<B>Company</B>&#148;), entered into an
Industrial/Commercial Lease (the &#147;<B>Lease Agreement</B>&#148;) with Boulder Investor&#146;s, LLC, a Virginia
limited liability company (the &#147;<B>Landlord</B>&#148;). The Lease Agreement relates to the approximately
152,022 square foot facility located at 4801 N. 63<SUP>rd</SUP> Street, Boulder, Colorado (the
&#147;<B>Facility</B>&#148;), which the Company leases for its Advanced Medical Operations. On September&nbsp;29, 2004,
Eastside Properties, LLC sold the Facility to Titan Real Estate Investment Group, Inc., an
affiliate of the Landlord.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursuant to the Lease Agreement, the Company will lease the Facility through September&nbsp;30,
2019. The base rent for the Facility is $113,321 for the first lease year and increases each year
to a maximum of $171,408 for the fifteenth lease year. The Lease Agreement also required the
Company to pay a security deposit of $1.5&nbsp;million (the &#147;<B>Security Deposit</B>&#148;). The Security Deposit
will be reduced to $150,000 if after completing four consecutive quarters of positive EBITDA (which
is defined as the Company&#146;s net income, excluding any deductions for interest, taxes, depreciation
or amortization) the Company delivers to the Landlord the greater of 100,000 shares of its common
stock, or that number of shares of its common stock representing 0.11% of all of its then
outstanding shares of common stock.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Company intends to sublease a portion of the Facility and currently is in negotiations for
such subleases. Pursuant to the Lease Agreement, any sublease requires the prior written consent
of the Landlord, which consent the Landlord will not unreasonably withhold or delay.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A
copy of the Lease Agreement is attached to this Current Report on
Form&nbsp;8-K as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 and is incorporated herein by
reference.
