<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Roper Industries, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;)
approved grants of restricted stock, effective as of December&nbsp;28, 2004, to certain of the Company&#146;s
executive officers. Nigel W. Crocker, Timothy J. Winfrey and Benjamin W. Wood each received an
award of 5,000 shares of restricted stock, and C. Thomas O&#146;Grady received an award of 3,000 shares
of restricted stock. Each award is evidenced by a Restricted Stock Award Agreement that provides
that the restricted shares will vest as to one-third of the shares on each of February&nbsp;28, 2005,
February&nbsp;28, 2006, and February&nbsp;28, 2007, or earlier upon the grantee&#146;s death or disability.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Brian D. Jellison, Chairman
of the Board, President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company, received an award of
55,000 restricted stock units on December&nbsp;28, 2004, which will vest as to one-third of the units on
each of December&nbsp;28, 2005, February&nbsp;28, 2006, and February&nbsp;28, 2007. The units will vest in full
upon Mr.&nbsp;Jellison&#146;s death or disability, or upon termination of his employment by the Company
without cause (as defined) or upon his resignation for good reason (as defined) within one year
following a change in control (as defined) of the Company. Vested units will convert to shares of
common stock on the later of (i)&nbsp;the vesting date, or (ii)&nbsp;if required by Section&nbsp;409A of the
Internal Revenue Code, six months after his separation from service.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The form of Executive Officer Restricted Stock Award Agreement and Mr.&nbsp;Jellison&#146;s Restricted Stock
Unit Award Agreement are filed as exhibits to this Form 8-K and incorporated by reference
herein.
