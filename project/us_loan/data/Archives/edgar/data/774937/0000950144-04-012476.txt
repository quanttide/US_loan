<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;4.01. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Changes in Registrant&#146;s Certifying Accountant</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">(a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Resignation of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(i)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; On December&nbsp;28, 2004, PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP (&#147;PwC&#148;) resigned as the independent
registered public accounting firm of Verilink Corporation (&#147;Verilink&#148;).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(ii)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The report issued by PwC on the financial statements of Verilink for the years ended July
2, 2004 and June&nbsp;27, 2003 did not contain an adverse opinion or a disclaimer of opinion, nor were
they qualified or modified as to uncertainty, audit scope or accounting principle, except that
PwC&#146;s report on Verilink&#146;s financial statements for the years ended July&nbsp;2, 2004 and June&nbsp;27, 2003
contained an explanatory paragraph indicating that substantial doubt exists about Verilink&#146;s
ability to continue as a going concern.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(iii)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; During each of the years ended July&nbsp;2, 2004 and June&nbsp;27, 2003 and through December&nbsp;28,
2004, there were no disagreements between Verilink and PwC on any matter of accounting principles
or practices, financial statement disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure, which
disagreement(s), if not resolved to the satisfaction of PwC would have caused them to make
reference thereto in their reports on the financial statements for such years; and there were no
&#147;reportable events&#148; as that term is used in Item&nbsp;304(a)(1)(v) of Regulation&nbsp;S-K occurring during
the years ended July&nbsp;2, 2004 and June&nbsp;27, 2003 and through December&nbsp;28, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(iv)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Verilink has provided PwC with a copy of the foregoing disclosures and has requested that
PwC review such disclosures and furnish a letter addressed to the Securities and Exchange
Commission stating whether or not PwC agrees with such statements. A copy of PwC&#146;s letter response
to such request is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;16.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">(b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Engagement of Ehrhardt Keefe Steiner &#038; Hottman PC



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;28, 2004, Verilink retained Ehrhardt Keefe Steiner &#038; Hottman PC (&#147;EKSH&#148;),
Certified Public Accountants and Consultants, as its new independent registered public accounting
firm. The decision to retain EKSH as Verilink&#146;s independent registered public accounting firm was
made by the Audit Committee of Verilink&#146; Board of Directors on December&nbsp;28, 2004. Verilink engaged
EKSH to audit Verilink&#146;s financial statements for the year ending July&nbsp;1, 2005. During the years
ended July&nbsp;2, 2004 and June&nbsp;27, 2003 and through December&nbsp;28, 2004, neither Verilink or anyone on
Verilink&#146;s behalf has consulted with EKSH regarding (i)&nbsp;the application of accounting principles to
a specific transaction, either completed or proposed, or the type of audit opinion that might be
rendered on Verilink&#146;s financial statements, or (ii)&nbsp;any matter that was either the subject of a
disagreement as that term is used in Item&nbsp;304 (a)(1)(iv) of Regulation&nbsp;S-K and the related
instructions to Item&nbsp;304 of Regulation&nbsp;S-K or a reportable event as that term is used in Item
304(a)(1)(v) and the related instructions to Item&nbsp;304 of Regulation&nbsp;S-K.
