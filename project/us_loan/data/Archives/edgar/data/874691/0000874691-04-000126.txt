<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">On December 21, 2004, Outback Steakhouse, Inc., entered into an amended two and one half year Credit and Guaranty Agreement, (the &ldquo;Credit Agreement&rdquo;), which replaces a three-year Credit and Guaranty Agreement that
was set to expire in December 2004.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">Under the terms of the Credit Agreement, we are the guarantor of an uncollateralized line of credit that permits borrowing of up to $24,500,000 by RY-8, Inc. (&ldquo;RY-8&rdquo;), which is our 50% partner in a joint venture
created to develop Roy&rsquo;s restaurants (the &ldquo;Joint Venture&rdquo;).&nbsp; The line of credit and the guarantee terminate in June 2007.&nbsp; Our Joint Venture with RY-8, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Roy&rsquo;s Holdings, Inc. (&ldquo;RHI&rdquo;), was
formed to develop, own, and operate a chain of restaurants featuring Pacific Rim cuisine.&nbsp; The culinary style and concept for these restaurants were developed originally by Chef Roy Yamaguchi and Hawaiian Pacific Restaurant Group, Inc., also a wholly owned
subsidiary of RHI.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">According to the terms of the Credit Agreement, RY-8 may borrow, repay, re-borrow, or prepay advances at any time before the termination date of the agreement.&nbsp; On June 30, 2007, the termination date of the agreement, the
entire outstanding principal amount of the loan then outstanding and any accrued interest is due.&nbsp; As of December 21, 2004, the outstanding balance on the line of credit was approximately $21,800,000.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">RY-8&rsquo;s obligations under the Credit Agreement are unconditionally guaranteed by us and RHI.&nbsp; If an event of default occurs (as defined in the Credit Agreement, and including our covenant commitments under existing
lines of credit) then the total outstanding balance, including any accrued interest, is immediately due from the guarantors.</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">If an event of default occurs and RY-8 is unable to pay the outstanding balance owed, we would, as guarantor, be liable for this balance.&nbsp; However, in conjunction with the Credit Agreement, RY-8 and RHI have entered into
an Indemnity Agreement and a Pledge of Interest and Security Agreement in favor of Outback Steakhouse, Inc.&nbsp; These agreements provide that if we are required to perform our obligation as guarantor pursuant to the Credit Agreement, then RY-8 and RHI will
indemnify OSI and OSP against all losses, claims, damages or liabilities which arise out of or are based upon our guarantee of the Credit Agreement.&nbsp; RY-8&rsquo;s and RHI&rsquo;s obligations under these agreements are secured by a first priority lien upon and a
continuing security interest in any and all of RY-8&rsquo;s interests in the Joint Venture.&nbsp;</font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman"></font></p>

<p><font size="3" face="Times New Roman">A copy of the Amendment to Credit Agreement is attached as Exhibit 10.1, a copy of the Amended Indemnity Agreement is attached as Exhibit 10.2, and a copy of the Amended Pledge of Interest and Security Agreement is attached as
Exhibit 10.3.</font></p>

<p align="center"><b><font size="2" face="CG Times">SECOND AMENDED AND RESTATED PLEDGE OF INTEREST AND SECURITY AGREEMENT</font></b></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; THIS SECOND AMENDED AND RESTATED PLEDGE OF INTEREST AND SECURITY AGREEMENT (&ldquo;Agreement&rdquo;) is executed this _____ day of December, 2004, by RY-8, INC., a
Hawaii corporation (the &ldquo;Borrower&rdquo;), on behalf of OUTBACK STEAKHOUSE, INC., a Delaware corporation (&ldquo;OSI&rdquo;), and OS PACIFIC, INC., a Florida corporation and wholly-owned subsidiary of OSI (&ldquo;OSP&rdquo;).&nbsp; OSI and OSP are sometimes
hereafter collectively called the "Guarantors".</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, the Borrower and the Guarantors are parties to that certain Credit and Guaranty Agreement with WACHOVIA BANK, N.A., a national banking association (the &ldquo;Bank&rdquo;), pursuant to which the Bank has agreed to provide
Borrower with a credit facility in the original amount of TWELVE MILLION AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($12,000,000.00) (the &ldquo;Original Line of Credit&rdquo;); and</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, the Borrower and the Guarantors have entered into that certain First Amendment to Credit and Guaranty Agreement with the Bank dated October 19, 2001, pursuant to which the Bank agreed to increase the Original Line of Credit
to TWENTY-FOUR MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($24,500,000.00) in the aggregate (the &ldquo;Amended Line of Credit&rdquo;); and</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, the Borrower and the Guarantors have entered into that certain Second Amendment to Credit and Guaranty Agreement of even date herewith with the Bank, pursuant to which the Bank agreed to extend the termination date of the

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, as a condition of providing the Line of Credit and the Amended Line of Credit to Borrower, the Bank&nbsp; required that the Guarantors guarantee the Line of Credit and the Amended Line of Credit; and</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, as a condition of providing the guaranty of the Original Line of Credit and the Amended Line of Credit, Guarantors have required that the Borrower enter into, respectively, that certain Indemnity Agreement dated October 31,
2000 (&ldquo;Original Indemnity Agreement&rdquo;) and that certain Amended and Restated Indemnity Agreement dated October 19, 2001 (&ldquo;Amended Indemnity Agreement&rdquo;) with Guarantors, pursuant to which the Borrower agreed to indemnify the Guarantors for any

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, as a condition of providing the Second Amended Line of Credit to Borrower, the Bank has required that the Guarantors guarantee the Second Amended Line of Credit (the &ldquo;Guaranty&rdquo;); and</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, as a condition of providing the Guaranty, Guarantors have required that the Borrower enter into that certain Second Amended and Restated Indemnity Agreement of even date herewith (the &ldquo;Second Amended Indemnity

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, to evidence Borrower&rsquo;s pledge of its entire interest in the Roy&rsquo;s/Outback Joint Venture to secure such indemnification obligations Borrower entered into the certain Pledge of Interest and Security Agreement dated

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">WHEREAS, the Borrower desires to further amend and restate the Amended Security Agreement in its entirety to reflect the effect of the extension of the termination date of the Second Amended Line of Credit and certain other

Venture, a Florida general partnership (the &ldquo;Joint Venture&rdquo;) and all proceeds of and other rights in connection with such interests (the &ldquo;Collateral&rdquo;).&nbsp; Borrower warrants that the Collateral is validly issued and outstanding and that
Borrower is the owner of the Collateral free from all liens, security interests or encumbrances of any nature with authority to pledge such Collateral to Guarantors.</font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times"></font></p>

<p><font size="2" face="CG Times">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <u>PRESERVATION OF COLLATERAL</u>.&nbsp; Borrower shall (i)&nbsp;keep the Collateral free from all liens,

Borrower&rsquo;s name on the Collateral; and (vi) pay any amount due with respect to the Collateral.&nbsp; In the event Guarantors pay any amounts due with respect to the Collateral on behalf of Borrower, the cost arising therefrom shall be one of the Obligations
