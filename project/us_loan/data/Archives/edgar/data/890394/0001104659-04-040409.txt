<h1 style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</font></b></h1>

<h1 style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 14, 2004, the Board of Directors of Monolithic System
Technology, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) authorized and adopted a Second Amendment (the
&#147;Second Amendment&#148;) to Rights Agreement, entered into between the Company and
Wells Fargo Bank, N.A., the stock transfer agent of the Company.&#160; The Second Amendment further amends the
Rights Agreement (the &#147;Rights Agreement&#148;) entered into between the Company and
Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. on October 11, 2000 and first amended on February 23,
2004.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The purpose of the Second Amendment is to enable Ingalls &amp; Snyder
LLC (&#147;I&amp;S&#148;), a registered broker dealer and investment advisor, to acquire
common stock of the Company exceeding the 15% limitation on beneficial
ownership under the Rights Agreement so long as such shares of common stock are
not acquired or held with the purpose or effect of changing or influencing
control of the Company, or in connection with or as a participant in any
transaction having such purpose or effect.&#160;
This exclusion is limited to shares of common stock as to which I&amp;S
brokerage account managers share dispositive power, have only non-discretionary
voting power and have no direct or indirect pecuniary interest.&#160; Any single account holder or retail customer
of I&amp;S, and any single broker at I&amp;S with respect to such broker&#146;s
personal trading account, shall remain subject to the 15% limitation on
beneficial ownership contained in the Rights Agreement.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A copy of the Second Amendment is attached hereto as Exhibit 4.01 and
is incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>

<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Exhibit 4.01</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECOND AMENDMENT TO RIGHTS AGREEMENT<br>
BETWEEN MONOLITHIC SYSTEM TECHNOLOGY, INC. AND <br>
WELLS FARGO BANK, N.A., AS RIGHTS AGENT</font></b></p>

<p align="left" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">THIS SECOND AMENDMENT TO RIGHTS AGREEMENT
(this &#147;Amendment&#148;) is made as of December 14, 2004, by and between Monolithic
System Technology, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) and Wells Fargo
Bank, N.A., formerly Wells Fargo Minnesota, N.A. (the &#147;Rights Agent&#146;).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Company and the Rights Agent are
parties to that certain Rights Agreement, dated as of October 11, 2000, as
amended by the First Amendment thereto made as of February 23, 2004 (as so
amended, the &#147;Rights Agreement&#148;) specifying the terms of the Rights (as defined
therein);</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Company is aware that Ingalls
&amp; Snyder LLC, a registered broker dealer and a registered investment
advisor (&#147;I&amp;S&#148;) invests and holds securities for the accounts of its
Affiliates, Associates and certain retail customers for whom individual brokers
at I&amp;S manage accounts over which they hold investment discretion;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Company understands that&#160; I&amp;S possesses shared dispositive&#160; power but not voting power or any pecuniary
interest with respect to Common Shares acquired in the name of I&amp;S for
customer accounts managed by individual brokers employed by I&amp;S, other than
personal trading accounts belonging to such brokers (I&amp;S and all Persons
acting as brokerage account managers at I&amp;S are referred to collectively as
&#147;I&amp;S Parties and individually as an I&amp;S Party), and that all Common
Shares held by I&amp;S Parties are acquired solely for investment purposes and
not with a purpose or effect of changing or influencing control of the Company,
or in connection with or as a participant in any transaction having that
purpose or effect;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Board of Directors has
determined pursuant to the authorization granted to it under the Rights
Agreement that for purposes of the Rights Agreement the I&amp;S Parties might
be deemed to beneficially own all Common Shares acquired by all and any of the
I&amp;S Parties;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of the
Company has determined that it is in the interests of the Company&#146;s
stockholders that the Rights Agreement be amended as set forth in this
Amendment to enable the acquisition by I&amp;S Parties, collectively, of shared
beneficial ownership of Common Shares outstanding from time to time and that
such acquisitions will not cause the I&amp;S Parties to become an Acquiring
Person or result in a Shares Acquisition Date or otherwise trigger the Rights;
provided that single account holders or retail customers and individual brokers
at I&amp;S will remain subject to the 15% limitation with respect to their
personal accounts;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Company has delivered to the
Rights Agent the appropriate writing in accordance with Section 27 of the
Rights Agreement; and</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, the Company desires to amend the
Rights Agreement in accordance with Section 27 of the Rights Agreement.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the
premises and mutual agreements set forth herein and in the Rights Agreement,
the parties hereby agree as follows:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section&nbsp;1.</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Defined Terms</p>

<p style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Capitalized
terms that are not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings ascribed to
them in the Rights Agreement.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section&nbsp;2.</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Amendment to
Rights Agreement</p>

<p style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(a)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Section
1(a) of the Rights Agreement, which sets forth the definition of &#147;Acquiring
Person,&#148; is hereby amended by adding the following new paragraph at the end of
Section 1(a):</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 1.0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Notwithstanding
anything in this Agreement that might otherwise be deemed to the contrary, none
of the I&amp;S Parties, individually or collectively, shall be deemed to be an
Acquiring Person as long as none of the Common Shares held by any of the
I&amp;S Parties are acquired or held with a purpose or effect of changing or
influencing control of the Company, or in connection with or as a participant
in any transaction having that purpose or effect, as determined in the sole
discretion of the Board of Directors of the Company.&#160; This exclusion is limited to Common Shares as
to which I&amp;S parties share dispositive power but have only limited voting
power of the type described in SEC Rule 13d-3(d)(2).&#160; Any single account holder or retail customer
of I&amp;S, and any single broker at I&amp;S with respect to such broker&#146;s
personal trading account, shall remain subject to the 15% limitation on
Beneficial Ownership contained in this Section 1(a).&#160; For purposes of this paragraph shares of
Common Stock as to which an I&amp;S Party has, or I&amp;S Parties have,
dispositive power and only non-discretionary voting power (as defined in SEC
Rule 13d-3(d)(2)), with no &#147;direct pecuniary interest&#148; or &#147;indirect pecuniary
interest&#148; (as defined under SEC Rule 16a-1(a)) therein, shall be excluded from
the calculation of Beneficial Ownership under Section 1(a).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(b)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; As
a consequence of the foregoing amendment to Section 1(a) of the Rights
Agreement, and subject to the condition stated in Section 1(a), the acquisition
or continued beneficial ownership by I&amp;S Parties of Common Shares will not
entitle or permit the holders of the Rights to exercise the Rights or otherwise
affect the rights of the holders of Rights except as otherwise stated in
Section 1(a), as amended hereby, and will not constitute a Shares Acquisition
Date or a Trigger Event, nor will it result in a Distribution Date.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section&nbsp;3.</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Effectiveness.</p>

<p style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Except as set forth herein, the Rights
Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and shall be otherwise
unaffected by this Amendment.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section&nbsp;4.</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Miscellaneous.</p>

<p style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 1.25in;page-break-after:auto;text-indent:-1.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(a)&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; If any term, provision, covenant or
