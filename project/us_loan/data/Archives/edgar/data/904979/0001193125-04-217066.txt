per share, diluted. For fiscal 2003, the Company reported net income of $3.6 million, or $0.22 per share, basic and diluted, and after including an after-tax goodwill impairment charge of $(2.0) million, or $(0.13) per share, recorded in the first
quarter of fiscal 2003 associated with an accounting change. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"

in the fourth quarter, and the continued strong demand of the heavy truck and lawn and garden industries. The new fourth </FONT></P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">quarter vehicle launches for which the Company is supplying parts included the Chevrolet Cobalt and Pontiac G6 models as

most of our operations continued to favorably affect operating results. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
