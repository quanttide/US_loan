ITEM 8.01

Other Events.
 

          On December 21, 2004, DIMON Incorporated and Standard Commercial Corporation jointly issued a press release announcing that, in connection with DIMON’s and Standard’s previously announced agreement to merge, the waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976 has expired without agency action.  A copy of that press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1.
