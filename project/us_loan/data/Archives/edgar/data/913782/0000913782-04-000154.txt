<p align="center"><b>GABLES' CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER TO RESIGN</b></p>



<p style='line-height:150%'>BOCA RATON, FLORIDA - December 17, 2004
- - Gables Residential (NYSE: GBP) (the &quot;Company&quot;), the 2004 NAHB Property Management Company of the
Year, today announced that after 20 years
of service, Mike Hefley, Chief Operating Officer, has decided to step away from
the business in order to spend more time with his family.&nbsp; Mr. Hefley informed
the Company that he intends to resign from Gables in 2005 and assist with an
orderly transition for his successor.&nbsp; The Company will begin a COO search
process immediately and has retained the services of Dru Haskins of Equinox
Partners.</p>



<p style='line-height:150%'>Mr. Hefley stated, &quot;I believe Gables
has achieved the level of operating excellence that will allow it to continue
to out perform in our markets, and I am proud to have had the chance to play a
role in building this great operating team.&quot;</p>



<p style='line-height:150%'>&quot;Mike, in addition to being a great
friend, has been instrumental in building the team and the systems that have
allowed Gables to attain the position of excellence we enjoy today.&nbsp; We wish
him well as he transitions into a new phase in his life,&quot; stated Chris Wheeler, CEO.</p>



<p align=left style='margin-right:-52.1pt;text-align:
left;text-indent:0in;line-height:150%'>With a
mission of Taking Care of the Way People Live&reg;, Gables
Residential has received national recognition for excellence in the management,
development, acquisition and construction of luxury multi-family communities in
high job growth markets. The Company's strategic objective is to produce total
returns through monthly dividends and share price changes that exceed the
NAREIT apartment sector index. </p>





<div class=MsoBodyTextIndent align=center style='margin-right:0in;text-align:
center;text-indent:0in'>

<hr size=2 width="100%" noshade color=navy align=center>

</div>



<br clear=all style='page-break-before:always'>










<p align=left style='margin-right:-52.1pt;text-align:
left;text-indent:0in;line-height:150%'>The
Company has a research-driven strategy focused on markets characterized by high
job growth and resiliency to national economic downturns. Within these markets,
the Company targets Established Premium Neighborhoods&trade; (&quot;EPN's&quot;), generally defined as areas with high per square foot prices
for single-family homes. By investing in resilient, demand-driven markets and
EPN locations with barriers to entry, the Company expects to achieve its
strategic objective. </p>



<p align=left style='margin-right:-52.1pt;text-align:
left;text-indent:0in;line-height:150%'><a
name="OLE_LINK1">The Company is one of the
largest apartment operators in the nation and currently manages 43,715
apartment homes in 169 communities, including the 85 communities it owns with
21,961 stabilized apartment homes primarily in Atlanta, Houston, South Florida,
Austin, Dallas, Washington, DC, and San Diego/Inland Empire and has an
additional 7 communities with 1,835 apartment homes under development or
lease-up. For further information, please contact Gables Investor Relations at
(800) 371-2819 or access Gables Residential's website at <u>www.gables.com</u></a>.</p>
