ITEM 5.02.  DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS.

        On December 17, 2004, Mike Hefley, Chief Operating Officer, informed Gables Residential Trust (the "Company") that he has decided to step down from his position as Chief Operating Officer in order to spend more time with his family.  Mr. Hefley informed the Company that he intends to resign from the Company in 2005 and assist with an orderly transition for his successor.  The Company will begin a Chief Operating Officer search process immediately and has retained the services of Equinox Partners.
