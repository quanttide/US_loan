<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>ITEM 5.02 </B>&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS;
APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Boards of Directors of WGL Holdings, Inc. and Washington Gas Light Company have policies
that do not enable directors to stand for re-election to the Boards of Directors after their
seventy-second birthday. Daniel J. Callahan, III, currently a director of WGL Holdings, Inc. and
Washington Gas Light Company, is seventy-two years old. Accordingly, Mr.&nbsp;Callahan will not stand
for re-election to the companies&#146; Boards of Directors at the next annual meeting on February&nbsp;23,
2005, and his tenure on the boards will cease on February&nbsp;23, 2005.
