Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors;
Appointment of Principal Officers.

On December 29, 2004, the Board of Directors of Digital Fusion, Inc. (the
"Company") elected Charles F. "Chuck" Lofty as a director of the Company for a
term expiring at the 2005 Annual Meeting of Shareholders. Mr. Lofty was also
appointed to the Audit Committee.

Item 7.01  Regulation FD Disclosure.

The Company issued a press release announcing Mr. Lofty's election which is
attached as Exhibit 99.1.
