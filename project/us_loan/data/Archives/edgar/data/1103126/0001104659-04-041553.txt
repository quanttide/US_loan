<p style="margin:0pt 0pt .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Watson Wyatt &amp; Company Holdings received a subpoena on December 17
from the State of New York, Office of the Attorney General (&#147;NYAG&#148;) requesting
information in connection with an investigation the NYAG is conducting
regarding compensation in connection with insurance placements, including
so-called override payments.&nbsp; Watson Wyatt will be cooperating fully with
the NYAG&#146;s investigation. Preliminary data indicate that override payments
accounted for less than 2/10 of 1% of the firm&#146;s revenues over the past four
fiscal years.</font></p>
