Item 3.02 Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.

 

On December 29, 2004, we agreed to issue 2,737,350 shares of Class A common stock in exchange for approximately $98.1 million fully accreted face value at maturity of 14% Senior Secured Discount Notes due 2009, issued by our subsidiary, XM Satellite Radio Inc., and guaranteed by us, in transactions exempt from registration under Section 3(a)(9) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. We expect to deliver the shares on or about December 30, 2004.
