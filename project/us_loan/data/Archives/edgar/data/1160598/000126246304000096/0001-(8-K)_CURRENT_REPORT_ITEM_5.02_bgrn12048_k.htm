Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officer

On December 22, 2004, the Company accepted the resignation of Jerry Brown from the Board of Directors. Prior to the resignation herein described, the Board of Directors appointed Mr. Robert Madia to the Board of Directors. The Board of Directors now consists of Robert Madia, James Medeiros, Bradford Miller, Gordon Sales and Alan Smith.

Robert Madia, Director - Mr. Madia has owned and operated an insurance agency in the Pittsburgh area since 1957. The agency handles all types of insurance for individuals and businesses. Along with a couple of non-profit organizations, he is also on the board of directors of a large, regional, bottled water company in Virginia and North Carolina, Diamond Springs Water Company.
