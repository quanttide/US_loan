Item 1.01.  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

 

On December 15, 2004, Integrity Mutual Funds, Inc. (“Integrity”) entered into an amendment to its original employment agreement with Bradley Wells, dated June 1, 2002.  The amendment shall be retroactively effective as of June 1, 2002.

 

The amendment provides that Mr. Wells will continue to receive a base salary and performance-based incentives, although the calculation of Mr. Wells’ recruitment override has been modified.  Mr. Wells will also receive a one-time payment consisting of $12,500 in cash, 100,000 common shares of Integrity, and options to purchase 250,000 common shares of Integrity at a strike price of $1.00 per share.  The options will expire on October 31, 2014.
