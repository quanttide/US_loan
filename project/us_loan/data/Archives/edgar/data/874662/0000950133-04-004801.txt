<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;23, 2004, Bayfield Low Income Housing Limited Partnership (&#147;Bayfield&#148;) entered
into a Purchase Agreement (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) by and among Bayfield, MPF Bayfield Acquisition, LLC
(the &#147;Purchaser&#148;) and Mackenzie Patterson Fuller, Inc. (the &#147;Guarantor&#148;) for the sale of certain
limited partnership interests held by Bayfield (the &#147;Partnership Interests&#148;). Pursuant to the
Agreement, the Purchaser agrees to purchase up to 49.5% of the Partnership Interests in each of 81
limited partnerships (the &#147;Partnerships&#148;) in which Bayfield is the limited partner. The Agreement
also provides the Purchaser with options to purchase the remaining Partnership Interests in the
Partnerships when such purchase and sale of the remaining Partnership Interests will not cause
termination of the Partnerships and subject to the consents of a majority in interest of the
limited partners of Bayfield. Under the terms of the Agreement, Guarantor will unconditionally
guarantee the Purchaser&#146;s obligations under the Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursuant to the terms of the Agreement, in exchange for up to 49.5% of the Partnership
Interests in the Partnerships owned by Bayfield, the Purchaser agrees to provide the following:

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<I>Options</I>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursuant to the Agreement, Bayfield grants Buyer (i)&nbsp;an option to purchase an additional 16.5%
of the Partnership Interests in certain of the Partnerships exercisable on or between January&nbsp;1,
2005 and January&nbsp;15, 2005, (the &#147;Short Option&#148;) and (ii)&nbsp;an option to purchase the balance of the
Partnership Interests in the Partnerships on or between December&nbsp;1, 2005 and December&nbsp;30, 2005 (the
&#147;First Option&#148;). Purchases pursuant to the First Option will require the consents of a majority in
interest of the limited partners of Bayfield. The aggregate consideration to be paid under the
Agreement if the Short Option is exercised shall be $34,494 and $1,740,594 if the sale under the
First Option is approved and exercised.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Agreement provides for a repurchase of any Partnership Interests by Bayfield in the event
that a Partnership experiences an event that would cause a minimum gain chargeback with respect to
that Partnership such that the Purchaser will not be able to match such income allocations with
applicable losses from the sale of such Partnership Interests.
