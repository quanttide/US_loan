                Section 5 - Corporate Governance and Management


ITEM 5.02.     DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF
               DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS.

On December 30, 2004, Heartland Partners, L.P. (the "Company") issued a press
release announcing the resignation of Daniel Bernardi, the Company's Chief
Financial Officer. The Company has engaged RSM McGladrey to provide accounting
services. This press release also announced the resignation of Richard
Brandstatter, President of CMC Heartland (a subsidiary of the Company),
effective January 1, 2005.
