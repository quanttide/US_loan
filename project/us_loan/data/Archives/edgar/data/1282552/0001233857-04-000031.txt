<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>AAMES
INVESTMENT CORPORATION</FONT><BR>
<FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Declares Initial Common Stock Dividend of $0.06 per share</FONT></H1>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>Los Angeles, California,
December 21, 2004 &#150; Aames Investment Corporation (NYSE: AIC)</B>, a
mortgage real estate investment trust, today announced that its Board of
Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.06 per common share for the period from
November 5, 2004, the date of Aames initial public offering, to year-end. The
dividend is payable on January 31, 2005, to shareholders of record as of
December 31, 2004. The ex-dividend date is December 29, 2004. New shareholders
interested in receiving this dividend must purchase common stock on or before
December 28, 2004. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The initial dividend represents
the distribution of more than 90% of the estimated taxable earnings for the period
from the Company&#146;s reorganization and initial public offering on November
5, 2004 through year-end, exclusive of any net income earned by its taxable REIT
subsidiary, Aames Financial Corporation, during the period, and therefore is not
expected to be a return of capital. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;We are very pleased
to declare our first dividend as a REIT,&#148; stated A. Jay Meyerson, Aames
Investment&#146;s Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer.
&#147;However, our initial dividend is reflective of the fact that we completed
our initial public offering and began building our loan portfolio in early
November,&#148; Mr. Meyerson explained. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The Company expects to
declare its first full regular quarterly dividend after the end of the first
quarter ended March 31, 2005. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>For more information,
contact either Ronald J. Nicolas, Jr., Executive Vice President and Chief
Financial Officer, or Jon D. Van Deuren, Senior Vice President, Finance and
Chief Accounting Officer, in Aames Investment&#146;s Investor Relations
Department at (323) 210-5311 or at <U>info@aamescorp.com</U> via email. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Additional information may
also be obtained by visiting <U>www.aames.net</U>, Aames Investment&#146;s
website. </FONT></P>
