Cell Therapeutics, Inc. Completes Registered Direct Offering

 

SEATTLE, Dec. 22, 2004 —Cell Therapeutics, Inc. (NASDAQ and Nuovo Mercato: CTIC) today announced that it has completed the previously announced sale of approximately 2,586,000 shares of its common stock to several institutional investors at a negotiated price per share of $7.10.

 

About Cell Therapeutics, Inc.

 

Headquartered in Seattle, CTI is a biopharmaceutical company committed to developing an integrated portfolio of oncology products aimed at making cancer more treatable. For additional information, please visit www.cticseattle.com.
