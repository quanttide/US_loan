<BR></FONT><FONT SIZE=2><B>ITEM 1.02 TERMINATION OF A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT    <BR>    </B></FONT></P>


<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;24, 2004, Mrs.&nbsp;Fields Franchising, LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Mrs.&nbsp;Fields Famous Brands, LLC ("Mrs.&nbsp;Fields"), and
Shadewell Grove IP, LLC ("Shadewell"), entered into an agreement terminating the Trademark License Agreement between their predecessors-in-interest dated January&nbsp;2, 2002
(the "License Agreement"). A copy of the License Agreement is included as Exhibit&nbsp;10.42 to Mrs.&nbsp;Fields Famous Brands, LLC's Registration Statement on Form&nbsp;S-4, as
amended, filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April&nbsp;30, 2004. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
License Agreement granted Shadewell certain rights to produce and market frozen cookie dough under the Mrs.&nbsp;Fields&reg; trademark and tradename in identified channels
of distribution in exchange for the payment of license fees and royalties during the term of the License Agreement. The termination agreement reached between the parties provides for payment by
Mrs.&nbsp;Fields to Shadewell of a termination fee of $1,050,000, and Shadewell's indemnification and release of Mrs.&nbsp;Fields for any and all claims arising from the License Agreement. </FONT></P>
