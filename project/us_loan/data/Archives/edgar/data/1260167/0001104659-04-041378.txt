<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 22, 2004, Worldspan, L.P. (the &#147;Company&#148;) entered into
Amendment No. 10 (the &#147;Amendment&#148;) to the CRS Marketing, Services and
Development Agreement (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) between the Company and IAC Global, LLC
(as successor in interest to Microsoft Corporation).&#160; The Agreement provides the terms under which
the Company provides travel information to and processing services for IAC
Global, LLC.&#160; The Amendment alters the
manner in which the Company compensates IAC Global, LLC for car rentals booked
using the Company's processing services, and it addresses the responsibilities
associated with communication fees and related expenses.</font></p>
