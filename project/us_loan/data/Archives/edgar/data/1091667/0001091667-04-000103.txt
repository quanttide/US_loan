<B><P>ITEM 8.01.  OTHER EVENTS. </P></B>

<FONT SIZE=2><P ALIGN="JUSTIFY">On December 23, 2004, Charter Communications, Inc. ("Charter") completed
the redemption of approximately $588 million principal amount of its 5.75% Convertible Senior Notes due
October 2005, at 101.15% of principal amount, plus accrued and unpaid interest to the date of
redemption.  Charter had agreed to such redemption using a portion of the proceeds of its November 22,
2004 sale of approximately $862.5 million original principal amount of Convertible Senior Notes due 2009
at the time of such notes sale.  The total cost of the redemption to Charter was approximately $601.1
million.  A press release announcing the completion of the redemption is attached hereto as Exhibit 99-1.

<B><FONT FACE="Arial" SIZE=4><P ALIGN="CENTER">Charter Communications, Inc. Completed Redemption<br>
of $588 Million 5.75% Convertible Senior Notes</P>
<P ALIGN="CENTER"></P>
</B></FONT><P ALIGN="CENTER">&nbsp;</P>
<B><P>ST. LOUIS -- </B>Charter Communications, Inc. (&quot;Charter&quot;) (Nasdaq: CHTR) today announced that on December 23, 2004, it completed the redemption of approximately $588 million principal amount of its 5.75% Convertible Senior Notes due October 2005, at 101.15% of principal amount, plus accrued and unpaid interest to the date of redemption.<FONT FACE="Arial" SIZE=2 COLOR="#0000ff"> </FONT>Charter had agreed to such redemption using a portion of the proceeds of its November 22, 2004 sale of approximately $862.5 million original principal amount of Convertible Senior Notes due 2009 at the time of such notes sale.  The total cost of the redemption to Charter was approximately $601.1 million. </P>
<FONT SIZE=3><P ALIGN="CENTER">#  #  #</P>
</FONT><B><FONT SIZE=2><P>About Charter Communications</P>
</B><P>Charter Communications, Inc., a broadband communications company, provides a full range of advanced broadband services to the home, including cable television on an advanced digital video programming platform via Charter Digital&trade; and Charter High-Speed&trade; Internet service. Charter also provides business-to-business video, data and Internet protocol (IP) solutions through Charter Business&trade;. Advertising sales and production services are sold under the Charter Media&reg; brand. More information about Charter can be found at <FONT SIZE=2>www.charter.com</FONT></A><FONT SIZE=2>.</P>
