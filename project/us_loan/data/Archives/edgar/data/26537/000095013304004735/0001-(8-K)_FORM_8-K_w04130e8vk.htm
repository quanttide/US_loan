ARGON ST, INC. AND SUBSIDIARIES

ITEM 5.04 TEMPORARY SUSPENSION OF TRADING UNDER REGISTRANT’S EMPLOYEE BENEFIT PLANS

On December 23, 2004, ARGON ST, Inc. (the “Company”) sent a notice to its directors and Section 16 officers notifying them of a blackout period with respect to the ARGON ST, Inc. 401(k) Savings Plan. The blackout period is expected to begin January 24, 2005 and end February 25, 2005. The Company provided the notice to the directors and officers in accordance with Section 306 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 and Rule 104 of Regulation BTR. A copy of the notice is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference.
