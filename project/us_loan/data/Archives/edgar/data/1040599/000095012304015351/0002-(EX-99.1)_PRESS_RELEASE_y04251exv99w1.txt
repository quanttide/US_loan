                     NEENAH FILES ANNUAL REPORT WITH THE SEC

              COMPANY SCHEDULES CONFERENCE CALL TO DISCUSS RESULTS


NEENAH, WI, December 23, 2004 - Neenah Foundry Company ("Neenah" or the
"Company") announced today that it has filed its Annual Report on Form 10-K for
the 12 month period ending September 30, 2004 with the Securities and Exchange
Commission on Thursday, December 23, 2004. Due to the recent registration of its
11% Senior Secured Notes and the 13% Senior Subordinated Notes, the Company is
required to file quarterly and annual financial reports with the SEC.

In addition to announcing the filing of the 10-K, Neenah's President & CEO,
William Barrett, has also scheduled a conference call to answer questions
regarding the recent filing. The conference call is scheduled for Wednesday,
January 5, 2005 at 10:00 a.m. (Central Daylight Time). The call-in number

ABOUT NEENAH FOUNDRY COMPANY

Neenah Foundry Company manufactures and markets a wide range of iron castings
and steel forgings for the heavy municipal market and selected segments of the
industrial markets.
Neenah is one of the larger independent foundry companies in the country, with
substantial market share in the municipal and various industrial markets for
gray and ductile iron castings and forged steel products. Additional information
about Neenah is available on the Company's web site at www.nfco.com.
