<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><U>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events</U>.</B>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;21, 2004, we filed our quarterly report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended September
30, 2004. After filing the report, we identified a transposition error in the production levels
referenced on page 29 in the section entitled &#147;Item&nbsp;2 &#151; Management&#146;s Discussion and Analysis of
Financial Condition and Results of Operations &#151; Segment Results &#151; Unregulated Businesses &#151;
Production Segment &#151; Production and Capital Expenditures.&#148; In the second sentence of the first
paragraph of that subsection, we stated that &#147;We expect our fourth quarter of 2004 production to
average 330 MMcfe/d and our annual production to average 295 MMcfe/d.&#148; Those numbers were
inadvertently reversed.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The second sentence in the first paragraph under the subsection &#147;Production and Capital
Expenditures&#148; on page 29 should read, &#147;We expect our fourth quarter of 2004 production to average
295 MMcfe/d and our annual production to average 330 MMcfe/d.&#148;
