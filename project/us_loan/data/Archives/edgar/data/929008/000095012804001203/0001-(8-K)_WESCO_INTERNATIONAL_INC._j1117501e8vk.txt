Item 8.01.  Other Events

     On December 22, 2004, WESCO International, Inc. (the "Company") closed its
public offering of 10,000,000 shares of its common stock, par value $.01 per
share ("Common Stock"), including 6,000,000 shares of Common Stock sold by
certain of the Company's existing stockholders, pursuant to the Underwriting
Agreement dated December 16, 2004 by and among the Company, certain selling
stockholders and Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Lehman Brothers Inc., as
representatives of the underwriters listed in Schedule II thereto (the
"Underwriters"), at a price of $26.25 per share.  The offering was made pursuant
to a registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-119909), including a
related Prospectus dated December 6, 2004, as supplemented by a Prospectus
Supplement dated December 16, 2004.

        The Underwriting Agreement dated December 16, 2004 relating to the
offering is filed herewith as Exhibit 1.1.  The opinion of Kirkpatrick &
Lockhart LLP regarding the validity of the common stock sold by the Company in
the offering is filed herewith as Exhibit 5.1.
