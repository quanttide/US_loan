<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Item 2.01.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets. </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Meritage
completed a sale and leaseback transaction on December 20, 2004, involving one of its
Wendy&#146;s restaurants. This was the third sale and leaseback of a Wendy&#146;s
restaurant to unrelated third parties during the month of December 2004 (the
&#147;December Transactions&#148;), and is part of an ongoing effort that Meritage
announced on November 4, 2004, to sell and leaseback a certain number of its Wendy&#146;s
restaurants to pay down long-term debt and increase cash on hand to support the
Company&#146;s strategic objective of maintaining a strong balance sheet and a
less-leveraged new store growth model. On December 3, 2004, the Company reported sale and
leaseback transactions involving three Wendy&#146;s restaurants occurring during the month
of November, 2004. This report is being filed because the proceeds received from the
December Transactions, in the aggregate, exceeded 10% of the Company&#146;s total assets. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
Company collected $4,790,000 in net proceeds from the December Transactions, used
$3,195,000 to pay down long-term indebtedness, and deposited the balance of $1,595,000
into the Company&#146;s treasury. The sales resulted in a deferred gain of $1,768,000
before income taxes which will be amortized over the 20-year lease terms. The sales
resulted in a one-time finance charge of approximately $192,000. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Subject
to market conditions, the Company may enter into additional sale and leaseback
transactions containing similar terms, with related charges and deferred gains, during
this fiscal year. These transactions could result in additional net proceeds of
approximately $16.7 million. The Company anticipates that it would use approximately 60%
of the pre-tax proceeds to pay off long-term debt, with remaining net proceeds being
deposited into treasury and available for general corporate purposes including new
O&#146;Charley&#146;s and Wendy&#146;s restaurant development. </FONT></P>
