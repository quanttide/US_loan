ITEM 1.01.  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

BOK Financial Plans to Acquire Phoenix-based Valley Commerce Bancorp

     On December 20, 2004, BOK Financial entered into a definitive merger
agreement to acquire Phoenix based Valley Commerce Bancorp Ltd. and its Valley
Commerce Bank subsidiary, giving the regional financial services company its
first full-service banking presence in the nation's sixth largest city.
Additional information regarding this agreement may be found at Exhibit 99 (a)
Text of Press Release dated December 21, 2004 and at Exhibit 10(a) Definitive
Merger Agreement.
