<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>Altus Explorations, Inc</B>.
(&#147;Altus&#148; or &#147;Assignor&#148;), a Nevada Corporation, and <B>Orbit Energy,
LLC </B>(&#147;Orbit&#148; or &#147;Operator&#148;), a Nevada Limited Liability Company,
are Parties to a Farmout Agreement made and entered into on the <SUP>30th</SUP> day of
June, 2004. Pursuant to the Farmout Agreement, Altus assigned its interests in certain oil
and gas leases in Chautaque County, Kansas to Orbit as Operator. Acreage associated with
such oil and gas leases is collectively identified as the Lake Creek Prospect Acreage. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>In connection with the continuing
development of the Kansas acreage, Altus and Orbit desire to amend the Lake Creek Prospect
Farmout Agreement to provide Altus the right to continue to participate in the drilling
and development of prospect acreage located outside the original Lake Creek Prospect
Acreage. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Pursuant to this amendment, Altus and
Orbit agree that Altus has the right to participate in the drilling and development of
Lake Creek area acreage with a working interest up to 25%, of which 10% shall be
&#147;carried&#148; by Orbit while Altus will be responsible for its proportionate share
of capital expenditures on the remaining 15% working interest. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>This amendment and Altus&#146; right
to participate in the continued drilling and development of Lake Creek area acreage
outside the original Lake Creek Prospect Acreage relates solely to the drilling and
completion of the Lake Creek No. 2 well, the Lake Creek No. 3 well, and the Lake Creek No.
4 well. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Altus&#146; timely remittance of
payment to Orbit, upon Orbit&#146;s request, for its proportionate share of capital
expenditures attributable to the 15% working interest shall constitute Altus&#146;
election to participate in the drilling and completion of a well. Non-remittance of
Altus&#146; proportionate share of capital expenditures when requested by Orbit, shall
constitute Altus&#146; election to participate in the drilling and completion of a well
with only its 10% &#147;carried&#148; working interest. </FONT></P>

Inc. Announces Second Kansas Well Spud </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Left-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A002></A>
<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>December 22, 2004 </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Left-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A003></A>
<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>OTCBB Symbol: ATUX </FONT></P>




<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>OLIVE BRANCH, MISSISSIPPI &#150;
Altus Explorations, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;Altus&#148;) announces that the
Lake Creek No. 3 well located in Chautaugua County in Southeast Kansas was spud on
December 20, 2004. The well&#146;s projected total depth is 2,500 feet to drill and test
the Arbuckle and the Mississippian formations. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The drilling of the Lake Creek No. 2
well that was spud on December 16, 2004 has been completed, with 4.5&#148; casing set at a
total depth of 1,695 feet to the Mississippian formation. Upon completion of the Lake
Creek No. 2 well, the drilling rig was moved to the Lake Creek No. 3 well location to
commence drilling. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Altus&#146; continuing participation
in the development of Kansas acreage, is pursuant to the amendment of its Lake Creek
Prospect Farmout Agreement with Orbit Energy, LLC (&#147;Orbit&#148;) dated December 16,
2004. In connection with the amendment, Altus received the right to continue its
participate in the drilling and development of the Lake Creek No. 2, Lake Creek No. 3, and
Lake Creek No. 4 wells whose acreage is located outside the original Lake Creek Prospect
area. Altus has the right to participate in the drilling and development of Lake Creek
area acreage with a working interest up to 25%, of which 10% shall be &#147;carried&#148;
by Orbit while Altus would be responsible for its proportionate share of capital
expenditures on the remaining 15% working interest. </FONT></P>
