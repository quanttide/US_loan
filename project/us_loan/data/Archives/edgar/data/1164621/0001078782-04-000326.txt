<P style="margin:0pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify><B>ITEM 4.01 &nbsp;CHANGES IN ACCOUNTANTS</B></P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt" align=justify><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:54pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(a)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:54pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">Previous independent accountants</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(i)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">On November 17, 2004, Weinberg &amp; Company, P.A., resigned from its position as the Company&#146;s independent accountants.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:54pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(ii)</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">The audit reports of Weinberg &amp; Company, P.A., for the year ended</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:306pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">&nbsp;December 31, 2003 contained an opinion expressing substantial doubt as to ability to continue as a going concern. Those audit reports contained no other adverse opinion, disclaimer of opinion or modification of the opinion.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(iii)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">OEF&#146;s Board of Directors participated in and approved the decision to change independent accountants.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(iv)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">In connection with its audit for the most recent fiscal year there have been no disagreements with Weinberg &amp; Company, P.A., on any matter of accounting principles or practices, financial statement disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure, which disagreement if not resolved to the satisfaction of Weinberg &amp; Company, P.A.,. would have caused them to make reference thereto in their report on the financial statements.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(v)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">During the two most recent fiscal years and the interim period until the date of dismissal there have been no reportable events (as defined in regulation S-K Item 304 (a)(1)(v)).</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:90pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(vi)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:90pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">OEF has requested that Weinberg &amp; Company, P.A., furnish it with a letter addressed to the SEC stating whether or not it agrees with the above statements. &nbsp;A copy of such letter is filed as an exhibit to this form 8-K.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:-13pt; padding-left:54pt; text-indent:-36pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">(b)</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:54pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">New Independent Accountants</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">On December 17, 2004, the board of directors voted to engage Hawkins Accounting, to audit its financial statements for the year ended December 31, 2004. OEF consulted Hawkins Accounting during the two most recent fiscal years regarding the application of accounting principles to a specified transaction, either completed or proposed, or the type of audit opinion that was rendered on the OEF&#146;s financial statements, and written reports and oral advise was provided to OEF by concluding there was an important factor to be considered by OEF in reaching a decision as to an accounting, auditing or financial issue. &nbsp;In the past two years OEF has not consulted Hawkins Accounting on any matter that was either the subject of a disagreement, as that term is defined in Item 304 (a)(1)(iv) of Regulation S-K and the related instructions to Item 304 of Regulation S-K, or a reportable event, as that term is define
d in Item 304 (a)(1)(v) of Regulation S-K.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; line-height:13pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt">As clarification, Hawkins Accounting was the Company&#146;s auditor prior to year ended December 31, 2003. &nbsp;Hawkins Accounting then became associated with Weinberg. &nbsp;Hawkins Accounting discontinued his association with Weinberg when his application to the PCAOB was approved.</P>
<P style="margin:0pt; padding-left:18pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:11pt"><BR></P>
