Item 1.01  Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

 

On December 21, 2004, The Medicines Company (the “Company”) entered into a consulting agreement with Strategic Imagery LLC.  Robert Savage, a member of the Company’s Board of Directors, is the President and the owner of Strategic Imagery LLC.  Under the terms of the consulting agreement, Mr. Savage will provide consulting services to the Company from time to time on organizational development and senior management coaching at an hourly rate of $625 per hour.  The initial term of the consulting agreement is one year, subject to renewal for successive periods upon further agreement of the parties. Either party may terminate the consulting agreement at any time upon 30 days written notice.
