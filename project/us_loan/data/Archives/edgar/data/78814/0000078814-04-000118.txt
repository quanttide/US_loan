ITEM 5.02.        DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF
                  DIRECTORS; APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS

(b) Departure of Directors

On December 15, 2004, Herbert L. Henkel gave notice of his intent not to stand
for re-election by stockholders upon the expiration of his term on May 9, 2005.
Mr. Henkel cited scheduling conflicts and the demands of his other commitments,
including service on three other public company boards, including the board of
Ingersoll-Rand, where he serves as chief executive officer.
