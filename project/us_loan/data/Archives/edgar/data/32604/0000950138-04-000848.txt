<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3><U>November &#145;04 Order
Comments:</U> </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3>Solid orders continued across the
company with favorable currency exchange rates contributing approximately 2.5 to 3
percentage points of the total +5% to +10% increase.</FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3> Process Management experienced strong
orders in measurement and analytical instruments, valves and power &amp; water solutions,
with strength in emerging markets.</FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3> Overall orders in Industrial Automation
were strong led by continued capital spending and industrial demand in North America.</FONT></P>


<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3>Orders in Network Power improved with strength in the systems business and strength in
Asia along with solid demand in North America. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3>Climate Technologies orders continue
to be impacted by slower growth in the U.S. residential air conditioning market and
tougher prior year comparisons.</FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=3> Orders in the Appliance and Tools segment stayed strong in
the storage business and were mixed in the motors, appliance, and tools businesses. </FONT></P>
