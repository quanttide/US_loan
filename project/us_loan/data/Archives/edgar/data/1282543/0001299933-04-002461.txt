<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>AFFIRMATIVE INSURANCE HOLDINGS, INC. ACQUIRES</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>AGENCY OPERATIONS IN FLORIDA AND TEXAS</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>ADDISON, Texas (December&nbsp;22, 2004) </B>&#150; Affirmative Insurance Holdings, Inc. (Nasdaq: AFFM) announced
today it has signed definitive agreements to acquire the assets of two non-standard automobile
insurance agency operations. In two separate transactions, Affirmative has agreed to acquire the
assets of the retail agency and franchise operations of 21<sup>st</sup> Century Holding Company
(Nasdaq: TCHC) and the assets of BW Insurance Agency, Inc.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Upon completion of the acquisition with 21<sup>st</sup> Century Holding Company, Affirmative will
acquire the assets of 24 company-owned retail stores and franchise operations consisting of 42
franchise locations operating in four designated market areas (&#147;DMA<sup>&#174;</sup>&#148;) in Florida. The
retail agency and franchise operations, which operate under the FED USA brand, produce
approximately $50.0&nbsp;million in non-standard automobile insurance premiums for multiple insurance
companies.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Affirmative will pay 21<sup>st</sup> Century Holding Company $7.0&nbsp;million in cash at closing and
will pay up to an additional $2.5&nbsp;million subject to certain performance criteria being met.
Following the close of the transaction, the retail stores and franchise locations will continue to
produce business for the insurance subsidiaries of 21<sup>st</sup> Century Holding Company as well
as for other insurance companies not affiliated with 21<sup>st</sup> Century Holding Company.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">In the transaction with BW Insurance Agency, Inc., Affirmative is acquiring the assets of 10 retail
stores in Texas. BW Insurance Agency, Inc. operates nine retail stores in the Houston
DMA<sup>&#174;</sup> and one retail store in the Beaumont DMA<sup>&#174;</sup>, and produces approximately
$5.0&nbsp;million of non-standard automobile insurance premium for multiple insurance companies.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Affirmative will pay BW Insurance Agency, Inc. $750,000 in cash at closing and will pay up to an
additional $750,000 subject to certain performance criteria being met. Following the close of the
transaction, the retail stores will be converted to the A-Affordable<sup>&#174;</sup> brand name and
will produce business under A-Affordable<sup>&#174;</sup>&#146;s multiple company distribution model.


<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt">- more -



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Both transactions are expected to be funded from cash on hand. The closings of both transactions
are expected to be completed within the next two weeks, subject to customary closing conditions.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;The retail agency and franchise operations acquired from 21<sup>st</sup> Century extend
Affirmative&#146;s distribution model in Florida and strengthen our position to capture a larger share
of the state&#146;s $3.7&nbsp;billion non-standard automobile insurance market,&#148; said Thomas E. Mangold,
president and chief executive officer of Affirmative Insurance Holdings, Inc. &#147;The acquisition of
the retail stores from BW increases our presence in the Houston DMA<sup>&#174;</sup> from 13 stores to
22 stores and expands our retail operations into the Beaumont DMA<sup>&#174;</sup>.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>ABOUT AFFIRMATIVE INSURANCE HOLDINGS</B>
<BR>
Headquartered in Addison, Texas, Affirmative Insurance Holdings, Inc., is a producer and provider
of non-standard automobile insurance policies to individual consumers in highly targeted geographic
markets. Affirmative currently offers products and services in 11 states, including Texas,
Illinois, California and Florida.
