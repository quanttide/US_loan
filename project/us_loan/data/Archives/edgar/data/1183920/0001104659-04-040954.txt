<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp; 1.01&nbsp;&nbsp; Entry
into a Material Definitive Agreement.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; On December 16,
2004, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Dade Behring
Holdings, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) established fiscal year 2005 performance
criteria for the annual incentive program (the &#147;Program&#148;) for executive
officers under the 2004 Incentive Compensation Plan.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Under the Program,
each executive officer has been assigned a target bonus determined as a
percentage of base salary.&#160; The target
bonus for the Chief Executive Officer (&#147;CEO&#148;) is 120 percent of base salary and
for each of the other executive officers is 75 percent of base salary.&#160; The maximum bonus amount is capped at approximately
200 percent of target bonus. &#160;The Program
will be funded (in the percentages indicated) upon achievement of the financial
measures established for each of the following: Net Income (50%), Cash Flow
(20%), EBITDA (earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization) (20%)
and Revenue Growth (10%). Each financial measure funds independently from the
others and the Program will be fully funded only if all four financial measures
are achieved. Each financial measure is more particularly described in the
Management Incentive Compensation Plan incorporated herein by reference. &#160;The calculation with respect to sixty percent
of each potential bonus amount is based upon a comparison of actual performance
for such financial measures to target objectives established for such financial
measures.&#160; The award related to the remaining
forty percent of the &#160;potential bonus is based
upon individual performance as evaluated and determined by the Compensation Committee
in the case of the CEO and as evaluated by the CEO and finally determined by
the Compensation Committee in the case of each other executive officer. &#160;Bonus amounts may be adjusted, but not
increased, at the discretion of the Compensation Committee.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="font-style:italic;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:normal;">&nbsp;</font></i></p>

<p style="font-style:italic;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:bold;">1.0&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; PLAN OBJECTIVES</font></i></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Dade Behring Management Incentive Compensation Plan was developed
to link a significant portion of Dade Behring&#146;s management employees&#146; total
cash compensation to the financial success of the company.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">This program is an integral component of the company&#146;s &#147;pay-for-performance&#148;
philosophy.&#160; The objectives of the Plan
are to:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Support Dade Behring&#146;s business plans and financial goals;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Motivate and reward both individual performance and teamwork;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Enable Dade Behring to attract and retain high quality managers.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The MICP plan structure is kept mostly consistent year over year for
continuity and simplicity purposes.&#160; The
emphasis on global Dade Behring results reinforce one Dade Behring/functional
concepts.&#160; In addition, the understanding
by participants will enhance the drive for results.&#160; Financial measures are reviewed each year to
ensure they correlate with the Dade Behring overall strategy and creation of
shareholder value.&#160; The plan continues to
place emphasis on global Dade Behring results to encourage a company-wide focus
on teamwork.&#160; Having said that, individual
performance is also a vital part of the plan.</font></p>

<h4 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></h4>

<h4 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">2.0&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; PARTICIPATION</font></b></h4>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">All US employees in salary grade level 17 and above are eligible to
participate in the Plan.&#160; Salary grade
level 16 Product Managers are also eligible to participate in the Plan.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For non-US employees, the participation is based on the US equivalent
grade level since global compensation structure is not formally in place in all
countries.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">All participants will be reviewed and approved by the Compensation
Administrative Committee contingent upon the participant&#146;s submission of
Individual Performance Objectives (IPOs).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Individuals hired or promoted into a bonus eligible position on or
before the close of the third fiscal quarter are eligible for a pro-rated bonus
target based on the number of full months of participation in the Plan.&#160; (Note:&#160;
The third quarter close deadline does not apply to job changes impacting
current participants in the plan.&#160; See
Section 3.1 for clarification.)</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Individuals hired or promoted into a bonus eligible position after the
close of the third fiscal quarter are <u>not</u> eligible to participate in the
Plan until the succeeding Plan year.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h2 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">3.0&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; TARGET
BONUS</font></b></h2>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The target bonus percentage will be communicated directly to individuals
through a participant letter from the CEO.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The MICP target bonus amount is determined by multiplying the
participant&#146;s annual base salary as of the close of the fiscal year by the
participant&#146;s target bonus percentage.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;text-align:center;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h3 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">4.1&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Global
Dade Behring Plan Funding</font></b></h3>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The MICP will fund 50% based on Dade Behring&#146;s Net Income
performance, 20% on Cash Flow performance, 20% on EBITDA performance, and 10%
on Revenue Growth performance.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The funding measurement targets have been approved by
the Board of Directors and may be subject to technical adjustments as a result
of business events or accounting transactions.&#160;
Results that fall between performance levels will be interpolated.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">5.0&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; PAYOUT
CALCULATION</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Once the MICP payout pools have been funded and approved by the Board
of Directors, individual bonus payouts will be calculated.&#160; The actual MICP award will be based on two
components:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h3 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;page-break-after:avoid;text-indent:-.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">a)</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Dade
Behring Overall Financial Performance</font></h3>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Sixty percent (60%) of a
participant&#146;s MICP award is based on how well Global Dade Behring has met its
financial objectives (Net Income, Cash Flow, EBITDA and Revenue Growth).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h3 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;page-break-after:avoid;text-indent:-.25in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">b)</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Individual
Performance</font></h3>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Forty percent (40%) of a
participant&#146;s award is based upon individual performance.&#160; Each participant&#146;s overall performance rating
will be determined by his or her supervisor, using the LEADR process.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Some functions may
mandate that 15% of the 40% individual performance payout will be measured by individual
financial objectives.&#160; These financial
objectives should reflect the way an individual supports his/her organization
(such as region target, functional target, cost center budget, etc.).&#160; One financial target should be of immediate
control (country target or cost center budget), with another financial target
measured at a higher level to reinforce teamwork (region instead of countries,
functional instead of departmental).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Other functions will
measure individual performance based primarily on the overall LEADR performance
rating as well as the results of individual performance objectives.&#160; These IPO&#146;s may still include individual
financial objectives, along with other project or initiative based goals.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Using the payout pool and
distribution of performance ratings, the Corporate Compensation Department will
develop a payout schedule.&#160; Final
performance ratings and payouts will be approved by Dade Behring&#146;s CEO.&#160; The total dollars granted under this portion
cannot exceed the funded pool.&#160; Also, the
total MICP payout for all participants cannot exceed the total funded pool.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">6.0&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; PAYMENT

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Awards are typically paid out during the first quarter of the following
year.&#160; A participant must be an active employee
at the close of the fiscal year in order to receive a MICP award.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">If a participant dies, retires at age fifty-five (55) or older, or is
involuntarily terminated (except for cause), plan participation will be
pro-rated.&#160; If the termination date is on
or before the 15<sup>th</sup> of the month, that month will not be
counted.&#160; If the termination takes place
after the 15<sup>th</sup> of the month, the pro-ration will include that month.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">If a participant resigns prior to January 1 of the succeeding Plan year,
or is terminated for cause at any time prior to the MICP payout, no bonus award
will be granted.&#160; Note:&#160; &#147;Cause&#148; is defined per Dade Behring&#146;s &#147;Performance
and Conduct&#148; policy.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The attainment of financial objectives does not
guarantee a payment of an MICP award.&#160;
MICP awards may be adjusted at the discretion of management based upon,
