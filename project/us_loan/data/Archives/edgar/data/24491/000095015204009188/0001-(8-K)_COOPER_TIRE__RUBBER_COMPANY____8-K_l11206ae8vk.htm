ITEM 2.01. Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.

     On December 23, 2004, Cooper Tire & Rubber Company (the “Company”) completed the sale of its automotive business, Cooper-Standard Automotive. An entity (the “Buyer”) formed by The Cypress Group LLC and Goldman Sachs Capital Partners purchased all of the outstanding stock of Cooper-Standard Automotive and its subsidiaries pursuant to a stock purchase agreement, dated September 16, 2004, as amended, among the Company, Cooper Tyre & Rubber Company UK Limited and Buyer. The purchase price paid to the Company by Buyer for Cooper-Standard Automotive was approximately $1.172 billion in cash, subject to certain post-closing adjustments.

     A copy of the press release issued by the Company is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference.
