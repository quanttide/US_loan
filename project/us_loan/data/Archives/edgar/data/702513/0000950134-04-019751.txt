<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Section&nbsp;8 &#151;  Other Events



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;21, 2004 the Company received written notification of the termination of
the Merchant Services Agreement between Cardservice International (&#147;CSI&#148;) and
Redding Bank of Commerce (&#147;Redding&#148;) dated April&nbsp;1, 1993, as amended and Transaction
Settlement Agreement between CSI and Redding dated August&nbsp;1, 2000 as amended (the
&#147;Settlement Agreement&#148;).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The termination is effective April&nbsp;1, 2005. Earnings under the agreement were
approximately $153,000 for 2004. It is management&#146;s opinion that the termination of
this agreement will not have a material affect on the financial performance of the
Company.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">In prior reporting periods, The Company had two reportable segments: commercial
banking and credit card services. Commercial banking includes all services to the
Company&#146;s customers except credit card services. Credit card services are limited to
those revenues, net of related data processing costs, associated with the Bank&#146;s
agreement to provide credit and debit card processing services for merchants
solicited by an ISO or the Bank who accept credit and debit cards as payments for
goods and services. In the years 2004 and 2003, the Company accounted for its
operations as one operating segment.
