Section 8 - Other Events
Item 8.01 Other Events

PPL Energy Supply, LLC ("PPL Energy Supply"), an indirect, wholly owned subsidiary of PPL Corporation, has reactivated its commercial paper program to provide it with an additional financing source to fund its short-term liquidity needs, if and when necessary or appropriate. PPL Energy Supply currently has no plans to access the commercial paper market in the near-term. Standard & Poor's Ratings Services, Moody's Investors Service, Inc. and Fitch Ratings have confirmed their ratings of A-2, P-2 and F-2, respectively, for PPL Energy Supply's commercial paper.
