<P><b>Section 8 - Other Events<BR>
  Item 8.01 Other Events</b></p>

<P>PPL Energy Supply, LLC (&quot;PPL Energy Supply&quot;), an indirect, wholly
  owned subsidiary of PPL Corporation, has reactivated its commercial paper program
  to provide it with an additional financing source to fund its short-term liquidity needs,
  if and when necessary or appropriate. PPL Energy Supply currently has no plans
  to access the commercial paper market in the near-term. Standard &amp; Poor's
  Ratings Services, Moody's Investors Service, Inc. and Fitch Ratings have confirmed
  their ratings of A-2, P-2 and F-2, respectively, for PPL Energy Supply's commercial
  paper.</P>
