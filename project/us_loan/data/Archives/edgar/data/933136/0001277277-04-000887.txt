<p class="MsoTitle" align="center"><b><font size="2" face="Arial">Confidential Executive Separation Agreement</font></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><font size="2" face="Arial">This Confidential Executive Separation and Release Agreement
(&ldquo;Agreement&rdquo;) is entered between Craig J. Chapman (&ldquo;Executive&rdquo;) and Washington Mutual, Inc. (&ldquo;Employer&rdquo;) and is in consideration of the mutual undertakings set forth below.</font></p>

<p class="MsoBodyText"><font size="2" face="Arial">Employer and Executive have mutually agreed to end Executive&rsquo;s
employment.&nbsp; In order to assist Executive in his transition to new employment and acknowledge past contributions, Employer
will provide Executive the benefits described below.&nbsp; To clearly set forth the terms and conditions of Executive&rsquo;s
departure, the parties agree as follows:</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The purpose of this Agreement is to set forth the mutual
understanding of the parties.&nbsp; This Agreement shall not be construed as an admission by Employer that it acted wrongfully with
respect to Executive, nor shall it be construed as an admission by Executive of any misconduct or impropriety.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Executive&rsquo;s employment with Employer is ended
effective _______________ (&ldquo;Separation Date&rdquo;).&nbsp;</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Employer shall provide to Executive the Benefits described
in Paragraph 3 of the Executive Management Agreement between Executive and Employer dated effective as of ________________,
2004.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; In exchange for the benefits contained in this Agreement,
Executive releases and discharges Employer, all subsidiary, parent, and affiliated entities, and any Employer-sponsored benefit
plans in which Executive participates, and all of their respective owners, officers, directors, trustees, shareholders, employees,
agents, attorneys, and insurers from any and all claims, actions, causes of action, rights, benefits, compensation, or damages,
including costs and attorneys&rsquo; fees, of whatever nature, whether known or unknown, suspected or unsuspected, matured or
unmatured, now existing or arising in the future from any act, omission, event, occurrence, or non-occurrence prior to the date
Executive signs this Agreement arising out of or in any way related to Executive&rsquo;s employment with Employer.&nbsp; This
release includes but is not limited to any claims under any federal, state, or local laws prohibiting discrimination in employment,
including Title VII, the Age Discrimination in Employment Act, and the Americans with Disabilities Act; based upon any employment
agreement, severance plan, compensation plan, or change in control agreement; based upon any alleged legal restriction on
Employer&rsquo;s right to terminate its employees; and based upon any obligations under WARN or ERISA.&nbsp; This Agreement shall
not affect Executive&rsquo;s entitlement to receive any 401(k), stock option, or pension benefits that have vested as of the
Separation Date.</font></p>

<font size="2" face="Arial">5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Executive agrees that, as a result of his employment by
Employer, he has been exposed to confidential information that is not generally known to the public, all of which information is
owned by Employer. This includes information developed by Executive, alone or with others, or entrusted to Employer by customers or
others.&nbsp; Employer&rsquo;s confidential information includes, without limitation, information relating to its finances,
business and strategic plans, unannounced acquisition and/or investment prospects, trade secrets, know-how, procedures, purchasing,
accounting, marketing, sales, customers and employees (including customer lists).&nbsp; Executive agrees that, during his remaining
employment by Employer and for all time thereafter, as long as such information is not made public by Employer, Executive shall
hold such information in strict confidence and not disclose or use it except as specifically authorized by Employer and for
Employer&rsquo;s benefit.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Executive recognizes that Employer&rsquo;s workforce and
contracting relationships are a vital part of its business.&nbsp; Therefore, Executive agrees that for twenty-four (24) months
after the Separation Date, Executive will not directly or indirectly be involved in (a) soliciting any employee or contractor to
leave his or her employment or contract relationship with Employer, or (b) hiring any employee or contractor who, at the time of
hiring or during the three month period before the hiring, was an employee of Employer.&nbsp; This includes that Executive will not
(a) disclose to any third party the names, backgrounds or qualifications of any of Employer&rsquo;s employees or contractors, or
otherwise identify them as potential candidates for employment, or (b) personally or through any other person approach, recruit,
interview or otherwise solicit employees or contractors of Employer to work for any other employer or to reduce their relationship
with Employer.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">7.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive agrees to fully and reasonably
cooperate with Employer with respect to business issues, claims, administrative charges, and litigation related to Employer or its
business interests.&nbsp; This would include, but not be limited to, responding to questions, providing information, attending
meetings, depositions, administrative proceedings, and court hearings, and assisting Employer, its counsel and any expert
witnesses.&nbsp; Employer will reimburse any actual and reasonable expenses necessarily incurred by Executive in complying with
this obligation.&nbsp; Executive agrees not to communicate with any party, its legal counsel, or others adverse to Employer in any
pending or threatened claims, charges, or litigation except through legal counsel designated by Employer.&nbsp;</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">8.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;Should Executive receive notice of a subpoena or
other attempt to communicate with or obtain information from Executive in any way relating to Employer or its business interests,
Executive agrees to notify Employer&rsquo;s Senior Vice President and Associate General Counsel, Litigation, and to provide a copy
of any such subpoena or request within two (2) business days of receipt of such notice, and not to provide any such information
except through counsel designated by Employer unless compelled to do so by court order after Employer has had an opportunity to
raise and resolve any objections.</font></p>

Agreement.&nbsp;&nbsp;</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>

before receiving the separation pay and other benefits described in this Agreement, he will return to Employer all of
Employer&rsquo;s property, including all physical property (laptop computers, cellular phones, personal digital assistants,
computer disks, access cards, etc.) as well as any and all documents, data, plans, or other information, whether on paper or in
electronic form.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">10. &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive represents that he has not filed any claim against Employer or any of

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">11. &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive agrees and covenants that he will not directly or indirectly,

methods, services, or products.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">12. &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive and Employer shall cooperate to coordinate appropriate internal and
external communications concerning Executive&rsquo;s separation from employment and to designate individuals to whom reference
requests shall be directed. &nbsp;Employer shall have final approval on all communications.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">13. &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive understands and acknowledges the significance and consequences of
this Agreement and agrees that it is voluntary, and that he is not signing as a result of any coercion.&nbsp; Executive has been
encouraged to seek the advice of an attorney and, to the extent desired, has availed himself of that opportunity.&nbsp; Executive
acknowledges that he has been given at least twenty-one (21) days after receipt of this Agreement during which to consider
it.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">14. &nbsp;&nbsp;Executive understands and acknowledges that he has seven (7) days after signing
this Agreement in which to revoke it.&nbsp; This Agreement will become effective after that period has expired.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">15. &nbsp;&nbsp;This Agreement is binding on and shall inure to the benefit of the parties and
to those individuals and entities released in paragraph 4, as well as to all of their heirs, successors, and assigns.</font></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style='margin-left:0in;text-indent:0in'>
<font size="2" face="Arial">16. &nbsp;&nbsp;The interpretation of this Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State
of Washington.&nbsp; If any of the provisions of the Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions
will nevertheless continue to be valid and enforceable to the fullest extent permitted by law.&nbsp;</font></p>
