ITEM 8.01 Other Events

        On December 28, 2004, Myriad Genetics, Inc. announced that it has submitted an Investigational New Drug (IND) application to the FDA to begin a Phase I human clinical trial with MPC-2130 (previously referred to as MPI-176716), a broad-acting inducer of apoptosis in cancer cells. The press release is attached hereto as an exhibit to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is being filed pursuant to this Item 8.01 as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.
