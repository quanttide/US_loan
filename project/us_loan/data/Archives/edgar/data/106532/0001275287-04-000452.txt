<p  align=right><font size="2" face="Times New Roman"><b>Exhibit 99.1 </b></font></p>
<p  align="center"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman"><b>Weyco Group Announces Retirement of Senior Vice President</b></font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;MILWAUKEE, Dec. 21 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Weyco Group, Inc. (Nasdaq: WEYS), a leading marketer of men&#146;s branded footwear, announced today that James Gorman, a Senior Vice President of the Company and President of the Company&#146;s Florsheim Division, will retire on February 28, 2005.&#160; Jim has been with the Company for 36 years and has been the President of the Florsheim Division since the Company acquired the brand in May 2002.&#160; Previous to that Jim had been President of the Stacy Adams Division for 27 years. </font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;David McGinnis, Vice President of Sales for the Florsheim Division, will replace Jim as President of the Florsheim Division.&#160; Dave has been in the shoe industry for 30 years and has been with the Company since May 2003.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Weyco Group, Inc. designs and markets moderately priced and better-grade men&#146;s branded footwear for casual, fashion, and dress lifestyles.&#160; The principal brands of shoes sold by the Company are Florsheim, Nunn Bush, Nunn Bush NXXT, Brass Boot, Stacy Adams and SAO by Stacy Adams.&#160; The Company also operates a small number of retail stores in the U.S. and Europe.</font></p>
