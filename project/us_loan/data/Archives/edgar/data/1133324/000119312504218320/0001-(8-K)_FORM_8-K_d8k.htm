Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On December 17, 2004, InPhonic, Inc. (“InPhonic”) and A1 Wireless USA, Inc. (“A1”) entered into an Asset Purchase Agreement (the “Agreement”) by which CAIS Acquisition LLC, a wholly-owned subsidiary of InPhonic, has agreed to acquire certain assets of A1, for up to $20.9 million in cash and shares of InPhonic’s common stock. The closing of the transaction, which is subject to customary closing conditions, is anticipated to occur in January 2005.

 

The press release issued by InPhonic on December 20, 2004 is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is hereby incorporated herein by reference.
