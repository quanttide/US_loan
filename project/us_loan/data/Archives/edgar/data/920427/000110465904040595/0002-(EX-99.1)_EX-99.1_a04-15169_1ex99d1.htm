Unity Bancorp Declares Cash Dividend

 

Clinton, NJ — Unity Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ: UNTY), parent company of Unity Bank, announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend of $0.04 per common share. Such dividend is payable on January 28, 2005 to shareholders of record as of January 14, 2005.

 

 

Unity Bancorp, Inc. is a financial services organization headquartered in Clinton, New Jersey, with $500 million in assets and $425 million in deposits. Unity Bank provides financial services to retail, corporate & small business customers through its 13 retail service centers located in Hunterdon, Middlesex, Somerset and Union counties in New Jersey.  For additional information about Unity visit our website at www.unitybank.com or call 800 618-BANK.
