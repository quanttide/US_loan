<P align="left"><FONT size="2"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>
</FONT>

<P><FONT size="2">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Middlefield Banc Corp. entered into an agreement on December&nbsp;23, 2004
to purchase from Sky Financial Group, Inc. all of the shares of Middlefield
common stock held by Sky Financial Group and subsidiaries, consisting of a total
of 34,024 shares. The price payable by Middlefield will be $36.00 per share, or
a total of $1,224,864, financed by Middlefield Banc Corp.&#146;s line of credit with
an area bank. The transaction is expected to be completed by the end of
December. The share repurchase program authorized by Middlefield&#146;s board of
directors in May 2004 will continue according to its original terms, and the
34,024 shares being repurchased from Sky Financial Group will count toward the
maximum 4.99% shares authorized to be repurchased. No other shares have yet been
repurchased under the authority of that May 2004 board action.
