Item 2.01.  Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.

On December 21, 2004, Equity Inns, Inc. (the "Company") completed the
acquisition of four hotels from the McKibbon Hotel Group Inc., an unrelated
third party hotel developer and management company, for an aggregate of
approximately $28.5 million, including the assumption of an aggregate of
approximately $13 million in mortgage debt (collectively, the "Acquisition").
The Acquisition includes the purchase of a Courtyard by Marriott hotel in each
of Knoxville, Tennessee and Mobile, Alabama, a Residence Inn hotel in Macon,
Georgia and a Springhill Suites hotel in Asheville, North Carolina. The four
hotels include an aggregate of 322 rooms and will continue to be managed by
McKibbon Hotel Management, Inc. for a minimum of three years under a performance
based management contract.
