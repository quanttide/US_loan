Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.

 

On December 17, 2004, by unanimous written consent, the Board of Directors of Shopping.com Ltd. (the “Company”) elected Elizabeth Cross to serve on its Board of Directors, effective immediately. When approved by shareholders at the Company’s next shareholders meeting, Ms. Cross will serve as one of the two external directors required under Israeli law for companies whose shares are publicly traded. In addition to Ms. Cross’s election to the Board of Directors, she has been appointed to serve on the Company’s Audit Committee and Nominating Committee.
