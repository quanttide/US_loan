Item 3.02 Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.

On December 22, 2004, Douglas Lake Minerals Inc. (the "Company") completed a private placement of 945,400 shares (the "Shares") of its common stock, $0.001 par value per share ("Common Stock") to certain investors (the "Purchasers") at a price of $0.25 per share, pursuant to the terms of a Subscription Agreement between the Company and each of the Purchasers (the "Subscription Agreement"). The aggregate purchase price for the Shares sold in the private placement was $236,350 before deducting expenses. The Company did not pay any finders fee or commission in connection with the private placement. The Company intends to use the net proceeds from the private placement for working capital and general corporate purposes.
