<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif" SIZE=3>MasTec&#146;s Third
Quarter 2004 Net Income Increased 83% Over 2003 </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-Arial" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P ALIGN="Justify"><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif" SIZE=2><B>Coral Gables, FL </B>(December 30,
2004) &#151; MasTec, Inc. (NYSE: MTZ) today announced that net income for the third
quarter of 2004 was up 83% to $4.2 million compared with net income of $2.3 million for
the comparable quarter of 2003. Revenues for the third quarter of 2004 were up 5% to $250
million, compared with $237 million for the comparable quarter of 2003. Total net income
per share for the third quarter of 2004 was $0.09 per share, compared with net income of
$0.05 per share for the same quarter of 2003. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-Arial" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P ALIGN="Justify"><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif" SIZE=2>Additionally, the Company has executed
an amendment and waiver with its bank group and is in compliance with all the financial
covenants under its $125 million bank credit facility. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-Arial" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P ALIGN="Justify"><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif" SIZE=2>The Company has filed its 2004 third
quarter Form 10-Q with the Securities and Exchange Commission. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-Arial" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P ALIGN="Justify"><FONT FACE="Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif" SIZE=2>Austin J. Shanfelter, MasTec&#146;s
President and Chief Executive Officer commented, &#147;The changes that we have made
internally are beginning to show through the financial performance in the third quarter
and we expect continued performance enhancements in 2005.&#148; </FONT></P>
