<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">ITEM 8.01 &#150; Other Events</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June 16, 2004, SatCon
Technology Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) filed with the Securities and Exchange
Commission a registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-116533) (the &#147;Registration
Statement&#148;), relating to the registration under the Securities Act of 1933, as
amended, of shares of common stock of the Company, $0.01 par value (&#147;Common
Stock&#148;), preferred stock and warrants with a maximum aggregate offering price
of $25,000,000.&#160; The Registration
Statement was declared effective on June 23, 2004.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Subsequently, on December
21, 2004, the Company entered into a Common Stock Purchase Agreement with
several unrelated institutional investors in connection with the offering of
(i) an aggregate of 4,848,484 shares of Common Stock and (ii) warrants to
purchase up to an aggregate of 2,181,818 additional shares of Common Stock (the
&#147;Warrants&#148;) and (iii) the shares of Common Stock to be issued upon exercise of
the Warrants (collectively, the &#147;Offering&#148;), all of which Common Stock and
Warrants are being issued pursuant to the Registration Statement.&#160; The Company received proceeds from the sale
of these shares and Warrants equal to approximately $8,000,000, less the
Company&#146;s expenses relating to the sale, which are estimated to be $530,000, a
portion of which represents investment advisory fees which are discussed in
more detail below.&#160; On December 22, 2004,
the Company completed the Offering.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As noted above, as part of the Offering, the Company
issued Warrants to purchase up to an aggregate of 2,181,818 shares of Common
Stock.&#160;&#160; These Warrants are exercisable
for a five-year term and have an exercise price of $2.00 per share.&#160; The Warrants, however, may be exercised only
by &#147;cashless exercise.&#148;&#160; &#147;Cashless
exercise&#148; means that in lieu of paying the aggregate exercise price for the
shares being purchased upon exercise of the Warrant in cash, the holder will
forfeit a number of shares underlying the Warrant with a market value equal to
such aggregate exercise price.&#160;
Accordingly, the Company will not receive any additional proceeds upon
exercise of the Warrants.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Burnham Hill Partners, a
division of Pali Capital, Inc., has been retained to act as the Company&#146;s
financial advisor in connection with potential capital raising activities,
including interacting with potential investors regarding pricing and
structuring of the shares of Common Stock and Warrants issued in the
Offering.&#160; The Company agreed to pay to
the financial advisor a fee in the amount of $480,000 in connection with the
sale of the shares of Common Stock and Warrants issued in the Offering.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Company&#146;s Press Release, dated December 22, 2004,
is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>
