<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;23, 2004, Syratech Corporation (the
&#147;Company&#148;) and the Company&#146;s Lenders under that certain Amended and Restated
Loan and Security Agreement dated as of March&nbsp;26, 2004 (as amended, the &#147;Loan
Agreement&#148;) entered into the First Amendment to Forbearance Agreement (the &#147;Amendment&#148;).&#160; Pursuant to the Amendment, the Forbearance
and Second Amendment dated as of November&nbsp;15, 2004 (the &#147;Forbearance&#148;) was
amended to provide that the Forbearance Period (as defined in the Forbearance)
was extended from December&nbsp;31, 2004 to January&nbsp;31, 2005.&#160; The Amendment also decreased the Company&#146;s
minimum Revolving Credit Availability requirement (as defined in the Loan
Agreement) under the Loan Agreement during the Forbearance Period.&#160; The Company paid a Forbearance Amendment Fee
of $50,000 in connection with the Amendment.&#160;
The Forbearance was previously filed as an exhibit to the Company&#146;s Form
10-Q filing on November&nbsp;16, 2004.&#160; A
copy of the Amendment is attached as Exhibit 99.1.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;28, 2004, the Company and the
Consenting Lock-Up Noteholders (as defined in the Lock-up Agreement, as defined
below) entered into the First Amendment to Lock-Up Agreement (the &#147;Lock-Up
Amendment&#148;), which amended the Lock-Up Agreement dated as of November&nbsp;15,
2004 (the &#147;Lock-Up Agreement&#148;) between the Company and certain holders of the
Company&#146;s 11% Senior Notes due 2007.&#160;
Pursuant to the Lock-Up Amendment, the deadline for the Company to
commence the prenegotiated chapter 11 filing under the Lock-Up Agreement was
extended from December&nbsp;31, 2004 to January&nbsp;31, 2005.&#160;&#160;&#160; The form of Lock-Up Agreement was
previously filed as an exhibit to the Company&#146;s Form 10-Q filing on November&nbsp;16,
2004.&#160; The form of Lock-Up Amendment is
attached as Exhibit 99.2.</font></p>
