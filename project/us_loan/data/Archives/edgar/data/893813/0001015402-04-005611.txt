Item  5.03.  Amendments to Articles of Incorporation or Bylaws; Change in Fiscal
Year

     On  December  17,  2004,  by  resolution  of  its board of directors and in
accordance  with its bylaws, Penn Octane Corporation changed its fiscal year end
from  July  31  to  December  31,  beginning  December  31,  2004.  Penn  Octane
Corporation  will  file  a  report  on Form 10-Q covering the transition period.
