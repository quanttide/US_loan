<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'><b>ITEM 4.01 Changes in
Registrant's Certifying Accountant</b>.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>On December 20, 2004, Forward
Industries, Inc. (the &quot;Company&quot;) dismissed Ernst &amp; Young LLP (&quot;E&amp;Y&quot;), the Company's
independent registered public accounting firm. On December 22, 2004,&nbsp; the
Company engaged Kaufman, Rossin &amp; Co., P.A. (&quot;Kaufman, Rossin&quot;) as its new
independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending
September 30, 2005. The Audit Committee of the Company approved of the
dismissal of E&amp;Y and recommended to the Company's Board of Directors the appointment
of Kaufman, Rossin after the solicitation of proposals from a number of
registered public accounting firms.&nbsp; The Company's Board of Directors
unanimously approved this recommendation.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>E&amp;Y's reports on the
Company's consolidated financial statements as of and for the fiscal years
ended September 30, 2004 and 2003 did not contain an adverse opinion or a
disclaimer of opinion, and were not qualified or modified as to uncertainty,
audit scope, or accounting principles.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>During the fiscal years ended
September 30, 2004 and 2003 and through the date of dismissal, there were no
disagreements with E&amp;Y on any matter of accounting principles or practices,
financial statement disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure that, if not
resolved to the satisfaction of E&amp;Y, would have caused E&amp;Y to make
reference thereto in their reports on the Company's consolidated financial
statements for such years.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>During the fiscal years ended
September 30, 2004 and 2003 and through the date of dismissal, there were no
reportable events as provided in Item 304(a)(1)(iv) (B) of Regulation S-B.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>On December 20, 2004, the
Company provided E&amp;Y with a copy of the disclosure it is making in response
to Item 4.01 of this Current Report on Form 8-K and has requested that E&amp;Y furnish
it with a letter addressed to the Securities and Exchange Commission stating
whether or not it agrees with the above statements. A copy of Ernst &amp; Young
LLP's letter to the Securities and Exchange Commission, dated December 21,
2004, is filed as Exhibit 16.1 to this Form 8-K.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-top:5.0pt;margin-right:0in;margin-bottom:5.0pt;
margin-left:0in'>During the fiscal years ended
September 30, 2004 and 2003 and through the date of dismissal of E&amp;Y, the Company did not consult with Kaufman,
Rossin &amp; Co., P.A., regarding any matters or events referred to in
Item 304(a)(2)(i) or (ii)&nbsp; of Regulation S-B.</p>
