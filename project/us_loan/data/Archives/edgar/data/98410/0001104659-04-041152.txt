<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><a name="Item1_01EntryIntoAMaterialDefiniti"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ITEM 1.01 ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL
DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT</font></a></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 17, 2004, Tipperary Oil &amp; Gas Australia Pty Ltd (&#147;TOGA&#148;),
the Australian subsidiary of Tipperary Corporation, entered into an agreement
to supply natural gas to Energex Retail Pty Ltd (&#147;Energex&#148;), an unrelated party.&#160; The contract commences on June 1, 2005 for a
term of 23 months and is an extension of the existing long-term gas sales
contract with Energex that expires June 1, 2005. &#160;The contract provides that Energex will
purchase a minimum of 7,300 and a maximum of 14,500 Mcf of gas per day.&#160; The new contract is similar in terms to the
existing contract.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 23, 2004, TOGA entered into an agreement to supply natural
gas to Orica Australia Pty Ltd &#160;(&#147;Orica&#148;),
an unrelated party.&#160; The contract
commences&#160; on June&nbsp;26, 2006 for a
term of 10 years.&#160; The contract provides
that Orica will purchase a minimum of 3,000 and a maximum of 4,800 Mcf of gas
per day.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">TOGA will supply gas under these contracts from the Comet Ridge coalseam
gas project located in Queensland, Australia. Tipperary Corporation and
subsidiaries have a 71.7% pre-royalty interest in the Comet Ridge production.
The other working interest owners may elect to participate by up to 28.3% of
gas deliveries for the new contract.</font></p>
