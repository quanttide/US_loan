<P style="margin:0pt; padding-right:-9pt; line-height:16pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:14pt" align=center><B>THE COMMUNITY NATIONAL CORPORATION ANNOUNCES ITS ANNUAL DIVIDEND</B></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; line-height:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt">It was announced today, December 21, 2004, by Paul J. Scheuermann, President of The Community National Corporation, that the Board of Directors has voted to pay a dividend of $0.50 per share. &nbsp;The dividend will be payable on January 21, 2005, to shareholders of record on December 31, 2004. &nbsp;Mr. Scheuermann stated that this year&#146;s dividend represents a 20% increase over last year. &nbsp;The combined dividend over the last two years equates to a 31% increase in the dividend payout to the shareholder. &nbsp;The Board of Directors based their decision on income tax benefits to the shareholder, due to changes in the tax code, and to reflect the appreciation in the stock value.</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; line-height:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt">The Board of Directors is giving the shareholders a choice of how they are to receive the distribution of the dividend. &nbsp;This dividend will be paid in either cash or whole shares of stock and the balance in cash.</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; line-height:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt">The Community National Corporation stock currently trades on the NASDAQ Bulletin Board. The corporation&#146;s symbol is CMNC.</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:6pt; line-height:14pt; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:12pt">The Community National Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary of the Community National Corporation, was founded in 1983. &nbsp;It currently has over $139 million in assets. &nbsp;In addition to the Franklin office, the Bank has office locations in Carlisle, Centerville, Springboro and Middletown. &nbsp;</P>
