<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;16, 2004, Warren Resources, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;, &#147;we&#148; or &#147;us&#148;)
entered into an Underwriting Agreement (the &#147;Underwriting Agreement&#148;) among the
Company and KeyBanc Capital Markets, as representative (the &#147;Representative&#148;)
of the several underwriters named in the Underwriting Agreement (the
&#147;Underwriters&#148;), in connection with the Company&#146;s initial public offering.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Underwriting Agreement provides for, among other things, the sale by the
Company of an aggregate of 9,500,000 shares to the Underwriters for sale to the
public. In addition, the Underwriters have an option to buy up to an additional
1,425,000 shares from the Company to cover over-allotments, if any.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company, its executive officers, directors and certain of its stockholders,
have agreed with the Underwriters not to dispose of or hedge any shares of the
Company&#146;s common stock during the period from the date of the Underwriting
Agreement and continuing through the date 180&nbsp;days after the date of the final
prospectus related to the initial public offering, except for certain
dispositions or with the prior written consent of the Representatives.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company has agreed to indemnify the several Underwriters against certain
liabilities, including liabilities under the Securities Act of 1933.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Certain of the Underwriters and their respective affiliates have, from time to
time, performed, and may in the future perform, various financial advisory,
hedging and investment banking services for the Company, for which they
received or will receive customary fees and expenses.



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">A copy of the Underwriting Agreement is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;1.1 hereto and is
incorporated by reference herein. The description of the Underwriting Agreement
contained herein is qualified in its entirety by the full text of such exhibit.
