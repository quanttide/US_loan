ITEM 8.01 OTHER EVENTS

Notice from The Nasdaq Stock Market, Inc. that Technology Solutions Company has regained compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 4450(a)(5)

As previously reported, on October 15, 2004 Technology Solutions Company (“TSC”) received a letter from The Nasdaq Stock Market, Inc. (“Nasdaq”) notifying TSC that for the 30 consecutive trading days preceding the date of the letter, the bid price of TSC’s common stock had closed below the $1.00 per share minimum required for continued inclusion on the Nasdaq National Market pursuant to Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 4450(a)(5). On December 21, 2004, TSC received a letter from Nasdaq notifying TSC that since the closing bid price of TSC’s common stock has been at $1.00 per share or greater for at least 10 consecutive days since the date of the October letter, TSC has regained compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 4450(a)(5).
