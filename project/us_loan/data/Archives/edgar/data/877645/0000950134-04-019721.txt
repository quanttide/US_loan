<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>ITEM 8.01 OTHER EVENTS</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><I>Notice from The Nasdaq Stock Market, Inc. that Technology Solutions Company has regained compliance
with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4450(a)(5)</I></B>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">As previously reported, on October&nbsp;15, 2004 Technology Solutions Company (&#147;TSC&#148;) received a letter
from The Nasdaq Stock Market, Inc. (&#147;Nasdaq&#148;) notifying TSC that for the 30 consecutive trading
days preceding the date of the letter, the bid price of TSC&#146;s common stock had closed below the
$1.00 per share minimum required for continued inclusion on the Nasdaq National Market pursuant to
Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4450(a)(5). On December&nbsp;21, 2004, TSC received a letter from Nasdaq
notifying TSC that since the closing bid price of TSC&#146;s common stock has been at $1.00 per share or
greater for at least 10 consecutive days since the date of the October letter, TSC has regained
compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4450(a)(5).
