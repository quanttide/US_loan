<H1 ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=4>Bulldog
Technologies Appoints Vice President of Sales to Drive Revenues <BR>Richard Booth to Drive
Next Phase of Growth </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>RICHMOND, British Columbia,
Canada, December 21, 2004 &#150; Bulldog Technologies Inc. (OTC BB: BLLD),</B> a leading
provider of wireless cargo security solutions, today announced that Richard Booth has been
appointed Vice President Sales. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>As a sales veteran of 19 years,
Richard Booth joins Bulldog Technologies as Vice President of Sales after having spent the
last 6 years building and growing his enterprise sales consultancy business with a
clientele of both technology start-ups and high profile multinational companies, including
Cisco, 3COM, HP, and Computer Associates. During this time Richard was instrumental in
establishing sales processes and assisting in stimulating sales growth. Previously, he
worked in continuously senior sales and sales management roles with Smallword Systems,
Accugraph Corporation as well as Nortel Networks. Richard has a BA from North Carolina
State University. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>With Bulldog&#146;s plan for
accelerated growth in both direct and distribution sales, Richard will be focused on
building the Company&#146;s sales infrastructure, and advancing Bulldog&#146;s
industry-leading wireless cargo security solution sales strategies. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;Richard&#146;s outstanding
track record in sales as well as in building and managing sales teams over the last 19
years will help to bring Bulldog to the next level. Richard will call on his extensive
background in growing technology companies to drive growth to new levels as we expand our
sales activities,&#148; said James McMillan, Chief Operating Officer, Bulldog
Technologies. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Head Major Left Bold-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<A NAME=A004></A>
<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>About Bulldog
Technologies </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Bulldog Technologies, Inc., a leading
provider of wireless cargo security solutions, researches, develops, and manufactures
real-time, comprehensive monitoring and intrusion detection BOSS<SUP>TM</SUP> (Bulldog
Online Security Solution) devices for use in the cargo transportation and storage
industry. Bulldog&#146;s systems allow dispatchers, security personnel, emergency response
teams and cargo transport drivers to monitor valuable cargo during the transport, storage
and delivery process. In contrast to radio-frequency tags, Bulldog&#146;s proprietary
technology employs a monitoring system that detects suspicious activity on a real-time
basis. Thus, their technology can prevent the illegal entry of port storage cargo
containers and theft of mobile or in-transit shipping containers. </FONT></P>
