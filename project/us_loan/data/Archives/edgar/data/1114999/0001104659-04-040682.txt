<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item
1.01&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Entry into a Material
Definitive Agreement</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 21, 2004, RadView Software Ltd. entered
into a license and distribution agreement with Allen Systems Group, Inc., or
ASG, under which RadView has granted to ASG the non-exclusive right to
distribute RadView&#146;s TestView product suite in object-code form for a term of
two years.&#160; ASG is required pay to
RadView royalties on its sales of the products and to distribute the products
under its own trade names.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; In addition, RadView
granted to ASG an option to purchase from RadView expanded rights to the
TestView suite of products through June 21, 2005.&#160; The option purchase price is $2,500,000,
payable in two installments of $1,250,000 each, the first on the date of option
exercise and the second on the first anniversary of the option exercise.&#160; In addition, ASG would continue to pay to
RadView royalties on its sales of the products for two years after the option
exercise at a reduced royalty rate.&#160;
Thereafter, ASG would have no further payment obligations to RadView for
the sale of the products by ASG.&#160; The
expanded rights include the right of ASG to access and use source code for the
TestView suite of products and the right to create derivative products.&#160; RadView is obligated to provide to ASG new
product releases for six months following the option exercise and product
corrections for two years following the option exercise.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 21, 2004, the Company issued a press
release announcing that it had entered into the agreement with ASG.&#160; A copy of this press release is filed as an
exhibit to this report.</font></p>

<h2 style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Exhibit 99.1</font></b></h2>

<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:#669966;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">ASG and RadView Software Form OEM Partnership to
Resell TestView Suite</font></b></p>

<p style="color:black;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:#669966;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><i><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Comprehensive
Web Application Testing Solutions Offered to Development Teams</font></i></b></p>

<p style="color:black;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">NAPLES, Fla. and
Burlington, MA December 21, 2004</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">&#151;</font></b>
</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ASG</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">, an enterprise
software and professional services provider to Global 5000 companies, and RadView&#153;
Software Ltd. (OTCBB: RDVW), a leading provider of solutions for verifying the
performance, scalability and integrity of business-critical Web applications,
today announced a global OEM partnership that enables ASG to resell RadView&#146;s
TestView Suite.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Targeted at organizations that want to
validate and test Web-based applications, ASG will market and distribute the
TestView Suite, which <em><i><font face="Times New Roman" style="font-style:normal;">allows
application development teams to identify issues early in the development
process by simulating real-world Internet conditions&#151;so teams can effectively
test, detect, diagnose, and report on application performance.</font></i></em></font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Test View Suite is comprised of:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .2in;text-indent:-.2in;"><b><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;font-weight:bold;">&#149;</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">TestView Manager for test
management through a central console</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .2in;text-indent:-.2in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WebFT, a robust testing tool
that efficiently verifies the functional accuracy of Web applications</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .2in;text-indent:-.2in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WebLOAD/WebRM for load
testing that reports bottlenecks, constraints, and weak points within a Web
application</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><em><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:normal;">ASG President and CEO Arthur L.
Allen said, &#147;RadView Software offers strong solutions to meet the challenges
faced by businesses with stringent software quality requirements.&#160; This partnership will help businesses who are
</font></i></em><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">moving from mainframe applications to a Web-based environment, <em><i><font face="Times New Roman" style="font-style:normal;">and it will also
strengthen ASG&#146;s overall application management portfolio.</font></i></em>&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The agreement expands RadView Software&#146;s
opportunities in global markets where ASG can offer a robust Web application
testing solution to complement its existing product lines.&#160; Additionally, ASG will penetrate emerging
markets such as China and Brazil where mutually developed localized versions of
the RadView products will be introduced.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;This partnership represents another solid
example of RadView&#146;s commitment to its strategy of adding strong global
partners to expand the Company&#146;s market presence worldwide,&#148; said Ilan
Kinreich, President and CEO of RadView Software.&#160; &#147;ASG offers RadView a larger global reach
plus the ability to expand into emerging markets.&#160; With this agreement we are looking forward to
a mutually beneficial business relationship with one of the most successful
software companies in the world.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">About ASG</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Founded in 1986, ASG is a privately held
global enterprise software and professional services firm that provides a full
range of software solutions in the Metadata Management, Security Management,
Applications Management, Operations Management, Information &amp; Data
Management, Performance Management and Infrastructure Management arenas &#151;
including business re-engineering, systems integration and systems development.&#160; ASG is headquartered in Naples, Florida, USA,
with more than 45 offices serving the Americas, Europe, Middle East, Africa and
Asia/Pacific.&#160; For more information,
visit ASG at </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">www.asg.com</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">.</font></p>
