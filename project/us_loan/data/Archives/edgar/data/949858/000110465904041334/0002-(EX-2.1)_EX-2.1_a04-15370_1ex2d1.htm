SALE AND EXCHANGE OF SHARES

 

Section 1.1.                                   Acquisition Price, Sale and Exchange of Shares.  Upon the terms and subject to the conditions of this Agreement, at the Closing, the Sellers shall sell, convey, assign, transfer and deliver to the Buyer free and clear of all Liens, and the Buyer shall purchase, acquire and accept from the Sellers, the Shares, in exchange for the consideration specified in Section 1.2.
