ITEM 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

On Tuesday, December 21, 2004, the Board of Directors approved a revised
Director Elective Income Deferral Agreement ("Agreement") for the Board of
Directors of County Bank and Capital Corp of the West, the bank holding company
for County Bank. The Agreement is a deferred compensation arrangement that
allows the members of the Board of Directors to defer receipt of certain
director fees in exchange for future payments of a fixed amount for a fixed
period of time. A copy of the agreement is contained in Exhibit 99.1 of this
filing. The amounts deferred for each director is contained in Exhibit 99.2.
