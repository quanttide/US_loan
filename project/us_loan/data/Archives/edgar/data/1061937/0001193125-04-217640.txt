 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>ITEM 8.01. Other Events. </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Host Marriott, L.P. deems the following information to be of importance to its securityholders: </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">On December 15, 2004, we executed a purchase and sale agreement with an
affiliate of Sarofim Realty Advisors, on behalf of an institutional investor, and Marriott International, Inc., whereby Sarofim will acquire 85% of our interest in CBM Joint Venture LLC (the &#147;Joint Venture&#148;) for $91,872,510. We and
Marriott International, Inc. each currently own a 50% interest in the Joint Venture, which owns 120 Courtyard by Marriott properties totaling approximately 17,500 rooms. Separately, Sarofim will also invest approximately $240 million of new equity
into the Joint Venture (contingent upon completion of the sale), which would give it a majority interest in the Joint Venture. Based on the valuation given to the sale of our 85% stake, Host Marriott&#146;s total equity interest in the Joint Venture
is currently worth approximately $108 million. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As part of the
transaction, the Joint Venture will be converted into a limited partnership and under the terms of the partnership agreement, Host Marriott will have the right to cause the partnership to redeem its remaining interest in the partnership under
certain conditions between December 8, 2007 and December 8, 2009. Starting December 9, 2009, the partnership will have the right to redeem Host Marriott&#146;s remaining interest. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The sale is expected to close in the first quarter of 2005. The transaction is subject to several closing conditions,
