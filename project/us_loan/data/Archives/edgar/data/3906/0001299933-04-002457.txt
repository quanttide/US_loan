    <TD valign="top"><DIV style="margin-left:0px; text-indent:-0px"><FONT style="font-size: 11pt"><B>Washington, DC &#151; December&nbsp;27, 2004 </B>&#150; Allied Capital Corporation (NYSE:ALD)<BR>
disclosed that on December&nbsp;22, 2004 it received letters from the U.S. Attorney for<BR>
the District of Columbia requesting the preservation and production of information<BR>
regarding Allied Capital and Business Loan Express, LLC in connection with a<BR>
criminal investigation. Based on the information requested by the U.S Attorney,<BR>
the nature of the inquiry appears to pertain to matters similar to those<BR>
allegations made by short sellers over the past two and one-half years. Allied<BR>
Capital has voluntarily agreed to cooperate with the U.S. Attorney&#146;s request.<BR>
<B>About Allied Capital</B><BR>
Allied Capital Corporation, a leading business development company with total<BR>
assets of more than $3&nbsp;billion, has paid regular, quarterly cash dividends to<BR>
shareholders since 1963. Since its IPO in 1960, the Company has been providing<BR>
long-term debt and equity financing primarily to private, middle market companies.<BR>
Allied Capital&#146;s mission is to generate solid total returns from a diversified<BR>
private equity and commercial real estate portfolio that invests in the American<BR>
entrepreneurial economy. In serving its shareholders, Allied Capital helps build<BR>
American companies and support American jobs. The Company&#146;s private finance<BR>
portfolio includes investments in over 100 companies with aggregate revenues of in<BR>
excess of $11&nbsp;billion, supporting more than 100,000 jobs.<BR>
Headquartered in Washington, DC, Allied Capital offers shareholders the opportunity<BR>
to participate in the private equity industry through an investment in the<BR>
Company&#146;s New York Stock Exchange-listed stock, which is traded under the symbol<BR>
ALD. For more information, please visit www.alliedcapital.com, call Allied Capital<BR>
investor relations toll-free at (888)&nbsp;818-5298, or e-mail us at<BR>
ir@alliedcapital.com. For all media inquiries, please call Stan Collender (202)<BR>
434-0601.<BR>
