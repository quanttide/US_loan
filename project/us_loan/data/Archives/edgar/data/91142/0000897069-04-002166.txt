<H1 ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>A. O.
Smith elects Jones to board of directors  </FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>Milwaukee,
Wis.&#151;</B> A. O. Smith Corporation (AOS-NYSE) today announced that President and Chief
Operating Officer Paul W. Jones has been elected to its Board of Directors. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Jones
was named president of the Milwaukee-based manufacturer of electric motors and water
heaters in January 2004 with responsibility for its Electrical Products and Water Systems
operations worldwide as well as its Corporate Technology Center. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Prior
to joining A. O. Smith, he was chairman and chief executive officer of U. S. Can Company,
Inc., and president and chief executive officer of Greenfield Industries, Inc. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In
addition to the A. O. Smith board, Jones serves as a director of Federal Signal
Corporation (FSS-NYSE), a diversified manufacturer with headquarters in Oak Brook, Ill. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A.          O.
Smith Corporation, with headquarters in Milwaukee, Wis., is one of North           America&#146;s
largest manufacturers of electric motors, with a comprehensive           line of hermetic
motors, fractional horsepower alternating current (AC) and           direct current (DC)
motors, and integral horsepower motors, as well as one of           North America&#146;s
largest manufacturers of residential and commercial water           heating equipment.
The company employs approximately 17,000 people worldwide.  </FONT></P>
