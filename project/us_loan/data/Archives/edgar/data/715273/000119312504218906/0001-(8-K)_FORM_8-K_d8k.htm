ITEM 2.01 COMPLETION OF ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS

 

On December 23, 2004, Wyndham International, Inc. issued a press release announcing that it has formed a joint venture with Lehman Brothers Real Estate Partners (LBREP) to expand and develop Summerfield Suites by Wyndham, Wyndham’s upscale, extended-stay brand. The transaction, which closed on Wednesday, December 22, included the sale of six Summerfield Suites by Wyndham properties and the sale of a partnership interest in its Summerfield brand to LBREP for approximately $105 million. The press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
