<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:4.5pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:9pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:36pt; width:540pt; line-height:11pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt">On December 20, 2004, the Company advised Amisano Hanson, Chartered Accountants, that it had been dismissed and would not be appointed as the Company&#146;s auditor for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2004. &nbsp;&nbsp;</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:36pt; width:540pt; line-height:11pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt">Amisano Hanson Chartered Accountants was the Company&#146;s independent auditor for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2003. &nbsp;The report of Amisano Hanson Chartered Accountants, for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2003, did not contain any adverse opinion or disclaimer of opinion and was not qualified or modified as to any uncertainty, audit scope or accounting principle.</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:36pt; width:540pt; line-height:11pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt">The Company&#146;s independent auditor for the fiscal years ended December 31, 2002 and 2001, was Tanner + Co. &nbsp;Tanner + Co was dismissed on March 10, 2004. &nbsp;The Company previously filed a report on Form 8K dated March 10, 2004, to report the dismissal of Tanner + Co. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt"><BR></P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:36pt; width:540pt; line-height:11pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt">During the Company's fiscal year ended December 31, 2003, and from January 1, 2004 to the date of this Report, there were no disagreements with Amisano Hanson Chartered Accountants on any matter of accounting principles or practices, financial disclosure, or auditing scope or procedure. There were no reportable events, as described in Item 304(a)(1)(v) of Regulation S-K, during the Company's fiscal year ended December 31, 2003, and from January 1, 2004 to the date of this Report.</P>
<P style="margin-top:0pt; margin-right:72pt; margin-bottom:0pt; width:540pt; font-family:Courier New; font-size:10pt"><BR></P>
