Checkers Drive-In Restaurants, Inc.

Reports Settlement of Mittman Claims

 

TAMPA, FL – December 21, 2004 – Checkers Drive-In Restaurants, Inc. (NASDAQ: CHKR), the nation’s largest double drive-thru chain, today announced it has settled claims in Jonathan Mittman, et al. v. Rally’s Hamburgers, Inc., et al. (CA# 3:94CV-39-S) and plaintiff’s appeal of the summary judgment (in the Company’s favor) has been dismissed.

 

Keith Sirois, President and C.E.O. of Checkers Drive-In Restaurants, Inc. commented: “We are very pleased that this matter is finally behind us. In concluding this matter now, the Company was not required to pay any money, and in fact will now recoup this year approximately $2 million in legal fees expended over the past decade.”
