<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On April&nbsp;21, 2004, the shareholders of LSB Bancshares, Inc. (the
&#147;Company&#148;) approved the Company&#146;s Comprehensive Equity Compensation Plan for
Directors and Employees (the &#147;Plan&#148;), a copy of which is included as Appendix
VI of the Company&#146;s Proxy Statement on Schedule&nbsp;14A filed with the Securities
and Exchange Commission (the &#147;SEC&#148;) on March&nbsp;16, 2004 (SEC File No.&nbsp;000-11448).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;21, 2004, the Company&#146;s Board of Directors, upon
recommendation by the Stock Option and Compensation Committee of the Company&#146;s
Board of Directors, approved a form of stock option award agreement for
directors (the &#147;Form&nbsp;Director Option Grant Agreement&#148;) and a form of incentive
stock option award agreement for employees (the &#147;Form&nbsp;Employee Option Grant
Agreement&#148;). The Company intends to use these agreements when it grants stock
options to directors and stock options to employees, respectively, under the
Plan. The Form&nbsp;Director Option Grant Agreement and Form&nbsp;Employee Option Grant
Agreement are filed as Exhibits 10.1 and 10.2, respectively, to this Current
Report on Form 8-K and are incorporated herein by reference.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events.</B>

shareholders of record as of January&nbsp;1, 2005. A copy of the press release
issued by the Company on December&nbsp;23, 2004 announcing the declaration of this
dividend is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and
incorporated herein by reference.
