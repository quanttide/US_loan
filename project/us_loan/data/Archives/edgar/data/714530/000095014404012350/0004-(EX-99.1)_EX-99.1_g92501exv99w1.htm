     LSB BANCSHARES, INC., ANNOUNCES FOURTH QUARTER DIVIDEND

     LEXINGTON, N.C., Thursday, December 23, 2004, 8 AM ET - At its meeting on Tuesday, December 21, 2004, the Board of Directors of LSB Bancshares, Inc., (Nasdaq: LXBK) parent company of Lexington State Bank, declared a quarterly cash dividend of $.16 per share of common stock, payable on January 15, 2005, to shareholders of record on January 1, 2005.

     LSB The Bank is one of the largest community banks in North Carolina, with 26 offices in Davidson, Forsyth, Guilford, Randolph and Stokes counties and a mortgage origination office in Wake County. Services are also available through 31 ATMs and cash dispensers, “LSB By Net” online banking and 24-hour “LSB By Phone” banking.

     LSB The Bank owns two subsidiaries: LSB Investment Services, Inc., which offers non-deposit, non-insured investment alternatives such as mutual funds and annuities; and Peoples Finance Co. of Lexington, Inc., which offers small loans and dealer financing.

     Common stock of the bank’s parent company, LSB Bancshares, Inc., is traded on the Nasdaq National Market and is quoted under the symbol LXBK. Additional information about LSB is available on its web site, www.lsbnc.com.
