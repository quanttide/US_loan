Item 1.01.      Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

     On April 21, 2004, the shareholders of LSB Bancshares, Inc. (the “Company”) approved the Company’s Comprehensive Equity Compensation Plan for Directors and Employees (the “Plan”), a copy of which is included as Appendix VI to the Company’s Proxy Statement on Schedule 14A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the “SEC”) on March 16, 2004 (SEC File No. 000-11448).

     On December 29, 2004, the Stock Option and Compensation Committee of the Company’s Board of Directors approved a form of director fee deferral agreement (the “Form Director Fee Deferral Agreement”). The Company intends to use this agreement (completed as applicable) as a mechanism for awarding deferred stock units pursuant to the Plan to directors who defer receipt of all or a portion of their quarterly director retainers and/or regular director meeting fees. The Form Director Fee Deferral Agreement is filed as Exhibit 10.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.
