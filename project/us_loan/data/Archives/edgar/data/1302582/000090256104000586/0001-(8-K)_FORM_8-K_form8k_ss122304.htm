Item 8.01        Other Events.

 On September 16, 2004, pursuant to a registration statement (No. 333-117983) Perpetual Trustees Australia Limited, in its capacity as issuer trustee (the "Issuer Trustee") of the SMHL Global Fund No. 7 (the "Trust"), publicly issued U.S.$750,000,000 of Class A1 Mortgage Backed Floating Rate Notes (the "Notes").
