ITEM 5.  Other Events and Regulation FD Disclosure.

     On October 14, 2004, Biggest Little Investments L.P., a Delaware limited
partnership (the "Partnership") commenced a program (the "Repurchase
Program") to redeem units of limited partnership interests in the Partnership
(the "Units") at a price of $69 per Unit in cash.  The Repurchase Program
terminated on December 20, 2004 and the Partnership redeemed 2,238 Units,
each of which was retired.

     The Partnership now has 180,937 Units outstanding.
