<P ALIGN="CENTER"><B>Rainier Pacific Financial Group, Inc. Completes Repurchase Program</P>
</B><P ALIGN="CENTER"></P>
<P ALIGN="CENTER">&nbsp;</P>
<P style="line-height: 200%">Tacoma, Washington - December 20, 2004 - Rainier Pacific Financial Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: RPFG) announced the completion of the repurchase of 5% of its outstanding Company stock, or 371,915 shares.  The repurchase program was announced on October 20, 2004.</P>

<P style="line-height: 200%">The shares were purchased between $17.50 and $18.13 per share, with an average per share price of $17.79.  </P>

<P style="line-height: 200%">Rainier Pacific Financial Group, Inc. is the bank holding company for Rainier Pacific Savings Bank, a Tacoma, Washington based state-chartered savings bank, operating 12 branch offices in the Tacoma-Pierce County and Federal Way areas. </P>

<P style="line-height: 200%">For additional information, visit Rainier Pacific's website at <U>www.rainierpac.com</U>.</P>
