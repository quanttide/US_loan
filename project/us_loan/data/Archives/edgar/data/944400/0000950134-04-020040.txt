<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events.</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Company Regains Compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4)


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;29, 2004, Remote Dynamics, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) received written notice from the
Nasdaq Listing Qualifications of the Nasdaq Stock Market that the Company had regained compliance
with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4) as the closing bid price for the Company&#146;s common stock has
been at $1.00 per share or greater for at least 10 consecutive business days. Accordingly, the
matter regarding the Company&#146;s non-compliance with Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4) was closed and the
Company&#146;s listing on the Nasdaq Small Cap Market was in good standing.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A copy of the press release, dated December&nbsp;30, 2004, announcing the Company&#146;s receipt of the
letter dated December&nbsp;29, 2004 from Nasdaq indicating that the Company had regained compliance with
Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4), is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1. A copy of the letter dated
December&nbsp;29, 2004 from Nasdaq indicating that the Company had regained compliance with Marketplace
Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4), is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;99.2.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Remote Dynamics Regains Compliance with Nasdaq Market Place Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4); Listing Now in Good Standing</B>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>RICHARDSON, Texas, Dec. 30, 2004 </B>&#150; Remote Dynamics, Inc. <B>(NASDAQ: REDI)</B>, a leading provider of
telematics-based management solutions for commercial fleets, today announced that on December&nbsp;29,
2004, it received written notice from the Nasdaq Listing Qualifications of the Nasdaq Stock Market
that the company had regained compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4) as the closing bid
price for the company&#146;s common stock had been at $1.00 per share or greater for at least 10
consecutive business days. Thus, the Marketplace Rule&nbsp;4310(c)(4) non-compliance matter was closed
and the company&#146;s listing on the Nasdaq Small Cap Market was in good standing.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>About Remote Dynamics, Inc.</B>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Remote Dynamics, Inc. (remotedynamics.com) markets, sells and supports state-of-the-art fleet
management solutions that contribute to higher customer revenues and improved operator efficiency.
Combining the technologies of the global positioning system (GPS)&nbsp;and wireless vehicle telematics,
the company&#146;s solutions improve the productivity of mobile workers by providing real time position
reports, route information and exception based reporting designed to highlight mobile workforce
inefficiencies. Based in Richardson, Texas, the company also markets, sells and supports a
customized, GPS-based fleet management solutions for large fleets like SBC Communications, Inc.,
which has approximately 30,000 installed vehicles now in operation.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&#147;Remote Dynamics&#148; and the Remote Dynamics logotype are trademarks and service marks of Remote Dynamics, Inc.
