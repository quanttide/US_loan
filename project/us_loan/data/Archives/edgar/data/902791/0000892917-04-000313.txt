Item 5.02.  Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors;
Appointment of Principal Officers

            Effective  December  15,  2004,  the Board of  Directors  of Barrett
Business Services,  Inc. (the "Company"),  elected Jon L. Justesen,  co-owner of
Justesen  Ranches in eastern Oregon,  as a director of the Company.  There is no
arrangement or understanding  between Mr. Justesen and any other person relating
to his election as a director. At the time of this report, there are no plans to
appoint Mr. Justesen to any committee of the Board of Directors.
