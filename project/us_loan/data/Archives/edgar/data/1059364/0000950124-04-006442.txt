(collectively &#147;EaglePicher&#148;) announce that they are revising downward their earnings guidance for
fiscal year 2004. EaglePicher expects to report revenues for the year ending November&nbsp;30, 2004
consistent with its prior guidance of $705 to $712&nbsp;million, Adjusted EBITDA (as defined below) of
$70 to $77&nbsp;million, down from its prior guidance of $82 to $86&nbsp;million, and Credit Agreement EBITDA

end is expected to be $406.3&nbsp;million, within its prior forecast of $400 to $410&nbsp;million, and its
total debt, including the investment in its accounts receivable program, is expected to be $426.3
million.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The reduced forecasted Adjusted EBITDA and Credit Agreement EBITDA ranges are primarily due to
reduced margin booking rates for two long term contracts accounted for under the percentage of
completion method in the Defense &#038; Space Power unit of EaglePicher&#146;s Power Group Segment due to
increased foreign exchange rates and reduced productivity performance and assumptions. These items
did not have a significant impact on cash flow in the fourth quarter. The reduced forecasted
ranges are also due to several lot acceptance test failures on battery programs in the Defense &#038;
Space Power unit, an inventory adjustment, additional vacation and severance accruals and other
miscellaneous items, all within the Defense &#038; Space Power unit.
