ITEM 2.02. COMPLETION OF ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS.

 

On December 15, 2004, Commonwealth Biotechnologies, Inc. (“CBI”) completed the acquisition of certain assets of Genetics & IVF Institute, Inc. related to paternity and forensic test analyses pursuant to an Asset Purchase Agreement executed November 18, 2004. CBI purchased such assets for an aggregate purchase price of $1.1 million payable as follows: (1) $500,000 in cash and (2) $600,000 in debt secured by a line of credit provided by Branch Banking & Trust. A copy of the Purchase Agreement is attached as an Exhibit hereto.
