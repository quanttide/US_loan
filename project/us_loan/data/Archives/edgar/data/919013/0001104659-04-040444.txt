<p align="right" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">EXHIBIT
99.1</font></b></p>

<p align="right" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Wave Systems Receives U.S. Military Academy Contract
for a Data Security Project</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Lee, MA &#150; Dec. 16,
2004 &#150; Wave Systems Corp. (NASDAQ:WAVX) today announced it has received an
order from the United States Military Academy&#146;s Information Technology
Operations Center for Wave to provide information technology products
associated with the academy&#146;s Secure Content Dissemination and Data Retrieval
Computer System project.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Wave will provide
technology, including its EMBASSY&#174; Trust Suite software, EMBASSY-enabled smart
card readers and TecSec&#174; Inc.&#146;s CKM&#174;-enabled Role Based Access Controls
software.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;We are very excited about this project and we believe
it will give our cadets a first hand appreciation for the challenge of getting
information securely into, out of, and across the Department of Defense&#146;s
Global Information Grid,&#148; said Dr. Aaron Ferguson, Department of Defense
Visiting Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science,
United States Military Academy.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The software and hardware provided by Wave supports
the Department of Defense funded cadet Capstone project for secure information
sharing,&#148; he said. &#147;This software and hardware should allow the cadet team to
develop a secure, smart card-enabled network enterprise prototype on a &#145;trusted&#146;
platform.&#148;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The USMA was recently re-designated by the National
Security Agency as a Center of Academic Excellence in Information Assurance
Education.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The project
prototype involves strongly authenticating authorized users of MSOffice&#174;
documents and data applications, allowing those users to process the documents
using digital signatures and allowing users to access specific documents or
portions of documents based on cryptographically-based role-based access
controls,&#148; said Dr. Ferguson.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<div>

<p align="center" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Cadets will be
using the technology from Wave and TecSec to demonstrate interoperability with
the U.S. government&#146;s Common Access Card. There is potential for broad
application of these technologies in the existing military data security
environment.&#148;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Wave
has invested heavily in Trusted Computing and Identity Management hardware and
software solutions,&#148; said Brian Berger, Wave&#146;s executive vice president,
marketing and sales. &#147;We believe these technologies could play an important
role in making government personal computers more trusted and secure while
making the PC applications more secure and easy to manage. We look forward to
participation in the project at West Point.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The use
of the Wave technology combined with TecSec&#146;s CKM products provide the project
with powerful tools to accomplish their objectives,&#148; said John Petty, TecSec&#146;s
Chairman and CEO.</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">CKM,
short for Constructive Key Management, provides enforced access control and
data management allowing users to control anything that can be named, from a
character, page, image or sound in a document or form to a field in a database.</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">The
approach of differentially encrypting data based on the need-to-know or
need-to-share principle allows secure communication among groups of individuals
with a variety of roles. Those individuals who have a legitimate need to view
information have access to it, while others don&#146;t. More information on TecSec&#146;s
CKM products and technology is available at www.tecsec.com.</font></p>

<p style="color:black;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;line-height:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">More
information on Wave Systems Trusted Computing solutions is available at
www.wave.com.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Consumers and
