<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;20, 2004, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of Stewart
Enterprises, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) granted restricted stock and stock options to the Company&#146;s
executive officers.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The stock option agreements for Thomas M. Kitchen, Executive Vice President and Chief
Financial Officer, and Everett N. Kendrick, Senior Vice President and President-Sales and Marketing
Division, will each provide for the grant of options to purchase an aggregate of 186,800 shares of
Company Class&nbsp;A common stock with an exercise price of $6.90 per share with the following vesting
schedule: 25&nbsp;percent on December&nbsp;20, 2005, 25&nbsp;percent on December&nbsp;20, 2006 and 50&nbsp;percent on
December&nbsp;20, 2007. These options expire on December&nbsp;20, 2011. The restricted stock agreements
will each provide for the grant of 29,000 restricted shares of Company Class&nbsp;A common stock with
the same vesting schedule as the stock options described above.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The stock option agreements for Lawrence B. Hawkins, Executive Vice President and
President-Investors Trust, Inc., Brent F. Heffron, Executive Vice President and President-Southern
Division, Michael K. Crane, Sr., Senior Vice President and President-Central Division, G. Kenneth
Stephens, Jr., Senior Vice President and President-Eastern Division, and Randall L. Stricklin,
Senior Vice President and President-Western Division, will each provide for the grant of options to
purchase an aggregate of 46,700 shares of Company Class&nbsp;A common stock with an exercise price of
$6.90 per share. The options vest equally on December&nbsp;20, 2005, 2006, 2007 and 2008 and expire on
December&nbsp;20, 2011. The restricted stock agreements will each provide for the grant of 7,300
restricted shares of Company Class&nbsp;A common stock with the same vesting schedule as the stock
options described above.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The description above of the restricted stock and stock option agreements are not complete and
are qualified in their entirety by the actual terms of the agreements, copies of which will be
filed as exhibits to the Company&#146;s Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended October&nbsp;31, 2004.
