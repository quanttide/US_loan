<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;MINNEAPOLIS,

of fiscal 2005. Net sales for the 13 weeks ended Nov. 28, 2004, increased 4 percent to
$3.17 billion. Earnings after tax increased 19 percent to $367 million, as higher
commodity and other input costs were more than offset by increased sales, lower
restructuring and other identified items expense, and a lower effective tax rate due to
the resolution of certain tax items. Diluted earnings per share (EPS) totaled 97 cents
compared to 81 cents in last year&#146;s second quarter. </FONT></P>
