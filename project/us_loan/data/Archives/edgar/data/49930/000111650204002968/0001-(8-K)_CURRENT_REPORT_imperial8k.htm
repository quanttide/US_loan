Item 8.01   Other Events.


On December 21, 2004, Imperial Industries, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a Press Release announcing that at a special meeting of stockholders, the stockholders authorized the Company’s Board of Directors to implement a reverse stock split at one of two possible ratios: either (a) one for four ratio; or (b) a one for five ratio to be determined by the Board in its discretion. The date for enactment of the reverse stock split as well as the ratio has not yet been determined. The Board of Directors has reserved the right to enact the reverse split at any time within one year of shareholder approval or not to enact it at all. The Company currently has approximately 9,514,073 common shares outstanding.
