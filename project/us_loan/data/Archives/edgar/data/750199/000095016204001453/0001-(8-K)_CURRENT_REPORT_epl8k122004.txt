Item 1.01.        Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

     On December 16, 2004, Energy Partners, Ltd. (the "Company") and its
wholly-owned subsidiary EPL of Louisiana L.L.C. entered into a purchase
agreement with Castex Energy 1995, L.P. and Castex Energy, Inc. (the "Purchase
Agreement"), pursuant to which the Company will acquire oil and natural gas
properties in south Louisiana for $150 million in cash. In conjunction with the
Purchase Agreement, the parties also entered into an exploration agreement,
dated as of December 16, 2004 (the "Exploration Agreement"), pursuant to which
they agreed to explore and develop oil and gas reserves in an area of mutual
interest encompassing over one million acres in south Louisiana over the next
two years.

     The foregoing descriptions of the Purchase Agreement and the Exploration
Agreement are qualified in their entireties by reference to the Purchase
Agreement and the Exploration Agreement attached as Exhibit 10.1 and 10.2,
respectively, to this current report on Form 8-K.
