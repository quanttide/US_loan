<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">ITEM&nbsp;1.01&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; ENTRY
INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;22, 2004, The Macerich Partnership, L.P., a Delaware
limited partnership (&#147;Macerich LP&#148;), The Macerich Company, a Maryland
corporation (&#147;Macerich&#148;), Wilmorite Properties, Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#147;Wilmorite&#148;)
and Wilmorite Holdings, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership (&#147;Wilmorite LP&#148;)
entered into a definitive agreement and plan of merger (the &#147;Merger Agreement&#148;).&#160; Pursuant to the Merger Agreement, a merger
subsidiary of Macerich LP will merge with and into Wilmorite, with Wilmorite
being the surviving entity (the &#147;Merger&#148;).&#160;
Another Macerich LP subsidiary will merge with and into Wilmorite LP,
with Wilmorite LP being the surviving entity.&#160;
Following the mergers and the subsequent liquidation of Wilmorite, Macerich
LP will become the general partner of Wilmorite LP.&#160; Wilmorite LP will also contribute certain of
its assets to Macerich LP.&#160; Wilmorite LP
will be referred to as &#147;New Wilmorite LP&#148; following the consummation of the
Merger.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The total consideration paid by Macerich LP in the mergers will be
$2.333 billion, including the assumption of approximately $882 million of
existing debt at an average interest rate of 6.43% and the issuance of
convertible preferred units and common units in New Wilmorite LP totaling an
estimated $320 million.&#160; The balance will
be paid in cash.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Each stockholder of Wilmorite will be entitled to receive cash in the
amount of the Merger Consideration as defined in the Merger Agreement.&#160; Each limited partner of Wilmorite LP will be
entitled to elect to receive the Merger Consideration in cash, or in the form
of convertible preferred units (&#147;CPUs&#148;) or common units in New Wilmorite LP.&#160; Each CPU will have a liquidation preference
of $62.39 per unit, an initial conversion price of $74.868 and an initial quarterly
dividend of approximately $0.897.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The CPUs will be subject to the following redemption and conversion
rights:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <i><font style="font-style:italic;">Rochester Redemption Rights.</font></i> Holders of CPUs will have the
right to elect to participate in certain redemption rights associated with a portion
of the Wilmorite portfolio generally located in the greater Rochester, New York
area (the &#147;Rochester Properties&#148;).&#160;
Holders of CPUs subject to these rights (&#147;Rochester CPUs&#148;) will have the
right to redeem them in exchange for an in-kind distribution of the Rochester
Properties, during a three-month period beginning on August&nbsp;31, 2007, subject
to certain conditions.&#160; New Wilmorite LP
will have the right to redeem the Rochester CPUs in exchange for the Rochester
Properties for a three-month period starting on December&nbsp;1, 2009.&#160; The Rochester Properties include Eastview
Mall, Eastview Commons, Greece Ridge Center, Marketplace Mall and Pittsford
Plaza.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <i><font style="font-style:italic;">Put Right and Forced Conversion Rights.</font></i>&#160; The holders of CPUs other than Rochester CPUs
(&#147;Non-Rochester CPUs&#148;) will have the right to require New Wilmorite LP to
redeem their Non-Rochester CPUs during a thirty-day period following the
seventh anniversary of the closing date of the transaction, for cash (or, at
the election of Macerich, for shares of Macerich common stock) at a price of $53.0315
per Non-Rochester CPU (subject to certain adjustments), up to an aggregate
maximum of $75 million.&#160; During the same
thirty-day period, New Wilmorite LP will have the right to compel the holders
of Non-Rochester CPUs to redeem their Non-Rochester CPUs for common units of New
Wilmorite LP for $82.3548 per Non-Rochester CPU (subject to certain
adjustments).</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <i><font style="font-style:italic;">General Unit Conversion Rights. </font></i>&#160;Non-Rochester CPUs will be convertible for
common units of New Wilmorite LP at any time.&#160;
Beginning on August&nbsp;31, 2010 (or earlier, under certain
circumstances), Rochester CPUs will be convertible for common units of New
Wilmorite LP.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <i><font style="font-style:italic;">Macerich LP Call Right.&#160; </font></i>For
a period of 12 months beginning on June&nbsp;1, 2011, New Wilmorite LP will
have the right to cause its CPUs to be exchanged for units of Macerich LP with
substantially similar financial and economic rights.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <i><font style="font-style:italic;">General Redemption Rights.</font></i>&#160;
Beginning one year after the closing date of the transaction, Non-Rochester
CPUs will be redeemable for cash or, at the option of Macerich, for shares of
Macerich common stock, pursuant to an established formula.&#160; Beginning on August&nbsp;31, 2010 (or
earlier, under certain circumstances), Rochester CPUs will be redeemable on the
same terms.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">MACERICH
ANNOUNCES $2.333 BILLION AGREEMENT TO ACQUIRE WILMORITE</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Santa Monica, CA (12/23/04)
&#150; The Macerich Partnership L.P., the operating partnership of The Macerich
Company (NYSE Symbol: MAC), today announced that it has signed a definitive
agreement to acquire Wilmorite Properties, Inc. and Wilmorite Holdings L.P. (&#147;Wilmorite&#148;).
&#160;&#160;The total purchase price will be
approximately $2.333 billion, including the assumption of approximately $882
million of existing debt at an average interest rate of 6.43% and the issuance
of convertible preferred units and common units totaling an estimated $320
million. Each limited partner of Wilmorite Holdings L.P. will be entitled to
elect to receive cash, the convertible preferred units or the common
units.&#160; &#160;The convertible preferred units will have a liquidation
preference of $62.39 per unit, a conversion price of $74.87 per unit and an
initial dividend yield of approximately 5.75% of the liquidation preference.&#160; Approximately $210 million of the convertible
preferred units can be redeemed, subject to certain conditions, for that
portion of the Wilmorite portfolio generally located in the greater Rochester
area. The balance of the consideration to Wilmorite&#146;s equity holders will be paid
in cash.&#160; This transaction has been
approved by each company&#146;s Board of Directors, subject to customary closing
conditions. A majority-in-interest of the limited partners of Wilmorite Holdings
L.P. and of the stockholders of its general partner, Wilmorite Properties, Inc.,
have also approved this acquisition.&#160; &#160;It is currently anticipated that this
transaction will be completed in March, 2005.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Wilmorite&#146;s existing
portfolio includes interests in 11 regional malls and two open-air community
centers, with 13.4 million square feet of space located in Connecticut, New
York, New Jersey, Kentucky and Virginia.&#160;
Approximately 5 million square feet of gross leaseable area is located
at three premier regional malls: Tysons Corner Center in McLean, Virginia, Freehold
Raceway Mall in Freehold, New Jersey and Danbury Fair Mall in Danbury,
Connecticut.&#160; The average tenant sales
per square foot, for these three centers, is in excess of $525.&#160; The total portfolio average of mall store
annual sales per square foot is $403.&#160; On
a pro forma basis reflecting this acquisition, Macerich will own 74 regional
malls with total portfolio square footage increasing to approximately 75.4
million square feet.</font></p>

history of success achieved through a commitment to strategic locations in the
northeast markets, and the development and acquisition of high-quality assets.&#160; &#160;The
addition of Tysons Corner Center, Freehold Raceway Mall and Danbury Fair Mall

super-regional malls in the East with combined total annual retail sales in
excess of $2 billion.&#160; This portfolio is
particularly appealing due to the strong internal growth we expect from these
assets.&#160; Furthermore there are
significant expansion and redevelopment opportunities at Tysons and Freehold.&#148;</font></p>

company.&#160; Macerich has a great track
record and a long history in the mall ownership and redevelopment
business.&#160; The combined strengths of both
organizations will have a positive impact on our mall portfolio.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In addition to the
assumed debt and the common and convertible preferred units, the transaction financing
has been arranged and will include a $600 million five year unsecured term note
and a $300 million interim loan with a term of up to 1.5 years.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In November, Macerich provided
guidance of its estimated 2005 Funds from Operations (&#147;FFO&#148;) per share-diluted
in the range of $4.20 to $4.30.&#160;
Management expects the Wilmorite acquisition to be neutral to FFO in
2005 and is reaffirming its 2005 FFO per share guidance.&#160; Further FFO and EPS guidance will be given after
the closing of the transaction.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Deutsche Bank Securities,
Inc. acted as financial advisor and O&#146;Melveny &amp; Myers acted as legal
counsel to Macerich on the transaction.&#160;
Morgan Stanley acted as financial advisor and Goodwin Procter LLP and
Harris Beach LLP acted as counsel to Wilmorite on this transaction.</font></p>

on the acquisition, leasing, management, development and redevelopment of
regional malls throughout the United States.&#160;
The Company is the sole general partner and owns an 81% ownership
