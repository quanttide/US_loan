ITEM 5.02    DEPARTURE OF DIRECTOR OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS;
             APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS

(b)      On December 21, 2004, the Board of Directors of the Company accepted
         the resignation of Mr. Victor Wang, due to his other business
         obligations.

(d)      On December 21, 2004, the Board of Directors of the Company appointed
         Mr. Victor Zhou to be a director, replacing Mr. Victor Wang. Mr. Zhou
         will not be assigned to any board committees at this time.

         Mr. Zhou is the President of Fixed Assets Investment of Huatai
         Securities Co. He has been with the company since 1997 and served in
         other senior management positions with increasing responsibilities.
         Before joining Huatai Securities, Mr. Zhou was with Jiangsu Securities
         Co. and held the General Manager position.

         Mr. Zhou received his Bachelor of Arts in Economics from Hunan
         University and EMBA from China Europe International Business School.
