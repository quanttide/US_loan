<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;1.02. Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On December&nbsp;21, 2004, PeopleSoft, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) and Aneel Bhusri, Vice Chairman of the
Board, agreed to terminate the Consulting Agreement previously entered into by the parties. Under
the terms of the Consulting Agreement, Mr.&nbsp;Bhusri is entitled to receive $125,000 per month
beginning October&nbsp;1, 2004 for his services as Vice Chairman and is reimbursed for reasonable out of
pocket expenses. Such payments are in addition to directors&#146; fees to which Mr.&nbsp;Bhusri is entitled
as a member of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors. Pursuant to the mutual agreement of the parties,
the Consulting Agreement will terminate on December&nbsp;31, 2004 and the obligations of each party under
the Consulting Agreement, including the Company&#146;s obligation to
pay Mr.&nbsp;Bhusri the monthly compensation provided in the
Consulting Agreement, will apply until such date. The terms of the agreement between
the Company and Mr.&nbsp;Bhusri are set forth in the Termination of Consulting Agreement attached hereto
as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1.

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>TERMINATION OF CONSULTING AGREEMENT</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This Termination of Consulting Agreement (this &#147;<I>Agreement</I>&#148;) is made as of December&nbsp;15, 2004 by
and between PeopleSoft, Inc. (the &#147;<I>Company</I>&#148;) and Aneel Bhusri (&#147;<I>Consultant</I>&#148;).


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><U>RECITALS</U>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;WHEREAS, the Company and Consultant previously have entered into that certain Consulting
Agreement dated as of December&nbsp;6, 2004 (the &#147;<I>Consulting Agreement</I>&#148;); and


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;WHEREAS, PeopleSoft and Consultant desire to terminate the Consulting Agreement.


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><U>AGREEMENT</U>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the covenants set forth herein and for good and valuable
consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Company and
Consultant agree as follows:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1.&nbsp;<U>Termination</U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Consulting Agreement shall be terminated as of December&nbsp;31, 2004 (the &#147;<I>Termination Date</I>&#148;)
and, following such date shall be of no further force or effect. The Company and Consultant hereby
waive the requirement of 30-days&#146; notice of termination in the Consulting Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;2.&nbsp;<U>Pre-Termination Payments and Other Obligations</U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The obligations of the Company and Consultant under the Consulting Agreement, including the
Company&#146;s obligation to pay Consultant the amounts owed under the Consulting Agreement, shall
continue in effect until the Termination Date.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;3.&nbsp;<U>Rights and Obligations</U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Following the Termination Date, PeopleSoft and Consultant shall have no further rights or
obligations under or pursuant to the Consulting Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4.&nbsp;<U>Counterparts; Facsimile Transmission</U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This Agreement may be executed in separate counterparts, both of which are deemed to be an
original and all of which taken together shall constitute one and the same agreement. Delivery of
executed signature pages hereof by facsimile transmission shall constitute effective and binding
execution and delivery of this Agreement.
