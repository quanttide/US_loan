Section 7 - Regulation FD

      Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

            On December 29, 2004, Airspan Networks, Inc. (the "Company") issued
a press release announcing that it has entered into a non-binding Letter of
Intent to acquire all of the authorized, issued and outstanding shares of common
stock of Arelnet Ltd. in exchange for cash and common stock of the Company. The
acquisition is subject to the execution of definitive agreements and certain
other conditions, including customary closing conditions. A copy of the press
release is attached to this Form 8-K as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein
by reference.
