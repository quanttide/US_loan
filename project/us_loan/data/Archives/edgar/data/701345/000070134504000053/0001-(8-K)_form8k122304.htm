Item 8.01      Other Events

     On December 23, 2004, US Airways, Inc. and US Airways Group, Inc. filed a monthly operating report for the period November 1, 2004 through November 30, 2004 with the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Eastern District of Virginia, Alexandria Division in connection with their voluntary petitions for reorganization under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code in Case No. 04-13819. A copy of the monthly operating report is furnished as Exhibit 99 to this Form 8-K.
