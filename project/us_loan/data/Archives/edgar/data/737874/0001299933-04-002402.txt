<B>
	Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
</B>
</FONT>
</P>
<P ALIGN="LEFT">
<FONT SIZE="2">
On December 21, 2004, the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (the "PBGC") and Laidlaw International, Inc. entered into an amendment to their Agreement dated June 18, 2003 (the "Amendment"), whereby the PBGC has agreed to extend the deadline for the sale of approximately 3.8 million shares of Laidlaw International, Inc. common stock held in trust for the benefit of the Greyhound U.S. pension plans to April 15, 2005, which will enable Laidlaw to use a portion of the proceeds from the sale of its healthcare companies, scheduled to close by the end of March 2005, to repurchase all or a portion of those shares.  Attached hereto and incorporated herein is the Amendment.   Laidlaw issued a press release on December 22, 2004 announcing this Amendment to its agreement with the PBGC, which press release is attached hereto as an exhibit and incorporated herein.

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 16pt"><B>AMENDMENT TO AGREEMENT</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 16pt; text-indent: 8%"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt">WHEREAS, Laidlaw International, Inc. (&#147;Laidlaw&#148;) as the reorganized successor to Laidlaw
Inc. under Chapter&nbsp;11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code, and the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
(&#147;PBGC&#148;) are parties to an Agreement dated June&nbsp;18, 2003 (the &#147;Agreement&#148;);</FONT>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 8%">WHEREAS, pursuant to the Agreement among other things Laidlaw agreed to establish a trust to
hold shares of its common stock as security for certain obligations under the Agreement (the &#147;Stock
Trust&#148;) and contribute shares of common stock having a value of no less than $50,000,000 at the
time of such contribution (the &#147;Stock&#148;);


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 8%">WHEREAS, Laidlaw entered into a trust agreement (the &#147;Trust Agreement&#148;) dated June&nbsp;20, 2003
with U.S. Trust Company, N.A., as trustee of the Stock Trust (the &#147;Trustee&#148;) pursuant to which the
Trustee would hold the Stock and in accordance with the terms of the Trust Agreement and the
Agreement would sell the Stock no later than December&nbsp;31, 2004 and contribute the proceeds thereto
to the Plans (as defined in the Agreement);


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 8%">WHEREAS, Laidlaw and the PBGC have mutually agreed that the Trustee may delay the sale of the
Stock and the contribution of the proceeds to the Plan from December&nbsp;31, 2004 until no later than
April&nbsp;15, 2005;



<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt">WHEREAS, the Agreement may be amended in writing signed by the parties thereto;



<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt">NOW, THEREFORE, the parties hereto hereby amend the Agreement as follows:

<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;<U>Interest</U>&#148; means an amount equal to interest accruing at a rate equal

December&nbsp;31, 2004 on a principal balance equal to the fair market value of the Stock
on December&nbsp;31, 2004, over the period commencing on December&nbsp;31, 2004 and ending on
the date the Stock is liquidated under the Stock Trust and the proceeds thereof
contributed to the Plans.



<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;<U>Stock</U>&#148; means the shares of common stock of Laidlaw held in the Stock
Trust.

<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;(c) Not later than April&nbsp;15, 2005, the Laidlaw Group shall pay in cash to the

plus the amount of Interest, which such Enhanced Contributions shall be allocated to
the Plan Years in which the payments are actually made (or to the prior Plan Years,
if made within eight and one-half (8 <FONT style="font-size: 75%">1/2</FONT>) months of the end of any such prior Plan
Years) for Funding Standard Account purposes. These payments will be in addition to
any Minimum Funding Contributions owed to the Plan for the corresponding Plan
Years.&#148;

<P align="left" style="margin-left:8%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">&#147;2. Laidlaw shall make the Stock freely transferable by the Trustee as soon as
reasonably practicable. The Stock may be sold in one or more transactions fro no
less than the current fair market value at the time of the sale. Subject to
compliance with applicable federal and state securities laws, the Trustee will have
the duty to sell the Stock as soon as practicable an in a manner designed to
maximize sale proceeds, but no later than April&nbsp;15, 2005. Immediately upon such
sale(s) the Trustee shall contribute all of the proceeds to the Plan pursuant to the
Trust Agreement. Upon sale of all of the Stock and liquidation of the Stock Trust,
Laidlaw shall contribute an amount equal to the Interest to the Plans. To the
extent such proceeds and the amount of Interest are allocated among the Pension Plan
in the proportions described in Section&nbsp;II.A.2, such proceeds and Interest shall be
treated as a cash payment to the Pension Plans under Section&nbsp;II.A.1.(c). Laidlaw&#146;s
consent will be required for any Trustee-initiated sales of Stock, and subject to
compliance with applicable federal and state securities laws, Laidlaw will have the
right to direct the Trustee to sell the Stock.&#148;

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>Laidlaw International To Repurchase Shares</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 4%">NAPERVILLE, IL, December&nbsp;22, 2004 &#151; Laidlaw International, Inc. (NYSE: LI) announced today
that the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation has agreed to extend the deadline for the sale of the
approximately 3.8&nbsp;million shares of Laidlaw International common stock held in trust for the
benefit of the Greyhound U.S. Pension Plans to April&nbsp;15, 2005. Laidlaw International intends to
use a portion of the proceeds from the sale of its healthcare companies, due to close by the end of
March&nbsp;2005, to repurchase all or a portion of those shares.
