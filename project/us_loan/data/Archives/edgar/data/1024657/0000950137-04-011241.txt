<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">WEST CORPORATION ANNOUNCES 2005 FINANCIAL OUTLOOK



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(Omaha, NE &#151; December&nbsp;15, 2004) &#151; West Corporation (NASDAQ: WSTC), a leading provider of
outsourced communication solutions, today announced its financial guidance for 2005. The company
expects to report revenues in the range of $1,375 to $1,425&nbsp;million, and net income between $120
and $130&nbsp;million for the fiscal year ending December&nbsp;31, 2005. These results are expected to yield
a consolidated operating margin between 15% and 15.75%.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>About West Corporation</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">West Corporation is a leading provider of outsourced communication solutions to many of the world&#146;s
largest companies. The company helps its clients communicate effectively, maximize the value of
their customer relationships and drive greater revenue from each transaction. West&#146;s integrated
suite of customized solutions includes customer acquisition, customer care and retention services,
interactive voice response services, conferencing and accounts receivable management services.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Founded in 1986 and headquartered in Omaha, Nebraska, West has a team of approximately 27,000
employees based in North America, Europe and Asia.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">For more information, please visit www.west.com.
