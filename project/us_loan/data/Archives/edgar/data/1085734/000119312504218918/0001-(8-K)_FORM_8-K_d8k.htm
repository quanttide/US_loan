On December 22, 2004, Bloomberg released an article discussing Blockbuster Inc.’s potential acquisition of Hollywood Entertainment Corporation, which included information obtained from an interview with Edward B. Stead, Executive Vice President and General Counsel of Blockbuster. A copy of the article is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto.
