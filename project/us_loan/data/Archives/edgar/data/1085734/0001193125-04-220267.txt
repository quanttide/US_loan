that is likely to lead to an agreement for an acquisition of Hollywood by Blockbuster. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1. Blockbuster also reiterated its willingness to consider a higher price if Hollywood&#146;s Board
cooperates and if the financial and other information sought from Hollywood support such an increase. Blockbuster has not been given access to such Hollywood information. Following the completion of any tender offer, Blockbuster intends to acquire
