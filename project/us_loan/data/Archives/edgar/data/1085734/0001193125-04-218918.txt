 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT

FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">2004-12-22 16:46 (New York) </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">By Jonathan Berr
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Dec. 22 (Bloomberg) &#151; Blockbuster Inc. said Hollywood
Entertainment Corp.&#146;s board is hindering its $700 million bid for the company by insisting that Blockbuster agree to not take the offer directly to shareholders for three years. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Hollywood Entertainment&#146;s directors are demanding a so-called standstill agreement from Blockbuster as the companies

STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are having some difficulty getting cooperation from the Hollywood
board,&#148; Stead said. &#147;We are unwilling to sign a three-year standstill agreement,&#148; he said. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Blockbuster, whose bid is supported by billionaire financier Carl Icahn, is competing to buy Hollywood Entertainment against Movie Gallery Inc. and buyout
firm Leonard Green &amp; Partners. Hollywood Entertainment&#146;s insistence that Blockbuster not take its offer to shareholders, which could be challenged in court, may signal it expects higher offers. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;Eventually, Hollywood is going to have to consider&#148; revising the
provision, said Stacey Widlitz, an analyst with Fulcrum Global Partners LLC, who has a &#147;neutral&#148; rating on Blockbuster and Hollywood Entertainment shares. &#147;If they don&#146;t, there are going to be major lawsuits. You can&#146;t just
ignore them.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Icahn&#146;s Backing </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Leonard Green &amp; Partners bid $10.25 a share for Hollywood Entertainment
