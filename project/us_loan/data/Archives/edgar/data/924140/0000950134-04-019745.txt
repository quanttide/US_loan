<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(a)&nbsp;On December&nbsp;20, 2004, the Company&#146;s Board of Directors appointed Monty R. Standifer, 64,
to serve as Executive Vice President, Chief Financial Officer and Secretary of Gadzooks, Inc. Mr.
Standifer has an extensive retail background and has previously been chief financial officer for
several national companies. Since 2003, Mr.&nbsp;Standifer has been a partner and served as Executive
Vice President and CFO of Buffet Partners, L.P., a privately held investment group. Prior to that,
Mr.&nbsp;Standifer served as a consultant to various companies in connection with business planning and
capital formation from 2000 to 2002. Mr.&nbsp;Standifer previously served as a Senior Vice President
and CFO of the Company from 1992 to 1999. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1
to this report.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(b)&nbsp;In connection with his employment, Mr.&nbsp;Standifer and Gadzooks entered into an employment
agreement that provides for a monthly base salary of $20,000. In addition to the base salary, Mr.
Standifer is entitled to an annual bonus based on his service and the Company&#146;s performance, as
determined by the Company&#146;s Board of Directors or Compensation Committee. Additionally, Mr.
Standifer will be granted options to acquire 1.5% of the Company&#146;s common stock outstanding after
the consummation by the Company of its proposed rights offering. The options will have an exercise
price equal to the volume weighted average price of the Company&#146;s common stock over a 30&nbsp;day period
commencing on the 31<SUP>st</SUP> day after the effective date of the Company&#146;s plan of
reorganization, with one-third of the grant vesting at the end of each year after such effective
date. The options are exercisable for five years from the date of grant. The employment agreement
also contains non-competition and non-solicitation provisions, which apply during the term of the
employment agreement and for a period of 12&nbsp;months thereafter. A copy of the employment agreement
is attached as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Company also entered into a severance agreement with Mr.&nbsp;Standifer, dated December&nbsp;20,
2004. Pursuant to the severance agreement, Mr.&nbsp;Standifer&#146;s employment may be terminated by either
party at any time upon 30&nbsp;days written notice. If the Company terminates the employment agreement
without cause, death or disability (as defined therein) or if Mr.&nbsp;Standifer terminates the
employment agreement for good reason, Mr.&nbsp;Standifer is entitled to severance compensation equal to
his accrued but unpaid base salary, earned bonus through the termination date, plus an additional
severance payment equal to one year of his base salary. Further, any stock options that would have
vested at the end of the year of termination shall vest upon the termination date. In addition, if
the Company terminates the employment agreement after the effective date of the plan of
reorganization, the amount of additional severance payment set forth above shall be doubled. A
copy of the severance agreement is attached as Exhibit&nbsp;10.2 to this report and incorporated herein
by reference.

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Gadzooks Announces Appointment of Monty Standifer as<BR>
Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Dallas, Texas, December&nbsp;21, 2004 &#150; Gadzooks, Inc. (OTC Pink Sheets: GADZQ) today announced
that Monty Standifer has joined the Company as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial
Officer. Mr.&nbsp;Standifer has an extensive retail background as a CFO for several national retailers.
Previous financial management includes RadioShack (formerly Tandy Corporation) and BizMart Office
Products. He also served as Gadzooks&#146; CFO from 1992 until 1999. He was most recently Executive
Vice President and CFO of Buffet Partners, operators of the Furr&#146;s Restaurants chain throughout the
Southwest.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Dallas-based Gadzooks is a specialty retailer of casual clothing, accessories and shoes for 16-22
year-old females. Gadzooks now operates 243 stores in 40 states.
