<p align="left" style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;text-indent:0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Item 2.05&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Costs Associated with Exit or
Disposal Activities.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;letter-spacing:-.15pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;letter-spacing:-.15pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 17, 2004, Sealed Air Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;)
committed to new profit improvement plans that primarily involve the relocation
of operations at three small facilities located outside of the United States in
the Company&#146;s food packaging segment, and the related elimination of positions.&#160; These plans are in addition to the profit
improvement plans announced by the Company on October 27, 2004.&#160; As a result, the Company estimates that its
aggregate pre-tax restructuring charge in the fourth quarter of 2004 will range
from $30 to $35 million as compared to its previously-disclosed range of $10 to
$15 million.&#160; Of the aggregate charges in
the fourth quarter, approximately $21 to $24 million will be charges for
employee termination costs and approximately $9 to $11 million will be asset
impairment costs.&#160; The Company
anticipates that it will make cash expenditures totaling approximately $22
million to $26 million starting in the fourth quarter of 2004 through the
second quarter of 2006.</font></p>
