ITEM 1.01. ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT.

         On December 22, 2004, Lindsay Manufacturing Co. (the "Company") entered
into a Second Amendment (the "Amendment") to the Employment Agreement (the
"Agreement") between the Company and Richard W. Parod, President and Chief
Executive Officer of the Company. This Amendment extended the term of the
Agreement for two additional years so that the term shall continue in effect
until April 5, 2007. A copy of the Amendment is filed as Exhibit 10(a) hereto.
