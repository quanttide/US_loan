Item 7.01  Regulation FD Disclosure.

 

On December 30, 2004, Southwest Casino Corporation issued a press release regarding correspondence from the National Indian Gaming Commission to the Cheyenne and Arapaho Tribes of Oklahoma, for whom Southwest manages the Lucky Star Casinos in Concho and Clinton, Oklahoma.  In a letter to the Tribes, the NIGC expressed its concern that the Tribes are not allocating the revenue received from their gaming operations appropriately and stating that improper allocation could lead to penalties up to and including closure of the Tribes’ gaming facilities.  The NIGC’s concerns are not related to Southwest’s management of gaming operations for the Tribes.
