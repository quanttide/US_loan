Item 1.01.                                              Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

As of December 23, 2004, Fleetwood Enterprises, Inc.’s financial services subsidiary, HomeOne Credit Corp., entered into an amendment to its $75 million warehouse line of credit with Greenwich Capital Financial Products, Inc. extending the termination date from December 30, 2004 to January 31, 2005.  All other terms and conditions of the warehouse line of credit remain the same, including the limited guaranty of Fleetwood Enterprises, Inc. of obligations under the line of credit.  The purpose of the extension is to provide the parties with additional time to document a renewal of the facility; however, no assurance can be given that HomeOne will be successful in its efforts to either renew the facility or to obtain a similar facility.

 

As of November 30, 2004 outstanding borrowings under the line of credit were approximately $30,500,000.
