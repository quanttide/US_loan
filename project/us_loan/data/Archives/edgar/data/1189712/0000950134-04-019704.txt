<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><I>Item&nbsp;1.01.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</I></B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;20, 2004, Dreyer&#146;s Grand Ice Cream Holdings, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) entered into a letter
agreement to amend the Bridge Loan Facility dated June&nbsp;11, 2003 by and between the Company and
Nestl&#233; S.A. to increase the Company&#146;s borrowing capacity under the facility from $400,000,000 to
$650,000,000. The terms of the Bridge Loan Facility, as amended, are described in the Company&#146;s
Form 10-Q Report for the quarterly period ended September&nbsp;25, 2004, in Part&nbsp;I, Item&nbsp;1 in Note 7 of
Notes to Consolidated Financial Statements, and in Part&nbsp;I, Item&nbsp;2 under the caption &#147;Financial
Conditions &#150; Liquidity and Capital Resources &#151; Nestl&#233; S.A. Credit Facility.&#148; Nestl&#233; S.A. is the
ultimate parent of two companies which own approximately 67% of the diluted shares of the Company.
