ITEM 8.01        Other Events.

 

                On December 23, 2004, Tenet Healthcare Corporation issued a press release announcing that a subsidiary which operates Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center in Palm Beach, Fla., had agreed in principle to settle 106 individual lawsuits filed against the hospital that alleged the plaintiffs had suffered post-surgical infections after having cardiac surgeries at the facility.  Under the agreement, Tenet will pay $31 million to the plaintiffs and their counsel.  The press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1.
