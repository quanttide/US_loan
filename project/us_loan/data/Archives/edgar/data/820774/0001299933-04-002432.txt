<B>
	Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.
</B>
</FONT>
</P>
<P ALIGN="LEFT">
<FONT SIZE="2">
On December 21, 2004, Ashworth, Inc. (the "Company") issued stock option grants to each of its executive officers and non-employee directors, as approved by the Company's board of directors and in accordance with its 2000 Equity Incentive Plan.  The opions have an exercise price equal to the fair market value of the Company's common stock on the grant date and will expire no more than 10 years from such date.<br><br>Each non-employee director was granted an option to purchase 20,000 shares of the Company's common stock.  With the exception of Mr. Gambucci whose options vest immediately, the options granted to the non-employee directors will vest in four quarterly installments on each of February 1, 2005, May 1, 2005, August 1, 2005 and November 1, 2005.  <br><br>The options granted to the executive officers will vest in three equal installments on each of the first, second and third anniversary of the grant date.  The exectutive officers were granted ptions to purchase up to the following number of shares of
 the company's common stock:<br><br>Randall L. Herrel, Sr. - Chairman, President and CEO - 45,000<br>Terence W. Tsang - Executive Vice President and CFO - 30,000<br>Peter s. Case - Senior Vice President-Finance - 5,000<br>Peter E. Holmberg - Senior Vice President-Sales - 15,000<br>Gary I. Schneiderman - Senior Vice President-Sales - 18,000<br><br>In accordance with the Company's 2000 Equity Incentive Plan, the Company enters into a stock agreement with each optionee at time of grant.  A copy of the Company's form of stock option agreement is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1.
