Item 5.02. Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers

On December 28, 2004, we announced the election of Mr. Chris MacFarland as a new member of our Board of Directors. The election of Mr. MacFarland increases the number of directors to five and the number of independent directors to two. Prior to his election to our Board of Directors, Mr. MacFarland had been serving on our advisory board. In connection with his service on the advisory board, in March 2004, we granted Mr. MacFarland an option to purchase 800,000 shares of our common stock, at an exercise price of $0.10 per share.

Our press release announcing this appointment is filed as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.
