<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On
December&nbsp;21, 2004, Western Gas Resources,&nbsp;Inc. ("Western") expanded its senior unsecured revolving credit facility with its existing syndicate of lenders from
$400&nbsp;million to a total of $500&nbsp;million. Any borrowings by Western pursuant to this increase in commitments will be subject to and in accordance with the terms and conditions of the
existing senior unsecured revolving credit facility. The additional availability under the facility is intended to be used for general working capital purposes if required. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Additionally,
Western entered into an amendment to increase the amount of aggregate commitments which Western can further request from lenders from time to time by an additional
$100&nbsp;million. All other terms and conditions of the senior unsecured revolving credit facility remain unmodified. In the event that Western seeks to expand the senior unsecured revolving credit
facility by this additional $100&nbsp;million the approval of Western's board of directors and approval of the lenders under the facility would be required. </FONT></P>
