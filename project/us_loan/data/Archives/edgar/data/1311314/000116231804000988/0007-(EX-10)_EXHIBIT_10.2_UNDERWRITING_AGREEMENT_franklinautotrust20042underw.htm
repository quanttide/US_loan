(“Franklin Resources”).  Pursuant to the Sale and Servicing Agreement, the Seller will sell the Receivables to the Trust and the Servicer will service the Receivables on behalf of the Trust.  In addition, pursuant to the Sale and Servicing Agreement, the Servicer will agree to perform certain administrative tasks on behalf of the Trust imposed on the Trust under the Indenture.  The Notes will be issued pursuant to the Indenture to be dated as of December 1, 2004 (as amended and supplemented from time to time, the “Indenture”), between the Trust and The Bank of New York (the “Trustee”).  The Seller will form the Trust pursuant to a Trust Agreement dated as of December 1, 2004 (the “Short-Form Trust Agreement”), as amended and restated by the Amended and Restated Trust Agreement to be dated as of December 1, 2004 (the “Amended and Restated Trust Agreement” and together with the Short-Form Trust Agreement, the “Trust Agreement”) between the Seller and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Delaware, as owner trustee (the “Owner Trustee”).  The Certificates, each representing a fractional undivided equity interest in the Trust, will be issued pursuant to the Trust Agreement.
