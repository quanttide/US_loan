<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;2.03. Creation of a Direct Financial Obligation or an Obligation under an Off-Balance Sheet Arrangement of a Registrant.</B>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;21, 2004 CanArgo Energy Corporation (&#147;<B>CanArgo</B>&#148;) entered into a letter of amendment with
Cornell Capital Partners, LP (&#147;<B>Cornell</B>&#148;) amending the terms of the Promissory Note between CanArgo
and Cornell dated May&nbsp;19, 2004 under which Cornell agreed to advance CanArgo the sum of $1,500,000
(&#147;<B>Promissory Note</B>&#148;). Under the terms of the letter of amendment, CanArgo and Cornell agreed that
any sums due in respect of interest accrued on the Promissory Note shall be paid in cash only.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">A copy of the Letter of Amendment between Cornell and CanArgo is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1
and incorporated herein by reference.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Dear Sirs


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Promissory Note between CanArgo Energy Corporation and Cornell Capital Partners, LP</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">We refer to the Promissory Note dated 19 May&nbsp;2004 between CanArgo Energy Corporation (&#147;<B>CanArgo</B>&#148;)
and Cornell Capital Partners, LP (&#147;<B>Cornell</B>&#148;) pursuant to which Cornell advanced CanArgo the sum of
$1,500,000 (the &#147;<B>Promissory Note</B>&#148;).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Whereas, in accordance with the terms of the Promissory Note, Cornell has the option to require
that any interest due in respect of the Promissory Note shall be paid in CanArgo common stock, par
value $.10 per share (&#147;<B>Common Stock</B>&#148;) or in cash. Now, therefore, for value received and other
good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged and
confirmed, Cornell and CanArgo hereby agree that the Promissory Note be, and it hereby is amended
to provide that all interest payments shall be paid in cash and the terms of the Promissory Note
are deemed amended accordingly.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Save to the extent expressly amended by this letter, the Promissory Note shall remain in full force
and effect. This letter and the Promissory Note set forth the entire understanding of the parties
with respect to the subject matter hereof and thereof and shall be treated together as one document
so that, upon the execution and delivery of this letter by the parties hereto, all references to
the Promissory Note shall include a reference to the Promissory Note as amended in accordance with
the terms of this letter.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">This letter may be executed and delivered in any number of counterparts, all of which taken
together shall be deemed to constitute one and the same document.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">This letter has been executed and delivered by the respective parties hereto by their
representatives, thereunto duly authorized, and shall be governed and construed in accordance with
the laws of the State of New Jersey.
