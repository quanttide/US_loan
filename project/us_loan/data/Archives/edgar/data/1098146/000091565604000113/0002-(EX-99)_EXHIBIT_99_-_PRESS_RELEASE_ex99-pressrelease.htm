PATRIOT NATIONAL BANCORP, INC. ANNOUNCES ITS QUARTERLY DIVIDEND


Stamford, CT, December 21, 2004. Patriot National Bancorp, Inc. (NASDAQ Small Cap "PNBK"), the parent of Patriot National Bank, announced that it has declared a quarterly dividend on its Common Stock.
 
The dividend, in the amount of $0.035 per share, will be payable on January 18, 2005 to shareholders of record as of the close of business on January 4, 2005.
