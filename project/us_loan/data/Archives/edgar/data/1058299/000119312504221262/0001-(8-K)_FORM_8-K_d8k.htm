INFORMATION TO BE INCLUDED IN THE REPORT

 

Section 1 – Registrant’s Business and Operations

 

Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

On December 23, 2004, the Company and certain of its subsidiaries entered into a Credit Agreement with HSBC Bank USA, National Association and Wachovia Bank, National Association to provide to the Company and its subsidiaries a revolving credit facility in an aggregate amount of $35,000,000 of which $25,000,000 will be available to the Company and its U.S. subsidiaries and of which $10,000,000 will be available to the Company’s non-U.S. subsidiaries. The Credit Agreement, including the guarantee and security agreements, are filed herewith as Exhibit 10.1.

 

Item 1.02 Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.

 

Coincident with its execution of a new Credit Agreement as described in Item 1.01 above, the Company has terminated its credit facility with Silicon Valley Bank.
