Item 8.01. Other Events

     On December 28, 2004, Brooks Automation, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing that it has accelerated the vesting of certain unvested options awarded to employees, officers and other eligible participants under the Company’s various stock option plans other than its 1993 Non-Employee Director Stock Option Plan. The affected options are those which had exercise prices greater than approximately $24 per share. A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
