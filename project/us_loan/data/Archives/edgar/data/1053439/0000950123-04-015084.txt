ITEM 1.01       Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

On December 20, 2004, Precision Engine Products Corp., a subsidiary of Stanadyne
Corporation, entered into a manufacturing and supply agreement with Equatorial
Enterprises Limited, a Public Limited company organized and existing under the
laws of India, to manufacture and supply valve train components for a period of
five years.  The supply of components is expected to begin in the first quarter
of 2005.
