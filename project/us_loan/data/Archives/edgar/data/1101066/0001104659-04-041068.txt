<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 4.01.&#160;
Changes in Registrant&#146;s Certifying Accountant.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December 22, 2004, Reef Partners, LLC (&#147;<u>Reef
Partners</u>&#148;), the managing general partner of each of the Reef Global Energy
Ventures (the &#147;<u>Registrant</u>&#148;) partnerships (the &#147;<u>Partnerships</u>&#148;),
dismissed Ernst &amp; Young LLP (&#147;<u>E&amp;Y</u>&#148;) as the Registrant&#146;s
independent registered public accounting firm.&#160;
Effective December 22, 2004, the manager of Reef Partners approved the
engagement of BDO Seidman, LLP (&#147;<u>BDO</u>&#148;) as independent auditors for Reef
Partners and each of the Partnerships for the fiscal year ending December 31,
2004.&#160; BDO will act as certifying
accountant on all future reports of the Registrant and the Partnerships
effective immediately.&#160; The decision to
dismiss E&amp;Y and appoint BDO was made by Reef Partners.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">E&amp;Y&#146;s reports on the financial statements of Reef
Partners and each of the Partnerships for the past two fiscal years did not
contain an adverse opinion or disclaimer of opinion and were not qualified or
modified as to uncertainty, audit scope or accounting principles.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">During 2002 and 2003, and through the date of E&amp;Y&#146;s
dismissal, there have been no &#147;reportable events&#148; requiring disclosure pursuant
to Item 304(a)(1)(v) of Regulation S-K.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connection with the audits of Reef Partners&#146; and
the Partnership&#146;s financial statements for the fiscal years ended December 31,
2002 and December 31, 2003 and in the subsequent interim period, there were no
disagreements between Reef Partners, the Registrant or the Partnerships and
E&amp;Y on any matter of accounting principles or practices, financial
statement disclosure or auditing scope or procedure, which, if not resolved to
the satisfaction of E&amp;Y would have caused E&amp;Y to make reference to the
matter in its report.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Registrant has requested E&amp;Y furnish it a
letter addressed to the Commission stating whether it agrees with the above
statements.&#160; A copy of that letter, dated
December 22, 2004, is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;16.1 to this Form 8-K.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 9.01.&#160;
