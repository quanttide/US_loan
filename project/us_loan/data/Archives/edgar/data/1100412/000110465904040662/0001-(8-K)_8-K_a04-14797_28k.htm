ITEM 1.01.  ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT.

 

On December 20, 2004, Array BioPharma Inc. amended and restated its Deferred Compensation Plan pursuant to which certain eligible members of management and other highly compensated employees of Array BioPharma may elect to defer all or a portion of their compensation. The amendments to the Deferred Compensation Plan consist of amendments to the timing of deferral elections, ability to change deferral elections and the types of permissible distributions in order to comply with certain requirements under the recently enacted American Jobs Creation Act. A copy of the Amended and Restated Deferred Compensation Plan is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1.
