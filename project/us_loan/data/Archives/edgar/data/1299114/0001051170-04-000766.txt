Item 8.01.    Other Events.

     This  current  report  on Form  8-K  relates  to the  monthly  distribution
reported to the holders of ABFC 2004-FF1 Trust, ABFC  Asset-Backed  Certificates
Series  2004-FF1  pursuant to the terms of the Pooling and Servicing  Agreement,
dated  July 1, 2004  among  Asset  Backed  Funding  Corporation,  as  depositor,
Countrywide Home Loans Servicing LP Loan Servicing LP as servicer,  and JPMorgan
Chase Bank, as trustee.

  On  December 27, 2004  distributions  were  made to the  Certificateholders.
Specific information with respect to the distributions is filed as Exhibit 99.1.
No other  reportable  transactions  or matters have occurred  during the current
reporting period.
