Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On December 23, 2004, Tercica, Inc. (“Tercica”) and Genentech, Inc. (“Genentech”) filed a complaint for patent infringement and a declaratory judgment of patent infringement in the U.S. District Court for Northern California against Insmed Incorporated of Glen Allen, Virginia. In the suit, Tercica and Genentech are seeking an injunction, damages and declaratory relief.

 

The suit alleges that Insmed’s activities relating to the commercialization of the Somatokine® product for the treatment of Growth Hormone Insensitivity Syndrome (GHIS) in the United States infringe two issued U.S. patents that Genentech exclusively licensed to Tercica. Somatokine® is a combination drug containing insulin-like growth factor-1 (IGF-1) and insulin-like growth factor binding protein-3 (IGFBP-3).
