Superior Store Economic Model Substantially all stores are profitable in the first 12 months of operation In 2003, average store revenues for stores opened for the entire year were $1.6 million; net retail sales per gross square foot* expected to approximate $600 in 2004 Store selling, general and administrative expense includes marketing and advertising expense of approximately 8% of total revenues and store payroll of 14% - 15% of total revenues For stores opened in 2003, investment per store averaged $485,000 - including cost of leasehold improvements (net of tenant allowances), fixtures and equipment, inventory (net of trade payables), and pre-opening expenses.
