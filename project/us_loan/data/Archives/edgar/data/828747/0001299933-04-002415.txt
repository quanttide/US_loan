<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 9pt">Collegiate Pacific to Present at ICR Leisure Conference</FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 9pt">DALLAS&#151;(BUSINESS WIRE)&#151;Dec. 22, 2004&#151;Collegiate Pacific (AMEX:</FONT><FONT style="font-size: 8pt"><U>BOO</FONT><FONT style="font-size: 9pt"></U>) today
announced that it has been invited and has accepted an invitation to present at the ICR Conference
Jan 13-14 in Carlsbad, Calif. The ICR conference is one of the nations fastest growing and
prestigious conferences for the leisure time and recreational industry with attendance expected to
exceed 600.</FONT>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 9pt">Michael Blumenfeld, CEO of Collegiate Pacific, stated &#147;We are pleased to be included in this
conference as it is one more step in the recognition of what we have accomplished within the
sporting goods industry. The recent $50&nbsp;million placement has resulted in a flurry of acquisition
activity which we suspect will result in several accretive transactions completed in the months
ahead. As stated in previous releases, our primary goal is to deploy these funds to create a
minimum annual revenue base of $200&nbsp;million with a significant increase in operating and net income
levels.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 9pt">Collegiate Pacific is the nation&#146;s fastest growing manufacturer and supplier of sports equipment
primarily to the institutional and team dealer markets. The Company offers more than 4,000 products
to 200,000 prospective and existing customers. The Company distributes approximately 1.5&nbsp;million
catalogs annually and employs over 140 professional road salesmen.
