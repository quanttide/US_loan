<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;20, 2004, the Compensation Committee of the Board of
Directors of The J. Jill Group, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) adopted The J. Jill Group,
Inc. 2005 Incentive Compensation Plan (the &#147;2005 Incentive Plan&#148;) and The J.
Jill Group, Inc. 2005 Spring Season Supplemental Executive Bonus Plan (the &#147;Spring
2005 Executive Bonus Plan&#148;).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the 2005 Incentive Plan, certain employees of the Company,
including its executive officers, are eligible to receive bonuses for the
Spring 2005 season and the Fall 2005 season if the Company&#146;s earnings per share
for the applicable season before payment of any bonuses for the season under
the 2005 Incentive Plan (&#147;Preliminary Incentive Plan Earnings Per Share&#148;)
exceed its targeted earnings per share for the season.&#160; The amount of the bonus for each
participating employee for each season depends on the amount by which the
Company&#146;s Preliminary Incentive Plan Earnings Per Share for the season exceed
its targeted earnings per share for the season, the bonus percentage assigned
to the employee by the Board of Directors or the Compensation Committee (in the
case of employees at the level of Operating Vice President and above) or the
President and Chief Executive Officer (in the case of all other employees
selected for participation in the 2005 Incentive Plan by the President and
Chief Executive Officer) and the base salary paid to the employee for the
season.&#160;&#160; Under the 2005 Incentive Plan,
the maximum bonuses payable to the Company&#146;s executive officers range from 80%
of his or her base salary for the applicable season for Executive Vice
Presidents to 100% of his base salary for the applicable season for the
President and Chief Executive Officer.&#160;&#160;
A copy of the 2005 Incentive Plan is attached as Exhibit 10.1 to this
Current Report on Form 8-K.</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the Spring 2005 Executive Bonus Plan, the executive officers of
the Company are eligible to receive supplemental bonuses for the Spring 2005
season if the Company&#146;s earnings per share for the season before payment of any
bonuses under the Spring 2005 Executive Bonus Plan but after payment of any
bonuses for the season under the 2005 Incentive Plan (&#147;Preliminary Executive
Plan Earnings Per Share&#148;) exceed its targeted earnings per share for the season
by at least $0.05 per share.&#160; The amount
of the bonus for each executive officer depends on the amount by which the
Company&#146;s Preliminary Executive Plan Earnings Per Share for the Spring season
exceed its targeted earnings per share for the season, the bonus percentage
assigned to the executive officer by the Board of Directors or the Compensation
Committee and the base salary paid to the executive officer for the
season.&#160; Under the Spring 2005 Executive
Bonus Plan, the maximum supplemental bonuses payable to the Company&#146;s executive
officers range from 50% of his or her base salary for the Spring season for
Executive Vice Presidents to 100% of his base salary for the Spring season for
the President and Chief Executive Officer.&#160;
A copy of the Spring 2005 Executive Bonus Plan is attached as Exhibit
10.2 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.</font></p>

<h1 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Purpose</font></u></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">This 2005 Incentive
Compensation Plan (the &#147;<u>Incentive Compensation</u>  <u>Plan</u>&#148;) is designed
to provide financial reward to employees of The J. Jill Group, Inc. (</font>the &#147;<u>Company</u>&#148;) for their contribution toward the
attainment of the Company&#146;s financial goals for the fiscal year ending December
31, 2005 (&#147;<u>FY 2005</u>&#148;).</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h1 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Participation</font></u></b></h1>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>A
&#147;<u>Participating Employee</u>&#148; shall mean each of the following employees of
the Company:&#160; (i) the President and Chief
Executive Officer; (ii) any Executive Vice President; (iii) any Senior Vice
President; (iv) any Vice President; (v) any Operating Vice President; and (vi)
any employee below the level of Operating Vice President who is selected for
participation in this Incentive Compensation Plan by the President and Chief
Executive Officer.&#160; No employee below the
level of Operating Vice President shall be entitled to participate in this
Incentive Compensation Plan unless and until selected to do so by the President
and CEO.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>No
Participating Employee shall be entitled to receive a bonus under this
Incentive Compensation Plan unless the Participating Employee satisfies the
eligibility criteria set forth below.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h2 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Eligibility
Criteria</font></u></b></h2>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>A
Participating Employee who is in performance counseling at any time during a
performance period will not be eligible for a bonus for the performance period.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>To
be eligible to receive a bonus for a performance period, a Participating
Employee must be actively employed by the Company for a minimum of three months
in the performance period.&#160; Time on leave
does not count as active employment.&#160;
Employment by a subsidiary of the Company shall be considered employment
by the Company for purposes of this Incentive Compensation Plan.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>To
be eligible to receive a bonus for a performance period, a Participating
Employee must be employed on the last day of the performance period.</p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Definitions:</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>If

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>If

the Participating Employee&#146;s Maximum Bonus for the performance period times the

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">BONUS
PLAN</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h1 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Purpose</font></u></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">This 2005 Spring Season
Supplemental Executive Bonus Plan (the &#147;<u>Supplemental Bonus Plan</u>&#148;) is
designed to provide supplemental financial reward to senior executives of The
J. Jill Group, Inc. (</font>the &#147;<u>Company</u>&#148;) for their contribution toward the
Company&#146;s exceeding its financial goals for the two fiscal quarters ending June
25, 2005 (the &#147;Performance Period&#148;).</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h1 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Participation</font></u></b></h1>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>A
&#147;<u>Participating Executive</u>&#148; shall mean each of the following employees of
the Company:&#160; (i) the President and Chief
Executive Officer; and (ii) any Executive Vice President.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>No
Participating Executive shall be entitled to receive a bonus under this
Supplemental Bonus Plan unless the Participating Executive satisfies the
eligibility criteria set forth below.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h2 style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:auto;text-decoration:underline;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Eligibility
Criteria</font></u></b></h2>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>A
Participating Executive who is in performance counseling at any time during the
Performance Period will not be eligible for a bonus for the Performance Period.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>To
be eligible to receive a bonus for the Performance Period, a Participating
Executive must be actively employed by the Company for a minimum of three
months in the Performance Period.&#160; Time
on leave does not count as active employment.&#160;
Employment by a subsidiary of the Company shall be considered employment
by the Company for purposes of this Supplemental Bonus Plan.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>To
be eligible to receive a bonus for the Performance Period, a Participating
Executive must be employed on the last day of the Performance Period.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Nothing in this

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Payments</font></u></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Bonus payments under this

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Calculation of Supplemental Bonus
Payment</font></u></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Definitions:</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>&#147;<u>Earnings
Per Share Target</u>&#148; for the Performance Period means the Company&#146;s fully
diluted earnings per share target for the Performance Period as set forth in
the Company&#146;s 2005 Financial Plan.</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>&#147;<u>Supplemental
Bonus Earnings Per Share Percentage</u>&#148; for the Performance Period means a
fraction, the numerator of which is the Company&#146;s Supplemental Bonus
Preliminary Earnings Per Share for the Performance Period minus the Company&#146;s
Earnings Per Share Target for the Performance Period and the denominator of
which is $0.25, expressed as a percentage.&#160;
</p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>The
&#147;<u>Supplemental Bonus Percentage</u>&#148; for a Participating Executive shall be a
percentage assigned to the Participating Executive for the purpose of
calculating the Participating Executive&#146;s bonus, if any, under this Supplemental
Bonus Plan.&#160; Supplemental Bonus
Percentages shall be determined by the Board of Directors or the Compensation
Committee of the Board of Directors. </p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>&#147;<u>Supplemental
Bonus Preliminary Earnings Per Share</u>&#148; for the Performance Period means the
amount the Company&#146;s fully diluted earnings per share for the Performance
Period would be if no bonuses under this Supplemental Bonus Plan were paid for
the Performance Period (but taking into account payment of bonuses for the
Performance Period, if any, under the Company&#146;s 2005&nbsp;Incentive
Compensation Plan). </p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Each
&#147;<u>Supplemental Bonus Unit</u>&#148; for a Participating Executive means a
Participating Executive&#146;s Supplemental Bonus Percentage times the base salary
paid to him or her for the Performance Period.</p>
