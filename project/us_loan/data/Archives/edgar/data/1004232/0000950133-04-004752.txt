<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;2.01. Dissolution and final distribution</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;CYCH, Inc., f/k/a CyberCash, Inc. (&#147;CYCH&#148;) filed a certificate of dissolution in accordance
with Section&nbsp;275 of the Delaware General Corporation Law. Such dissolution was deemed to have
occurred and was authorized by the Order Confirming Debtor&#146;s First Amended Liquidating Plan of
Reorganization entered on November&nbsp;7, 2001, effective November&nbsp;18, 2001 by the United States
Bankruptcy Court for the District of Delaware (Case No.&nbsp;01-0622 (MFW) (the &#147;Plan&#148;).


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;CYCH is liquidating assets and distributing proceeds to former shareholders in accordance with
the Plan. On December&nbsp;30, 2004, CYCH will distribute, from available cash, a liquidating dividend
totaling $0.0125 (1.25 cents) per share to holders of rights to CYCH&#146;s liquidating dividends
(&#147;Rights Holders&#148;). The distribution will be to Rights Holders on record as of December&nbsp;28,
2004. CYCH believes that this distribution represents its final liquidating dividend.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Certificate of Dissolution is attached to this Form 8-K as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1.
