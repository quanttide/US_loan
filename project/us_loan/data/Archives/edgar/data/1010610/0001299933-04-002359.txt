<P align="center" style="font-size: 9pt"><FONT style="font-size: 20pt"><B>TLC</B><B><I>Vision </I></B><B>Announces Stock Buy-Back Program</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 20pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>St. Louis, MO, December&nbsp;20, 2004: </B>TLC Vision Corporation (NASDAQ:TLCV; TSX:TLC), North
America&#146;s premier eye care services company, announced today that it intends, subject to regulatory
approval, to purchase up to 2,000,000 shares (or up to 3%) of its 68,153,000 common shares
currently outstanding.</FONT>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Upon approval of the buy-back from the Toronto Stock Exchange, the purchases may take place from
time to time, depending on market conditions, through the facilities of the NASDAQ National Market
and the Toronto Stock Exchange. The prices which TLC<I>Vision </I>will pay for any common shares will be
the market price of the shares at the time of acquisition.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Jim Wachtman, TLC<I>Vision</I>&#146;s CEO, stated &#147;With a healthy balance sheet and strong cash flows, we are
well positioned to invest for growth as well as continue our commitment to enhancing shareholder
value. The Executive Team and the Board of Directors believes that TLC<I>Vision </I>shares are
undervalued, and that this stock buy-back represents a good use of the corporation&#146;s funds and will
benefit its shareholders.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">About TLC<I>Vision</I>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">TLC<I>Vision </I>is North America&#146;s premier eye care services company. The key drivers of TLC<I>Vision</I>&#145;s
strategy are our affiliated network of thousands of eye doctors, proven corporate and consumer
marketing and education programs, and access to state of the art clinical technologies. We are
supported by a strong operations management culture and superior information technology. More
information about the company can be found on the TLC<I>Vision </I>web site at www.tlcv.com.
