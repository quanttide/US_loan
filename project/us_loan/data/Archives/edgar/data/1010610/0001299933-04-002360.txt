<P align="center" style="font-size: 9pt"><FONT style="font-size: 20pt"><B>TLC</B><B><I>Vision </I></B><B>Partners With Doctors</B></FONT>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 20pt"><B>In Leading Houston Ambulatory Surgery Center</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 20pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>St. Louis, MO, December&nbsp;20, 2004 </B>&#150; TLC Vision Corporation (NASDAQ:TLCV; TSX:TLC), North
America&#146;s premier eye care services company, announced today it&#146;s subsidiary, OR Partners, has
become the single largest shareholder in Summit Ambulatory Surgery Center, a 12-partner facility
located in Houston, Texas.</FONT>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">This is the fifth ambulatory surgery center (&#147;ASC&#148;) OR Partners and TLC<I>Vision </I>have acquired in the
last 16&nbsp;months, with several ASC deals in development. OR Partners acquires, develops, and manages
ambulatory surgery centers in partnership with physicians. These centers provide out-patient
surgery services in a less institutional atmosphere than can be achieved in a hospital setting and
appeal to doctors seeking improved efficiencies and the highest degree in patient satisfaction.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Led by John J. Garrrett, MD, a cataract surgeon with more than 30&nbsp;years experience, the Summit
Ambulatory Surgery Center is a highly successful facility featuring two operating rooms and
performing nearly 5000 ophthalmic and pain management procedures annually. Dr.&nbsp;Garrett said, &#147;OR
Partners is well capitalized, managed by a team of experienced professionals and committed to
achieve results. Most importantly, from a physician&#146;s perspective, they understand that patients
are the number one priority. I am very excited about being associated with OR Partners, improving
operational efficiencies and growing the center.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Mary T. Green, MD, one of 12 partners in Summit Ambulatory Surgery Center, has also worked with the
laser refractive division of TLC<I>Vision </I>for 3&nbsp;years. Dr.&nbsp;Green said, &#147;I know the quality of service
this company provides and the commitment to their doctor customers. When I heard about the OR
Partners subsidiary, and the great work they were doing in ASCs, it just made sense to continue the
relationships already built with TLC<I>Vision</I>.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;Through our current network of over 1100 ophthalmologists, we will continue to acquire or develop
similar centers in association with some of the country&#146;s most prominent surgeons,&#148; commented Jim
Wachtman, TLC<I>Vision</I>&#146;s CEO. &#147;This approach will broaden our eye care services strategy and be
immediately accretive to TLC<I>Vision&#146;</I>s cash flow and profitability.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">About TLC<I>Vision</I>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">TLC<I>Vision </I>is North America&#146;s premier eye care services company. The key drivers of TLC<I>Vision</I>&#145;s
strategy are our affiliated network of thousands of eye doctors, proven corporate and consumer
marketing and education programs, and access to state of the art clinical technologies. We are
supported by a strong operations management culture and superior information technology. More
information about the company can be found on the TLC<I>Vision </I>web site at www.tlcv.com.
