<H1 align=center><FONT face="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" size=2>Tellabs acquires Vinci Systems</FONT></H1>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>Naperville, Ill. &#151;
</B>Tellabs today acquired Vinci Systems, a privately held developer of optical networking
terminals (ONTs) for Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) access systems. Under the terms of the
agreement, Tellabs will pay up to $52.5 million in cash and restricted Tellabs stock. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>The move brings Tellabs a family of
third-generation ONTs that complements the Tellabs FiberDirect<SUP>SM</SUP> solution.
Vinci Systems, a start-up based in Vienna, Va., was founded in 2002 and has demonstrated
interoperability of its ONT products with the industry&#146;s leading Optical Line
Terminal (OLT) vendors. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&#147;This acquisition further
illustrates our commitment to expanding into the growing FTTx market,&#148; said Carl
DeWilde, executive vice president of access products at Tellabs. &#147;Vinci&#146;s ONT
technology enables us to deliver a more cost-effective fiber access solution.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush" FSL="Default" -->

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>About Tellabs</B><BR>
Tellabs delivers technology that
transforms the way the world communicates&#153;. Tellabs experts design, develop, deploy
and support wireless and wired network solutions. The company&#146;s comprehensive
broadband portfolio enables carriers in more than 100 countries to succeed in the new
competitive environment. Tellabs is part of the NASDAQ-100 Index. <U>www.tellabs.com</U>. </FONT></P>
