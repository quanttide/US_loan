Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

     On December 21, 2004, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (the “Compensation Committee”) of Alnylam Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (“Alnylam”) granted to John M. Maraganore, Ph.D., the President and Chief Executive Officer of Alnylam, a stock option (the “Option”) to purchase up to 250,000 shares of common stock of Alnylam at an exercise price of $7.47 per share under its 2004 Stock Incentive Plan. The Option vests upon Alnylam’s attainment of a corporate goal. The Compensation Committee will determine whether the corporate goal has been attained and the number of shares as to which the Option vests upon such attainment.

     A form of nonstatutory stock option agreement that Alnylam uses under its 2004 Stock Incentive Plan is attached as Exhibit 10.1 hereto.
