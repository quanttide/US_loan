<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><u>Item&nbsp;5.02</u>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of
Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company received notice of resignation from its former Chief Financial Officer, Myles
McCormick, on December&nbsp;13, 2004, as previously disclosed in the Company&#146;s Current Report on Form
8-K dated December&nbsp;13, 2004, filed with the SEC on December&nbsp;15, 2004. Mr.&nbsp;McCormick&#146;s resignation
became effective on December&nbsp;17, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;17, 2004, the Board of Directors of the Gymboree Corporation appointed Blair Lambert to
serve as its Chief Financial Officer. Mr.&nbsp;Lambert will also serve as the Company&#146;s Chief Operating
Officer, as previously announced. The Company anticipates that Mr.&nbsp;Lambert&#146;s employment will
commence effective January&nbsp;10, 2005.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Although the Company has not entered into a written employment agreement with Mr.&nbsp;Lambert, on
December&nbsp;6, 2004 the Company agreed to a compensation arrangement with Mr.&nbsp;Lambert. Effective when
Mr.&nbsp;Lambert commences employment as the Company&#146;s Chief Operating Officer and Chief Financial
Officer, Mr.&nbsp;Lambert will receive the compensation previously disclosed in Item&nbsp;1.01 of the
Company&#146;s Current Report on Form 8-K dated December&nbsp;6, 2004, which was filed with the SEC on
December&nbsp;8, 2004, is incorporated herein by reference. Mr.&nbsp;Lambert will not receive any additional
compensation with respect to his employment as the Company&#146;s Chief Financial Officer.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The other information about Mr.&nbsp;Lambert disclosed in Item&nbsp;5.02 of the Company&#146;s Current Report on
Form 8-K dated December&nbsp;6, 2004, which was filed with the SEC on December&nbsp;8, 2004, is incorporated
herein by reference.
