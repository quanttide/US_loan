<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 11pt"><B>STOCK OPTION AGREEMENT</B></FONT>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>(Non-Qualified Option &#150; Immediate Vesting &#151; Restricted Stock)</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>THIS AGREEMENT GRANTS A </B><U><B>NON-QUALIFIED STOCK OPTION</B></U><BR>
<U><B>RESALE</U> OF STOCK ISSUED ON EXERCISE OF THIS OPTION WILL BE </B><U><B>RESTRICTED</B></U>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">Dear &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(&#147;Grantee&#148;):


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">In view of your substantial contributions toward the achievement of the business goals and
objectives of DNB Financial Corporation (the &#147;Corporation&#148;) and DNB First, National Association
(the &#147;Bank&#148;) and the expectation of your future contributions, the Board of Directors of the
Corporation is pleased to award you an option to purchase shares of the Common Stock of the
Corporation pursuant to the 1995 Stock Option Plan of DNB Financial Corporation (As amended and
restated, effective as of April&nbsp;27, 2004) (the &#147;Plan&#148;). This letter will serve as the stock option
agreement between you and the Corporation. The option awarded to you is subject to the following
terms.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>1.&nbsp;NUMBER OF SHARES: </B>You are awarded an option to purchase a total of &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;shares of the Common
Stock of the Corporation, subject to the terms, conditions and restrictions set forth in this
Agreement and the Plan.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>2.&nbsp;RESALE RESTRICTION ON SHARES:</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(a)&nbsp;When issued, your resale or other disposition of these shares will be restricted for
<U><B>two (2)&nbsp;years from the date the Common Stock is issued to you</B></U>, on the following terms and
will bear the following legend:


<P align="left" style="margin-left:5%; margin-right:5%; font-size: 11pt">&#147;Sale, assignment, pledge, gift, or any other disposition, alienation or
encumbrance of the shares represented by this certificate are restricted and
prohibited until &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;pursuant to the terms of a Stock Option
Agreement dated December&nbsp;22, 2004, between DNB Financial Corporation and the
holder of the shares named on this certificate, which may be examined at the
principal office of the Company, and such shares may be sold, transferred,
assigned, pledged, given or otherwise disposed of, alienated or encumbered
only upon compliance with the terms of that Agreement.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(b)&nbsp;Notwithstanding the foregoing, upon and after issuance of the shares on your exercise of
this option, but subject nevertheless to any other resale restrictions applicable to these shares
(including without limitation any imposed if the shares are not registered under the Securities Act
of 1933) you will be authorized to resell these shares free of the restriction set forth in
subsection (a)&nbsp;of this Section solely for the purpose of paying or reimbursing the Corporation for
federal, state or local withholding taxes applicable to your exercise of any stock options relating
to shares of Common Stock of the Corporation.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>3.&nbsp;TYPE OF OPTION: </B>The option awarded to you is a <U><B>Non-Qualified Option</B></U> as that term is
defined in the Plan.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>4.&nbsp;EXERCISE PRICE: </B>The shares may be purchased upon your exercise of this option for the price of
$&nbsp;&nbsp;per share


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>5.&nbsp;DATE OF GRANT OF AWARD: </B>The <U><B>Grant Date</B></U> of the award of this option is December&nbsp;22,
2004, which is also the date of this Agreement.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>6.&nbsp;STATED EXPIRATION DATE: </B>Unless earlier terminated as explained below, the option awarded to you
expires (with respect to any number of shares subject to this option not previously exercised) on
the 10th anniversary of the Grant Date stated above. This is the <U><B>Stated Expiration Date</B></U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>7.&nbsp;DATE OPTION BECOMES EXERCISEABLE; LOSS OF OPTION IN CERTAIN CIRCUMSTANCES: </B>The stock option
awarded to you is exerciseable at any time after the Grant Date stated above. The stock option
remains exerciseable by you until the expiration of the option in accordance with the terms of this
Agreement and the terms of the Plan.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>8.&nbsp;EXERCISE OF OPTION: </B>You may exercise the option awarded to you from time to time as provided
above by delivering to the Corporation all of the following:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(a)&nbsp;Written notice of the exercise marked for the attention of the Secretary specifying the
number of whole shares in respect of which you are exercising the option.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(b)&nbsp;Payment of the exercise price for such shares in any of the following forms: (i)&nbsp;cash,
(ii)&nbsp;certified check payable to the order of the Corporation, (iii)&nbsp;shares of Common Stock of the
Corporation already owned by you, (iv)&nbsp;shares of Common Stock of the Corporation you are entitled
to receive as a result of stock option exercises that you are entitled to make for such purpose,
but only if those options are &#147;in the money,&#148; or (v)&nbsp;any combination of the foregoing.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(c)&nbsp;Payment of any federal, state and local withholding taxes required in respect of such
exercise in any combination of the forms of payment described in (b)&nbsp;above.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(d)&nbsp;Only if the shares to be acquired upon exercise have not been registered under the
Securities Act of 1933 (the 1933 Act ) :


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 7%">(i)&nbsp;If required by the Board of Directors of the Corporation, a written statement that you are
purchasing the shares for investment and not with a view toward their distribution and will not
sell or transfer any shares received upon the exercise of the option except in accordance with the
1933 Act and following the expiration of the second anniversary of the Exercise Date of the option
with respect to such shares;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 7%">(ii)&nbsp;If required by the Board of Directors of the Corporation, with such information as is
necessary for the Corporation to comply with securities laws.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">Notwithstanding the foregoing, Shares of Common Stock of the Corporation may only be applied
against the exercise price or to pay any federal, state or local withholding taxes to the extent
consistent with any restrictions applicable to such shares. If shares of Common Stock of the
Corporation are to be applied in whole or partial payment of the exercise price or any withholding
taxes, they shall be applied at their fair market value (as determined under the Plan) on the
Exercise Date.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">Upon receipt of the documents and payments listed above, the Corporation will issue you a
certificate for the number of shares with respect to which you have exercised the option.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>9.&nbsp;EXERCISE DATE: </B>The date on which the Corporation receives the documents specified above in
complete and otherwise acceptable form and the payments specified above will be treated as the
Exercise Date with respect to your exercise of the stock option.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>10.&nbsp;NON-ASSIGNABILITY OF OPTION: </B>Except as provided by the Plan, the option awarded to you is
exerciseable only by you. The option may not be transferred, assigned, pledged as security or
hypothecated in any other way and shall not be subject to execution, attachment or similar process
even if you agree with someone else that it will be, except that if you die while still employed
with the Corporation or the Bank, your estate or the person who acquires the right to exercise the
Stock Option upon your death by bequest or inheritance may exercise your option. Upon any attempt
by you to transfer, assign, pledge, hypothecate or otherwise dispose of this option or of any
portion thereof or upon the levy of any execution, attachment or similar process on this option or
on any portion thereof, the option awarded to you will immediately expire with respect to the
number of shares not exercised prior to such event.

subject to this option or of any rights of a holder of such shares unless and until the shares are
issued to you as evidenced by stock certificates.

Therefore, none of the rights awarded to you by this letter affect, in any way, your employment or
service relationship with the Corporation or the Bank.

your separation from service as a Director of the Corporation and the Bank, if applicable, the
unexercised portion of this option will terminate according to the following terms:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(a)&nbsp;If you terminate or separate on account of death or disability or you terminate on account

<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(b)&nbsp;If the termination of your employment or separation from service as a Director is a

separation.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(c)&nbsp;If you terminate or separate by your own act, your option will terminate at the close of
business on the earlier of the Stated Expiration Date described above or on the ninetieth (90th)
day following the date of your termination or separation.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt; text-indent: 3%">(d)&nbsp;If you terminate or separate for any other reason, your option will terminate at the close
of business on the earlier of the Stated Expiration Date described above or on the third (3rd)
