<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Proxim Corporation Updates Fourth Quarter 2004 Financial Guidance</B>



<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">SUNNYVALE, Calif., Dec. 29 &#151; Proxim Corporation (Nasdaq: PROX &#151; News), a global leader in
wireless networking equipment for Wi-Fi and wide area networks, today updated its financial
guidance for the fourth fiscal quarter of 2004. Proxim now expects fourth quarter 2004 revenue will
be in the range of $22&nbsp;million to $24&nbsp;million, compared to the $32&nbsp;million to $35&nbsp;million target
previously provided in October&nbsp;2004. Based on revenue in this range, Proxim expects to report a
non-GAAP or operating pro-forma loss per share in the range of ($.34) to ($.29) for the fourth
fiscal quarter of 2004, which compares to the target range previously provided for the quarter of
($.14) to ($.09). At this time, Proxim is unable to provide guidance on its projected GAAP loss per
share results.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">About Proxim



<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">Proxim&#174; Corporation is a global leader in wireless networking equipment for Wi-Fi and broadband
wireless networks. The company provides enterprise and service provider customers with wireless
solutions for the mobile enterprise, security and surveillance, last mile access, voice and data
backhaul, public hot spots, and metropolitan area networks. Product families include the
Award-winning ORiNOCO Wi-Fi products, Tsunami Ethernet bridges, and Lynx point-to-point digital
radios. Proxim is a principal member of the WiMAX Forum&#153; and a member of the Wi-Fi&#174; Alliance.
The company is publicly traded on the NASDAQ under the symbol PROX and is on the Web at
www.proxim.com.

<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">To supplement our consolidated financial statements presented on a GAAP basis, Proxim uses
