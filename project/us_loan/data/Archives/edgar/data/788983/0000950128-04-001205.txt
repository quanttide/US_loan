<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><U><B>PORTOLA PACKAGING REPORTS FIRST QUARTER RESULTS</B></U>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;SAN JOSE, CA&#151;December&nbsp;21, 2004 &#151; Portola Packaging, Inc. today reported results for its
first quarter of fiscal year 2005, ended November&nbsp;30, 2004. Sales were $62.8&nbsp;million compared to
$59.8&nbsp;million for the same quarter of the prior year. Portola had operating income of $1.7&nbsp;million
for the first quarter of fiscal year 2005, compared to operating income of $2.1&nbsp;million for the
first quarter of fiscal year 2004. Foreign exchange gain for the first quarter of fiscal 2005
totaled $2.0&nbsp;million as compared to a gain of $1.3&nbsp;million for the same period in fiscal 2004.
Portola reported a net loss of $1.6&nbsp;million for the first quarter of fiscal year 2005 compared to a
net loss of $1.0&nbsp;million for the same period of fiscal year 2004. <U> </U>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The increase in net loss of $0.6&nbsp;million is primarily attributed to delays in passing resin
increases to customers, competitive pressures in the US and UK markets in fiscal year 2004 that
adversely affected first quarter fiscal year 2005 prices, as well as higher interest expense. This
was partially offset by increased sales volume in Canada, lower selling, general, administrative
and research and development costs as well as a higher gain on foreign exchange.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;EBITDA<SUP>(a), (c) </SUP>decreased $0.2&nbsp;million to $7.7&nbsp;million in the first quarter of
fiscal year 2005 compared to $7.9&nbsp;million in the first quarter of fiscal year 2004. Adjusted
EBITDA<SUP>(b), (c)</SUP>, which excludes the effect of restructuring charges, (gains)&nbsp;losses on
sale of assets, one-time relocation costs, and warrant interest (income)&nbsp;expense, decreased to $7.8
million in the first quarter of fiscal 2005 compared to $8.2&nbsp;million in the first quarter of fiscal
2004.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">CONFERENCE CALL:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Portola Packaging, Inc. executives will hold a conference call to discuss the first quarter of
fiscal 2005 results. The conference call is scheduled for December&nbsp;22, 2004 at 10:00&nbsp;A.M. Pacific

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">ABOUT PORTOLA PACKAGING, INC:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Portola Packaging is a leading designer, manufacturer and marketer of tamper evident plastic
closures used in dairy, fruit juice, bottled water, sports drinks, institutional food products and
other non-carbonated beverage products. The Company also produces a wide variety of plastic
bottles for use in the dairy, water and juice industries, including various high density bottles,
as well as five-gallon polycarbonate water bottles. In addition, the Company designs, manufactures
and markets capping equipment for use in high speed bottling, filling and packaging production
lines. The Company is also engaged in the manufacture and sale of tooling and molds used in the
blow molding industry. For more information about Portola Packaging, visit the Company&#146;s web site
at <u>www.portpack.com</u>.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">ABOUT PORTOLA TECH INTERNATIONAL:


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Portola Tech International (&#147;PTI&#148;) is a leading manufacturer and marketer of plastic packaging
components to the cosmetic, fragrance and toiletries industry. PTI&#146;s capabilities include
injection and compression molding, thermal and ultraviolet metallizing, ultraviolet one coat spray
technologies, silk screening, hot stamping, lining and multiple component assembly. In addition to
offering the largest stock line of closures in the industry, with over 450 styles and sizes, PTI
has a complementary line of heavy wall PETG and polypropylene jars. For more information about
PTI, visit PTI&#146;s web site at <u>www.techindustries.com</u>.
