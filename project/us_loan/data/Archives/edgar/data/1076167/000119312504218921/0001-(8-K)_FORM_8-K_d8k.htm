Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On December 23, 2004, Adolor Corporation (the “Company”) reported top-line results from a Phase 3 clinical study (SB-767905/001), Study 001, of its product candidate alvimopan for the management of postoperative ileus. The study was conducted in Europe, Australia and New Zealand by GlaxoSmithKline, the Company’s collaborator on the worldwide development and commercialization of alvimopan. The press release issued by the Company concerning the announcement is attached to this Report as Exhibit 99.1.
