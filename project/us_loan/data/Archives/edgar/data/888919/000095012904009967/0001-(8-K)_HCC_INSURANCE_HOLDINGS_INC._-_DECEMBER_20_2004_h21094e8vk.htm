Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.

     On December 20, 2004, HCC Insurance Holdings, Inc. (“HCC”) completed the sale of 250,000 common shares of Argonaut Group, Inc. (“Argonaut”). The sale had the effect of reducing HCC’s investment in Argonaut shares to an aggregate 3,001,984 shares of common and mandatory convertible preferred stock, which represents less than 10% of Argonaut outstanding shares for insurance regulatory reporting purposes.

     The information contained herein shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference in any filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act.
