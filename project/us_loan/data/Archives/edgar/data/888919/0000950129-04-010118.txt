<P align="right" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>EXHIBIT 99.1</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>HCC CONTRIBUTES $75 MILLION CAPITAL TO INSCO<BR>
SUBSIDIARIES WITH OPTIMISTIC OUTLOOK FOR 2005</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">HOUSTON (December&nbsp;27, 2004) . . .<BR>
<B>HCC Insurance Holdings, Inc. (NYSE symbol: HCC) </B>announced today that the Company had utilized a
significant part of the proceeds from its recently completed equity offering, in making a $75
million capital contribution to its insurance company subsidiaries.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Stephen L. Way, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, said, &#147;Despite the perceived generally
negative industry analysis we are hearing from reinsurers and sell side analysts alike, we remain
very optimistic about our specialty lines of business and the outlook for 2005.&#148; Mr.&nbsp;Way added, &#147;We
expect our net premium to grow significantly next year due to acquisitions made in 2004 and less
ceded reinsurance.&#148;

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">HCC is an international insurance holding company and a leading specialty insurance group since
1974, based in Houston, Texas with offices across the USA and in Bermuda, England and Spain. HCC
has assets of more than $5.5&nbsp;billion, shareholders&#146; equity of over $1.25&nbsp;billion and is rated AA
(Very Strong) by Standard &#038; Poor&#146;s and A&#043; (Superior) by A.M. Best Company.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">For more information, visit our website at <U>www.hcch.com</U>.
