<P align="center" style="font-size: 11pt"><FONT style="font-size: 14pt"><U><B>WPT ENTERPRISES LANDS DEAL WITH DISCOVERY&#146;S &#147;TRAVEL AND ADVENTURE NETWORK&#148; FOR WORLD POKER<BR>
TOUR&#174; DISTRIBUTION IN THE CARIBBEAN AND LATIN AMERICA</B></U></FONT>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 14pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><U><B><I>The Addition of the Two Regions Brings International Reach to 57 Countries </I></B></U></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><FONT style="font-size: 11pt"><B>West Hollywood, CA (December&nbsp;16, 2004) &#151; WPT Enterprises, Inc. </B>(Nasdaq: WPTE) announced today
that it has inked a deal to widen its footprint to nearly 60 countries with the distribution of its
<B>WORLD POKER TOUR&#174; (WPT) </B>show to the Caribbean and Latin America through Discovery Network&#146;s &#147;Travel
and Adventure Network.&#148; The agreement advances the relationship between the two companies, which
together launched the wildly successful show in the United States on Discovery&#146;s Travel Channel.
Discovery will air the <B>WORLD POKER TOUR </B>in Latin America on Wednesday nights at 9 pm, bringing the
<B>WPT </B>brand into more than 10&nbsp;million homes. The <B>WPT </B>continues to be seen in the U.S. Wednesday
nights on the Travel Channel at 9 pm ET/PT.</FONT>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">In the U.S., the <B>WORLD POKER TOUR</B>, the show that reinvented poker as a televised spectator sport,
will broadcast 16 new tournaments filmed at leading casinos and exotic locales from Las Vegas to
Paris, beginning with the launch of its Third Season on March&nbsp;3, 2005. Discovery&#146;s &#147;Travel and
Adventure Network&#148; will be airing Season One in the Caribbean and Latin America beginning on
December&nbsp;28, 2004. .


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">&#147;This agreement with Discovery more than triples our previous distribution and expands our
relationship with this visionary company,&#148; said Steve Lipscomb, President of <B>WPTE</B>. &#147;Adding Latin
America and the Caribbean to our portfolio will give the <B>WORLD POKER TOUR </B>a chance to grow new fans
among an enormous potential audience. This addition truly enables us to extend our brand globally.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">The deal will open the door for the <B>WORLD POKER TOUR </B>to air in: Anguilla, Antigua &#038; Barbuda,
Argentina, Aruba, Bahamas, Barbados, Belize, Bermuda, Bolivia, Brazil, British Virgin Islands,
Cayman Islands, Chile, Columbia, Costa Rica, Cuba, Dominica, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, El
Salvador, Falkland Islands, French Guyana, Grenada, Guadeloupe, Guatemala, Haiti, Honduras,
Jamaica, Martinique, Mexico, Montserrat, Netherlands Antilles, Nicaragua, Panama, Paraguay, Peru,
Puerto Rico, St. Kitts &#038; Nevis, St. Lucia, St Vincent &#038; Grenadines, Suriname, Trinidad &#038; Tobago,
Turks &#038; Caicos Islands, U.S. Virgin Islands, Uruguay and Venezuela.


<P align="center" style="font-size: 11pt"><B>(more)</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">The <B>WORLD POKER TOUR </B>is currently broadcasted in Australia, Sweden, the Philippines, South Korea,
India, Denmark, Greenland, Iceland, the Faroe Islands, the U.K. and Canada.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">International distribution is handled by Alfred Haber Distribution, Inc., the world&#146;s largest
distributor of U.S. network specials (from networks such as CBS, ABC, NBC and FOX). Haber currently
represents more than 1,000 hours of programming from 200 of the world&#146;s most creative television
producers, spanning the genres of award shows, music events, documentaries, variety specials,
wildlife programs and holiday celebrations, as well as series, mini-series and made-for-television
motion pictures. The company can be reached at (201)&nbsp;224-8000 or via their website:
<U>www.alfredhaber.com</U>.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt"><FONT style="font-size: 10pt"><U><B>About WPT Enterprises, Inc.</B></U>
<BR>
WPT Enterprises, Inc. (Nasdaq: WPTE) is a media and entertainment company engaged in the creation
of branded entertainment through the development, production and marketing of televised programming
based on poker and other gaming themes. To date, operations have principally revolved around the
creation of the World Poker Tour brand through the production and licensing of a television series
exhibited on the Travel Channel that is based on a circuit of previously-established high-stakes
poker tournaments that we have affiliated under the &#147;World Poker Tour&#148; brand. WPT Enterprises, Inc.
is a majority owned subsidiary of Lakes Entertainment, Inc. (Nasdaq: LACO). Photos and media
information can be found online at: <U>www.worldpokertour.com.</U></FONT>
