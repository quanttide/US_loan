<P ALIGN=LEFT><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Beach First National
Announces Second Hilton Head Island Office </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hilton Head Island, SC December 27,
2004 &#150; Beach First National Bank today announced that it will open its second office
on Hilton Head Island in early January. Located at The Village at Wexford at 1000 William
Hilton Parkway, near the Island&#146;s south end, the new office joins the bank&#146;s
first location that opened in February 2003 in the Pineland Station Office Building. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Paul Walter, Beach First National
vice president and regional manager, said, &#147;We have always been pleased with the
reception our Pineland Station office received from both business and personal customers
in Hilton Head. A second location near the Island&#146;s south end will help make Beach
First&#146;s community banking services even more convenient to Islanders. Local
management and local decision making are the key components of our banking philosophy and
we have a fine local staff in place at this office.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Several staff members have been hired
in anticipation of the new office&#146;s opening. Kenny Maguire will serve as vice
president and commercial lender, while Janet Hunter has been appointed assistant vice
president and branch manager. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Maguire, a resident of Hilton Head
since 1988, has nearly 20 years of banking experience. He most recently served Lighthouse
Community Bank, now SunTrust, as a vice president for commercial lending. He is a graduate
of Emory University. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Hunter has 27 years of banking
experience in branch management and operations and most recently served Lighthouse
Community Bank as an operations manager. She is a graduate of Montclair State College with
a B.A. degree and has resided in Hilton Head for 22 years. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Lv 0-TNR" FSL="Project" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Beach First National is $242 million
financial institution headquartered in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. Beach First operates
offices in Myrtle Beach, Surfside Beach, North Myrtle Beach, Pawleys Island, and Hilton
Head Island, South Carolina. Beach First offers a full line of banking products and
services, including NetTeller internet banking with PowerPay. The company&#146;s stock
trades under the symbol BFNB and its website is beachfirst.com. </FONT></P>
