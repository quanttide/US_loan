                             ITEM 8.01--OTHER EVENTS

         Essential Innovations Technology Corp. ("Essential") has terminated its
primary offering of securities under the registration statement on Form SB-2
(SEC File No. 333-106839), which was declared effective by the Securities and
Exchange Commission on August 6, 2004. Essential has not sold any shares in that
offering. The offering of shares by the selling stockholders identified in that
registration statement will continue.
