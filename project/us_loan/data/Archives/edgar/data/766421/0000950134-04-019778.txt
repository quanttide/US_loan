<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Item&nbsp;4.02 Non-Reliance on Previously Issued Financial Statements or a Related Audit Report or
Completed Interim Review</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">In December&nbsp;2004, the Company discovered a clerical error in its calculation of deferred Mileage
Plan revenue that existed as of September&nbsp;30, 2004. Management discussed the issue with its Audit
Committee and independent auditor, KPMG. To provide investors with timely information, management
and the Audit Committee determined on December&nbsp;20, 2004 to file
a Form&nbsp;10-Q/A for the quarter ended
September&nbsp;30, 2004 to reflect the impact of this item to the Company&#146;s third quarter and year to
date reported condensed consolidated financial statements rather than record the adjustment in the
fourth quarter. As described in greater detail in the Form&nbsp;10-Q/A (also filed today), the revision
increases total Mileage Plan liability and deferred revenue balance
from $379.2&nbsp;million to $387.6&nbsp;million and results in an $8.4&nbsp;million (pretax)&nbsp;decrease to other revenues for the third quarter
and nine months ended September&nbsp;30, 2004.
