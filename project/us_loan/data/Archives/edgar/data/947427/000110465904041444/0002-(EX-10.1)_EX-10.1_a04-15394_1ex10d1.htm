AXS-One Inc.

1998 Stock Option Plan

(as amended by the stockholders on June 13, 2001 and June 9, 2004)

 

ARTICLE I

PURPOSE

 

The purpose of the AXS-One Inc. 1998 Stock Option Plan (the “Plan”) is to enhance the profitability and value of AXS-One Inc. (the “Company”) and its Affiliates for the benefit of the Company’s stockholders by enabling the Company:  (i) to offer employees and Consultants of the Company and its Affiliates, stock based incentives and other equity interests in the Company, thereby creating a means to raise the level of stock ownership by employees and Consultants in order to attract, retain and reward such individuals and strengthen the mutuality of interests between such individuals and the Company’s stockholders, and (ii) to make equity based awards to Non-Employee Directors of the Company thereby attracting, retaining and rewarding such Non-Employee Directors and strengthening the mutuality of interests between such individuals and the Company’s stockholders.
