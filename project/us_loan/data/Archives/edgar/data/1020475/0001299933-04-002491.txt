<P ALIGN="LEFT">
<FONT SIZE="2">
<B>
	Item 4.01. Changes in Registrant's Certifying Accountant.
</B>
</FONT>
</P>
<P ALIGN="LEFT">
<FONT SIZE="2">
On December 28, 2004, Pacific Biometrics, Inc. entered into an engagement agreement with Williams & Webster P.S. ("Williams & Webster") to serve as the Company&#x2019;s new independent registered public accountants to audit the Company&#x2019;s financial statements for its fiscal year ending June 30, 2005.  Williams & Webster will commence its engagement with its review of the Company&#x2019;s financial statements for the quarter ending December 31, 2004.<br><br>The decision to engage Williams & Webster as the Company&#x2019;s independent registered public accountants was approved by the Company&#x2019;s audit committee.  The engagement of Williams & Webster was made following the resignation on December 6, 2004 of Grant Thornton LLP as the Company&#x2019;s prior independent registered public accounting firm.<br><br>During the Company&#x2019;s two most recent fiscal years and the subsequent interim period through December 28, 2004, the Company has not consulted with Williams & Webster regarding any matter or
 event.<br><br>
