<P align="center" style="font-size: 9pt"><FONT style="font-size: 18pt"><B>Xenonics Reports Fiscal 2004 Results<BR>
and Restates Fiscal 2003 Loss Per Share</B></FONT>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 18pt"><FONT style="font-size: 15pt"><U><I>Management Expects Continued Growth in Fiscal 2005</I></U></FONT>



<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 15pt; text-indent: 1%"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt">CARLSBAD, CALIFORNIA &#151; December&nbsp;21, 2004 &#151; <B>XENONICS HOLDINGS, INC.
(NASDAQ:XNNC) </B>today reported substantially higher revenue and net income for fiscal 2004
compared to fiscal 2003, in line with the guidance provided on December&nbsp;7, 2004, as the
company&#146;s patented NightHunter high-intensity, portable illumination systems gained
traction in the marketplace.</FONT>


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 1%">&#147;We expect continued revenue and earnings growth in fiscal 2005 as we pursue
opportunities for our NightHunter products in defense, homeland security, law enforcement,
fire, emergency, search and rescue, and related commercial and recreational markets. We
also are excited about the growth potential of the new products we plan to launch in 2005
that will address new markets for our illumination and night vision technologies,&#148; said
Chairman and CEO Alan Magerman. &#147;We are building value for Xenonics&#146; shareholders with
proprietary products targeted at large markets and a commitment to excellence in every
aspect of our operations.&#148;


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt"><B>Fiscal 2004 Results and Restated Fiscal 2003 Loss Per Share</B>


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 1%">For the twelve months ended September&nbsp;30, 2004, revenue increased to $11,927,000 from
$1,580,000 for fiscal 2003, a nine-month &#147;stub&#148; year due to a change in fiscal year end.
Net income for fiscal 2004 increased to $1,476,000, or $0.09 per diluted share. This
compares to a net loss for fiscal 2003 of ($1,963,000), or ($0.29) per share. The net
loss per share for fiscal 2003 has been restated to reflect a change in the number of
basic and diluted shares used in the calculation.


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 1%">Net cash provided by operating activities for fiscal 2004 increased to $1,670,000.
This compares to net cash used in operating activities of ($1,174,000) for fiscal 2003.
At September&nbsp;30, 2004, the company had cash of $3,269,000, a current ratio of 2.9, and no
debt.


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt"><B>Operations Review</B>


<P align="left" style="margin-left:3%; margin-right:2%; font-size: 12pt; text-indent: 1%">&#147;Xenonics shipped nearly 5,200 NightHunter systems in fiscal 2004, primarily to
customers in the military but also to customers in homeland security and public safety
agencies, demonstrating that our products add value in a wide variety of applications.
During the year we continued to refine our corporate infrastructure and invest in product
development and marketing to position the company to take full advantage of our many
opportunities for long-term growth,&#148; Magerman said.
