Item 1.01    Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

        On December 14, 2004, the Board of Directors of Hecla Mining Company (the “Company”) approved certain changes to the compensation payable to the Company’s independent directors. Effective January 1, 2005, the compensation structure for the Company’s independent directors will be as follows:

Cash Compensation

  Annual Retainer

    •   $20,000 annual retainer for each independent director

  Attendance Fees

    •   $3,000 fee for each regular or special board meeting attended

    •   $2,000 fee to each committee chairman who attends a regular or special committee meeting

    •   $1,000 fee for members of each committee (other than the chairman of each committee) for attending a regular or special committee meeting
