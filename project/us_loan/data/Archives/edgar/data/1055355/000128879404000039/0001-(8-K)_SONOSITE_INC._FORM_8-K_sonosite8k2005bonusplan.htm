Item 1.01

 

Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement

      

                                                                                                                                                                      

     On December 17, 2004, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of SonoSite, Inc. ("Registrant") approved the SonoSite, Inc. FY2005 Variable Incentive Bonus Plan (the "Plan"), an incentive compensation program for fiscal year 2005.   The Plan is designed to motivate, retain and reward the Company's executive officers based on the achievement of specified corporate revenue and operating profits objectives.  The revenue and operating profits objectives are calculated with the revenue objective weighed slightly more heavily than operating profits in calculating bonus payments at 100% and greater achievement of the Plan targets.  For payouts of Plan bonuses at less than 100% achievement of Plan targets, the operating profits objective is more heavily weighted than the revenue objective.  Participants eligible under the Plan are certain executive level employees designated by the Compensation Committee, including the following executive officers: Kevin Goodwin, Bradley Garrett, Mike Schuh and Kathy Surace-Smith.  Bonuses are calculated as a percentage of the participant's base salary and in any event cannot exceed for any one individual $1 million during fiscal year 2005.  The Company has the discretion to pay less than the full amount of any bonus otherwise earned under the Plan.  The attached Exhibit 10.1, incorporated herein by reference, is the form of Plan so approved.
