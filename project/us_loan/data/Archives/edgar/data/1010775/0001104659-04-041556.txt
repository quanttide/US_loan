<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Item 1.01&nbsp;<b><font style="font-weight:bold;"> Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</font></b></font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On December&nbsp;30,
2004, Mirant Corporation (&#147;Mirant&#148;) entered into a Separation Agreement and
Release of Certain Claims (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) with S. Marce Fuller, its Chief
Executive Officer (&#147;Fuller&#148;).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In general, the Agreement
provides for the payment by Mirant of a lump sum separation payment to Fuller
of $3.4 million upon the termination of Fuller&#146;s active employment with
Mirant.&#160; In addition, the Agreement
provides that Fuller will be paid her 2004 short-term incentive at her target
amount of $850,000.&#160; In exchange, Fuller
releases all rights and claims under any change-in-control agreement or plan
with Mirant.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The terms of the
Agreement have been approved by the Bankruptcy Court for the Northern District
of Texas pursuant to an Order issued on September&nbsp;2, 2004 and were agreed
to by all of Mirant&#146;s bankruptcy committees.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Agreement is attached
