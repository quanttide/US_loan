<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>ITEM 1.01. ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT.</B>



<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">On December&nbsp;17, 2004, at its regularly scheduled meeting, the Board of Directors of BackWeb
Technologies Ltd. established the compensation of Bill Heye, BackWeb&#146;s Chief Executive Officer, for
2005. In addition to $180,000 in base salary, Mr.&nbsp;Heye is eligible to receive a bonus of up to
$120,000 based upon the achievement of performance goals to be determined by BackWeb&#146;s Board of
Directors. Mr.&nbsp;Heye will receive quarterly bonuses of $12,500 in each of the first three fiscal
quarters of 2005, with the $37,500 in aggregate quarterly bonuses to be credited towards the annual
bonus Mr.&nbsp;Heye is to receive. In the event that Mr.&nbsp;Heye&#146;s overall bonus for 2005 is less than the
$37,500 he receives in quarterly bonuses, BackWeb&#146;s Board of Directors will determine whether Mr.
Heye is entitled to retain the excess compensation or whether such excess will be deducted from his
future compensation.


<P align="left" style="margin-left: 0%; text-indent: 0%; margin-right: 0%; font-size: 10pt">A copy of the 2005 compensation for Mr.&nbsp;Heye is attached as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1 to this Form 8-K and is
incorporated herein by reference.
