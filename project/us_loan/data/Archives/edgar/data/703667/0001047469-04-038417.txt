<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Interests not voted either in favor of or against the merger are effectively counted against the merger. However, approximately 91% of all votes cast by limited
partners not affiliated with NTS&nbsp;Realty or any of the Existing Partnerships were voted in favor of the merger. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;NTS&nbsp;Realty
also jointly announced with NTS-Properties&nbsp;III and NTS-Properties&nbsp;IV that limited partners holding in excess of fifty percent
(50%) of the outstanding limited partnership interests in each of NTS-Properties&nbsp;III and NTS-Properties IV voted in favor of amending their respective limited
partnership agreements. The purpose of the amendments is to cause NTS-Properties&nbsp;III and NTS-Properties&nbsp;IV to be subject to the Georgia Revised Uniform Limited
Partnership Act and the Kentucky Revised Uniform Limited Partnership Act, respectively, thus effectively permitting the merger to occur. </FONT></P>

<P><FONT SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A
spokesperson for NTS&nbsp;Realty explained that the merger is expected to occur on December&nbsp;28, 2004, shortly after trading on the American Stock Exchange closes at 4:00
eastern time. NTS&nbsp;Realty expects that its limited partnership units will be able to be traded on the American Stock Exchange beginning on or about December&nbsp;29, 2004, on a
"when-issued" basis, as described in its press release dated December&nbsp;17, 2004. NTS&nbsp;Realty will issue a subsequent press release announcing, among other things, the final net
asset value for each Existing Partnership and the number of NTS&nbsp;Realty's limited partnership units to be issued for interests in each Existing Partnership. However, after the merger has
