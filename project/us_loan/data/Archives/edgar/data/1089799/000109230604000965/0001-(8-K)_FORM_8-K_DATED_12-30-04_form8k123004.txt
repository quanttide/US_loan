ITEM 5.02.  DEPARTURE OF DIRECTORS OR PRINCIPAL OFFICERS; ELECTION OF DIRECTORS;
            APPOINTMENT OF PRINCIPAL OFFICERS.

            On December 27, 2004,  Sourav  Goswami  resigned  from the AccuImage
            Diagnostics  Corp.  Board of Directors,  effective on that date. Mr.
            Goswami  indicated that the sole reason for his  resignation was his
            acceptance of employment at a financial  institution whose employees
            are  prohibited  from  serving on the Board of Directors of publicly
            traded companies.
