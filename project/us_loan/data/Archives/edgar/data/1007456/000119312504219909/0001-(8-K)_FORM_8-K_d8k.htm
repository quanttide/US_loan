Section 5 – Corporate Governance and Management

 

Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Principal Officer; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers

 

In connection with his appointment as President of the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas to be effective April 4, 2005, Richard W. Fisher notified Electronic Data Systems Corporation (“EDS”) on December 21, 2004 of his intent to resign from the EDS Board of Directors. The effective date of Mr. Fisher’s resignation from the EDS Board of Directors has not yet been established, but will be not later than April 4, 2005. EDS will report such effective date by amendment to this Form 8-K.
