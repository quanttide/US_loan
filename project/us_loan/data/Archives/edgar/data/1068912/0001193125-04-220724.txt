recipient. The Incentive Stock Option Award Agreement and the Stock Award Agreement contain provisions: (i) requiring a award recipient to maintain the confidentiality of Registrant confidential information and (ii) that restrict any award recipient
from soliciting employees or customers of the Registrant after termination of service for a period of one year. The summary of the Agreements in this paragraph should be read in conjunction with the Agreements which are attached as Exhibits 10.1,
